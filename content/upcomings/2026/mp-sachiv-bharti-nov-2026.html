Gram Panchayat Secretary 23,011 (A) For General Candidates:
(1) Bachelor's Degree from any recognized University.

(B) If the candidate was appointed as GRS before these rules came into force:
(1) Higher Secondary (10+2) passed from Madhya Pradesh Board.

(C) If the candidate was appointed as GRS after these rules came into force:
(1) Bachelor's Degree from any recognized University.
(2) Computer proficiency with DCA and Hindi Typing in CPCT.
