MP ADDET 2026 Course Wise Eligibility Criteria
Educational Qualification and Age Limit for Animal Husbandry & Dairy Technology Diploma Courses
Course Name Eligibility
Animal Husbandry Diploma Passed Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent examination with Biology, Mathematics, or Agriculture subjects from a recognized board. General category candidates must have at least 50% marks. SC/ST candidates are eligible for a 5% relaxation. Minimum age should be 17 years as on 31 December 2026.
Dairy Technology Diploma Passed Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent examination with Biology, Mathematics, or Agriculture subjects from a recognized board. General category candidates must have at least 50% marks. SC/ST candidates are eligible for a 5% relaxation. Minimum age should be 17 years as on 31 December 2026.
