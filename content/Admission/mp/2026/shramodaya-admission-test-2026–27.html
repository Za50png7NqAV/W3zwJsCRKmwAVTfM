Shramodaya Awasiya Vidyalaya Admission Test 2026–27 – Eligibility
Exam Name Eligibility
Shramodaya Awasiya Vidyalaya Admission Test 2026–27 1. Candidate must be a child of a registered Construction Worker under MP Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
2. Construction Worker registration must be valid and from Madhya Pradesh.
3. Candidate must be studying in a recognized school during the academic session 2025–26.
4. Candidate must fulfill the prescribed age limit for Class 6, 7, 8 or 9 as on 01/04/2026.
5. Candidate should belong to the same district where the Construction Worker is registered.
