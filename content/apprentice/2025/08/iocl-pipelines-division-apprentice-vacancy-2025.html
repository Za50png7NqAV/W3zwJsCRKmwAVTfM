Data Entry Operator (Fresher) Minimum 12th Pass (but below Graduate)
Domestic Data Entry Operator (Skill Certificate Holders) Minimum 12th Pass (but below Graduate) and Skill Certificate of “Domestic Data Entry Operator” (training < 1 year) issued by an NSQF/central Govt. recognized awarding body
