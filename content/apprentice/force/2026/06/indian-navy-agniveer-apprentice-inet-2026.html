Indian Navy Agniveer (Apprentice) 01/2027 & 02/2027 – Post Wise Eligibility Details
Post Name Qualification / Eligibility
Agniveer (Apprentice) 01/2027 & 02/2027 Batch
  • Only Unmarried Male Candidates are eligible.
  • Passed Matriculation (10th) Examination with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks from a recognized board.
  • Completed a 3-Year Diploma Course from an AICTE-recognized Polytechnic Institute with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
  • Eligible Diploma Streams:
    • Engineering: Marine Engineering & Systems, Mechanical, Production/Automobile, Refrigeration & Air Conditioning, Repair & Maintenance, Power Plant Engineering, Industrial/Production, Maintenance, Machine, Manufacturing/Manufacturing Technology, Mechatronics, Metallurgy, Instrumentation, Electronics Instrumentation & Control Engineering.
    • Electrical: Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Instrumentation Technology, Electronics, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics Instrumentation & Control Engineering.
  • Candidate must fulfill all age, physical, medical, and other eligibility conditions prescribed in the official notification.
Note: Candidates are strongly advised to read the official notification carefully for detailed eligibility criteria, age limit, physical standards, medical requirements, selection process, and other important instructions before applying.
