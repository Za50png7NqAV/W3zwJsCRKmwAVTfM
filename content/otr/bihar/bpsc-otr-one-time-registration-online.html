BPSC Overview 2025
Point Details
What is BPSC? Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) is a state agency responsible for conducting recruitment exams and selecting candidates for various administrative, technical, medical, and educational posts in Bihar government departments.
Purpose / Objective To ensure fair, transparent, and merit-based selection of candidates for government services in Bihar.
Benefits / Labh Candidates get opportunities for stable government jobs, career growth, and service in various important state departments.
Eligibility Generally, graduation from a recognized university is required. Age limit is usually 21-37 years, with relaxations for reserved categories.
Exam Mode Online or offline written exams, interviews, and in some cases physical tests.
Documents Required Photograph, signature, educational certificates, caste/domicile proof, identity proof, and other relevant certificates.
Registration One-time registration is done on the BPSC portal; the same account can be used for multiple exams.
