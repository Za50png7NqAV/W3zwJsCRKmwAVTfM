RPSC Statistical Officer Recruitment 2025 – Post Details
Post Name Total Posts Qualification
Statistical Officer 113
  • At least Second Class Master’s Degree in any one of the following:
    • Economics
    • Statistics
    • Mathematics (with paper in Statistics)
    • Commerce (with Statistics)
    • M.Sc. (Agriculture) Statistics
  • RS-CIT Certificate (or equivalent recognized computer course)
  • Experience of handling official statistics for at least 1 year in Government Department / University / reputed organization
  • Working knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and knowledge of Rajasthan culture
