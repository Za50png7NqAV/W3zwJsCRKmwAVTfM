ECGC Probationary Officer Recruitment 2025–26: Post Details
Post Name Total Post Qualification
Probationary Officer (Generalist) 28 Bachelor’s Degree / Graduate in any discipline from a University recognized by the Government of India or any equivalent qualification recognized by the Central Government.
Probationary Officer (Specialist - Rajbhasha/Hindi) 2 Master’s Degree with minimum 55% for SC/ST and 60% for others in any of the following:
  • Hindi / Hindi Translation with English as core or elective subject at Bachelor’s level, OR
  • English with Hindi as core or elective subject at Bachelor’s level, OR
  • Any discipline with English and Hindi as core or elective subjects at Bachelor’s level, OR
  • Both English and Hindi / Hindi Translation at Master’s level.
