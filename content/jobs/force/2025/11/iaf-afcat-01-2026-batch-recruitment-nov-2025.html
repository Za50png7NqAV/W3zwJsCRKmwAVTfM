AFCAT 01/2026 – Post Name, Total Posts & Qualification
Post Name / Branch Total Posts Essential Qualification
Flying Branch (SSC) 38 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics with minimum 50% marks each and Graduation (3-year) in any discipline with 60% OR BE/B.Tech (4-year) with 60% marks from a recognized University.
Ground Duty (Technical) – Aeronautical Engineer (Electronics) [AE(L)] 129 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics (50%) and 4-year Engineering/Technology degree (60%) in relevant disciplines such as Electronics, Electrical, Computer Science, IT, AI, Communication etc.
Ground Duty (Technical) – Aeronautical Engineer (Mechanical) [AE(M)] 59 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics (50%) and 4-year Engineering/Technology degree (60%) in Mechanical, Aeronautical, Production, Automobile, Mechatronics or related streams.
Ground Duty (Non-Technical) – Weapon Systems (WS) 26 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics (50%) and Graduation (3-year) in any discipline with 60% OR BE/B.Tech (4-year) with 60% marks.
Ground Duty (Non-Technical) – Administration 60 Graduate in any discipline with minimum 60% marks OR cleared Section A & B of AMIE / Aeronautical Society of India with 60% marks.
Ground Duty (Non-Technical) – Logistics (LGS) 11 Graduate in any discipline with minimum 60% marks or equivalent from a recognized University.
Ground Duty (Non-Technical) – Accounts (ACCTS) 10 B.Com / BBA / BMS / BBS (Finance Specialization) or CA / CMA / CS / CFA with minimum 60% marks from a recognized University.
Ground Duty (Non-Technical) – Education (EDN) 4 Post-Graduation in any discipline with minimum 50% marks and Graduation with 60% marks.
Ground Duty (Non-Technical) – Meteorology (MET) 3 B.Sc (Physics & Mathematics) with 60% OR 4-year Engineering/Technology degree with 60% in relevant streams such as Electronics, Electrical, IT or Mechanical Engineering.
NCC Special Entry (Flying Branch) Included within AFCAT Vacancies (Approx 10% of CDSE & AFCAT Seats) NCC Air Wing ‘C’ Certificate holders with minimum 60% marks in Graduation and 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics. No written exam; direct AFSB interview.
Total (AFCAT Entry + NCC Special Entry) ≈ 340 Posts (AFCAT vacancies including NCC quota) All eligibility conditions as per Indian Air Force official notification AFCAT 01/2026.
