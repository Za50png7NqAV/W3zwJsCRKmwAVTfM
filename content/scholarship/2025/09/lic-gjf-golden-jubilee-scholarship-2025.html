LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship Scheme 2025 – Details at a Glance
Post / Field Short Details
Post Name Golden Jubilee Scholarship Scheme – 2025
Objective To provide scholarships to meritorious students from Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) for higher education and better employment opportunities.
Eligibility
  • General Scholarship: After Class 12 or Class 10 with ≥60% marks
  • Special Scholarship for Girl Child: Female students after Class 10 with ≥60% marks
  • Parental/guardian annual income ≤ ₹4,50,000
  • Regular courses only (no correspondence/open university)
Duration General Scholarship: Entire course duration (excluding internship)
Special Girl Scholarship: 2 years
Amount of Scholarship
  • Medicine: ₹40,000 per annum
  • Engineering: ₹30,000 per annum
  • Graduation / Diploma / Vocational: ₹20,000 per annum
  • Special Girl Scholarship: ₹15,000 per annum
Payment Mode Scholarship transferred via NEFT to student’s bank account in 2 installments per year.
Selection Process
  • Online application assigned to LIC Division based on PIN code
  • Candidates ranked by marks in income bands (0–2.5 L, 2.5–4.5 L)
  • Divisions select candidates based on vacancies and rank
  • Document verification followed by scholarship credit to bank account
