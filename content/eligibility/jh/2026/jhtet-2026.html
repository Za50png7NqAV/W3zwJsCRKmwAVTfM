JHTET 2026 Eligibility Criteria for Primary (Class 1–5) and Upper Primary (Class 6–8)
Level Eligibility Criteria
Primary Level (Class 1–5) 1. Senior Secondary (50%) + 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed)

2. Senior Secondary (45%) + 2-year D.El.Ed (as per NCTE 2002 norms)

3. Senior Secondary (50%) + 4-year Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed)

4. Senior Secondary (50%) + 2-year Diploma in Special Education

5. Graduation + 2-year D.El.Ed
Upper Primary Level (Class 6–8) 1. Graduation + 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed)

2. Graduation (50%) + Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) (after 29-07-2011)

3. Graduation (45%) + B.Ed (as per NCTE norms, before 29-07-2011)

4. Senior Secondary (50%) + 4-year B.El.Ed

5. Senior Secondary (50%) + 4-year Integrated BA/B.Sc + B.Ed

6. Graduation (50%) + B.Ed (Special Education)

7. Post Graduation (55%) + 3-year Integrated B.Ed-M.Ed
For detailed eligibility criteria, candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully.
