CTET September 2026 Eligibility Criteria
Paper Eligibility Criteria
Paper-I
(Class I to V)		 Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed).
Senior Secondary with at least 45% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education as per NCTE regulations.
Senior Secondary with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 4-year Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed).
Senior Secondary with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Education (Special Education).
Graduation and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education.
Paper-II
(Class VI to VIII)		 Graduation and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education.
Graduation with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in 1-year Bachelor in Education (B.Ed).
Graduation with at least 45% marks and passed or appearing in 1-year B.Ed as per NCTE regulations.
Senior Secondary with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 4-year B.A.Ed/B.Sc.Ed or B.A.B.Ed/B.Sc.B.Ed.
Graduation with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in 1-year B.Ed (Special Education).
Post Graduation with at least 55% marks and passed or appearing in 3-year Integrated B.Ed-M.Ed.
Note: Relaxation up to 5% in qualifying marks is available for SC/ST/OBC/PwD candidates as per government rules.
Read the Official Notification Carefully for Detailed Eligibility Criteria.
