UGC NET December 2025 – Educational Qualification
Criteria Details
Minimum Qualification Master's Degree or equivalent in the concerned subject from a recognized university.
Minimum Marks General / Unreserved / General-EWS: 55% marks (without rounding off)
OBC-NCL / SC / ST / PwD / Third Gender: 50% marks
Final Year Students Candidates appearing in the final semester of their Master’s Degree or awaiting results can also apply. They must complete eligibility within 2 years.
Ph.D. Holders (Before 19 September 1991) Candidates who completed Ph.D. before 19.09.1991 are eligible for 5% relaxation in aggregate marks (i.e., 55% → 50%).
