Food License (FSSAI) Short Info
Topic Information
Department Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)
Ministry Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Govt. of India
Types Basic, State and Central License
Fees Basic: ₹100/year, State: ₹2,000–5,000/year, Central: ₹7,500/year
Validity 1 to 5 years, renewal required on time
Application Process Online through foscos.fssai.gov.in
Penalty Up to ₹5 lakh fine and imprisonment without license
License Info Basic for small businesses, State for medium, and Central for large/import-export businesses
Conclusion FSSAI License is mandatory for all food businesses, ensuring customer trust and legal protection
