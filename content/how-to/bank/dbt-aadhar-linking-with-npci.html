Complete Information About NPCT DBT
Point Details
What is NPCT DBT? It is a system operated by NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India) through which government subsidies and other benefits are transferred directly to beneficiaries' bank accounts.
Purpose To provide direct funds to beneficiaries, eliminate intermediaries, and ensure transparency.
Who are the beneficiaries? Students, farmers, pensioners, MGNREGA workers, LPG consumers, etc.
Eligibility Requirement Aadhaar card must be linked with the bank account (Aadhaar seeding).
How is money transferred? Government departments use the NPCI's Aadhaar Payment Bridge (APB) to transfer funds directly to the bank account.
Payment Identification It appears in the bank passbook or SMS as “NPCT/DBT”.
Where is it used? LPG subsidy, pensions, scholarships, farmer assistance, MGNREGA wages, etc.
Benefits Funds are received on time, no fraud, and beneficiaries receive direct benefits.
How to check? Check through bank passbook, mini statement, UMANG app, or DBT Bharat Portal.
If any problem occurs Contact your bank, verify Aadhaar seeding, or use the DBT helpline number.
Security All transactions are digital and secured by NPCI.
Future Importance DBT is being gradually implemented in all government schemes to ensure 100% transparency.

How to Update Aadhaar NPCI Mapping Online Through BASE Portal

Step 1: Visit the Official NPCI Website

Open the official NPCI website and go to the home page.

Step 2: Click on Customer

From the top navigation menu, click on the Customer option.

Step 3: Select Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE)

From the dropdown menu, click on Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE).

Step 4: Open Aadhaar Seeding / De-Seeding

Click on Aadhaar Seeding / De-Seeding from the left sidebar menu.

Step 5: Enter Aadhaar and Bank Details

Fill in the required information:

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Select Seeding Request Type
  • Choose Your Bank Name
  • Enter Bank Account Number
  • Confirm Account Number

Read the declaration carefully and tick the consent checkbox.

Step 6: Submit the Request

Enter the captcha code and click the Submit button. Your request will be sent to the selected bank for verification.

Step 7: Wait for Approval

After successful submission, the bank will verify your details. Once approved, your Aadhaar Number will be mapped with your bank account in the NPCI database and eligible DBT benefits will be credited to the mapped account.

Step 8: Check Aadhaar Mapping Status

To verify the mapping status, click on Aadhaar Mapped Status, enter your Aadhaar Number and captcha, then click Check Status.

Important Note: Aadhaar Seeding requests are subject to verification and approval by the selected bank. NPCI mapping updates may take a few days after approval. Ensure that the Aadhaar Number and Bank Account details entered are correct.
