RRC Western Railway Sports Quota Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Western Railway has released a notification for Sports Quota Recruitment 2025-26, inviting online applications from eligible sportspersons across India. A total of 64 vacancies will be filled, which include 5 posts under Level 5/4 (Group C) for graduates, 16 posts under Level 3/2 (Group C) for candidates with 12th pass, ITI, Diploma, or Apprenticeship, and 43 posts under Level 1 (Group D) for candidates who have passed 10th, ITI, NAC, or hold a Diploma. The recruitment is open for various disciplines such as Cricket, Kabaddi, Wrestling, Athletics, Football, Basketball, and more. The selection will be based on sports achievements and trial performance—no written exam will be conducted. The online application process begins on 30 July 2025 and will close on 29 August 2025 via the official website www.rrc-wr.com.

Indian Navy SSC Executive (IT) Vacancy Jan 2026
Commission (SSC) in Information Technology (Executive Branch) for the January 2026 course. A total of 15 vacancies are available. Applicants must hold a degree in BE/B.Tech/M.Tech/MCA/MSc in Computer Science, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, or related fields with a minimum of 60% marks, along with at least 60% marks in English in Class 10 or 12. Eligible candidates should be born between 02 January 2001 and 01 July 2006 (both dates inclusive). The online application window opens on 02 August 2025 and closes on 17 August 2025. The selection process includes shortlisting based on normalized marks, SSB interview, medical examination, and merit list. Selected candidates will be appointed as Sub Lieutenants and undergo training at the Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Kerala. The initial service tenure is 10 years, which may be extended up to 14 years (10+2+2) based on performance and service requirements. The basic pay starts at ₹56,100/- plus applicable allowances. For more details and to apply online, visit the official website: www.joinindiannavy.gov.in.

MPESB Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025 Online Form
Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the notification for the Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025. A total of 10,150 Varg 3 Primary Teacher posts will be recruited under the School Education Department. Eligible candidates who have qualified the MP Primary TET 2020 or 2024 can apply online. Applicants must check the detailed eligibility, age limit, and other conditions in the official notification before applying. Candidates from outside MP can also apply but will not get any domicile reservation or relaxation benefits.
