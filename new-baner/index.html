MSME Udyam Registration 2025- Online Form
Udyam Registration is an official government process under the Ministry of MSME (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) in India. It allows small business owners, startups, manufacturers, and service providers to register their businesses and get recognized as an MSME. The registration is completely free, online, and requires only basic documents like Aadhaar and PAN. Once registered, businesses get access to government benefits such as subsidies, low-interest loans, tax exemptions, and priority in tenders. It is mandatory for businesses that want to avail of MSME schemes and participate in government opportunities.

RPSC Assistant Agriculture Engineer AAE Vacancy 2025
The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the notification for the recruitment of Assistant Agriculture Engineer (AAE) 2025 with a total of 281 vacancies. Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Agricultural Engineering from a recognized university to be eligible. Additionally, applicants should have a working knowledge of Hindi written in Devanagari script and a good understanding of Rajasthan’s culture. The online application process will be open from 28 July 2025 to 26 August 2025. Vacancies are distributed across various categories including General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS, and MBC. Candidates are advised to check the detailed notification and ensure they meet the eligibility criteria before applying
