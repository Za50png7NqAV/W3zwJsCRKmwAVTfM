BRLPS JEEViKA Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Bihar Rural Livelihoods Promotion Society (BRLPS), under the Rural Development Department of the Government of Bihar, has released the official notification for JEEViKA Recruitment 2025. A total of 2747 vacancies have been announced across various posts including Block Project Manager, Livelihood Specialist, Area Coordinator, Accountant, Office Assistant, Community Coordinator, and Block IT Executive. The required educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from Intermediate (for female Community Coordinators) to Graduation, Post-Graduation, Diploma, or B.Tech. The minimum age for applicants is 18 years, while the upper age limit goes up to 42 years depending on the category. The selection process involves a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and a typing test for relevant positions. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website https://brlps.in from 30th July to 18th August 2025. This is a great opportunity to contribute to Bihar’s largest rural development mission and support the empowerment of rural communities.

Haryana HPSC ADO Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has released the notification for the recruitment of 785 Agricultural Development Officer (ADO) posts under the Administrative Cadre (Group-B) in the Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Department, Haryana. Candidates possessing a B.Sc. (Hons) in Agriculture and having studied Hindi or Sanskrit up to Matric or higher are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 5th August 2025 and will remain open until 25th August 2025 (up to 5:00 PM). Applicants must be between 18 to 42 years of age as on 01 July 2025. The selected candidates will be placed in Pay Level-6 of the 7th CPC pay matrix, with a salary range of ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400. All 27 PwBD reserved posts are included in the total of 785 vacancies. Interested candidates can apply through the official HPSC website at hpsc.gov.in. Category-wise vacancy breakup, reservation rules, fee details, and application instructions are available in the official notification.

RRC Eastern Railway Apprentice Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Eastern Railway, Kolkata has released the official notification for Apprentice Recruitment 2025 for a total of 3115 trade-wise vacancies. The recruitment is open for various divisions and workshops including Howrah, Sealdah, Asansol, Malda, Liluah, Jamalpur, and Kanchrapara. Candidates who have passed Class 10th and possess an ITI certificate in the relevant trade are eligible to apply. The age of the applicant should be between 15 and 24 years. The selection will be based purely on merit, considering the marks obtained in 10th and ITI – no written exam will be conducted. Applications must be submitted online through the official RRC ER website within the prescribed dates.
