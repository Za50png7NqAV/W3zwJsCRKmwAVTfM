IBPS Specialist Officer SO 15th Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the notification for the Specialist Officer (SO) CRP SPL-XV Recruitment 2025. IBPS Probationary Officer PO 15th Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the notification for CRP PO/MT-XV Recruitment 2025 for the post of Probationary Officer (PO)/Management Trainee (MT). A total of 5208 vacancies have been announced across 8 participating public sector banks. Candidates who hold a Bachelor’s Degree in any discipline from a recognized university in India are eligible to apply. The recruitment process includes a preliminary exam, main exam, and interview. Applications must be submitted online through the official IBPS website www.ibps.in. Selected candidates will be appointed across various locations in India.

MPTAAS Profile Registration 2025
MPTAAS Profile Registration 2025 is a mandatory process for SC, ST, and OBC students in Madhya Pradesh to apply for Post Matric Scholarships. The profile is created on the Tribal Affairs portal, enabling students to apply for schemes, track status, and receive benefits via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer). Important documents like Aadhaar, Samagra ID, caste certificate, domicile proof, and bank details are required. Once registered, students can log in and apply for scholarships online.

MP Rojgar Panjiyan Online Registration 2025
MP Rojgar Panjiyan 2025 is the official employment registration system launched by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Through this portal (mprojgar.gov.in), job seekers can register for free, get notified about government and private job opportunities, participate in job fairs, and renew their registration every 3 years. The process is fully online, and applicants need to provide basic personal information, educational documents, and Aadhaar details to complete their profile.
