RPSC OTR Registration 2025
RPSC OTR (One Time Registration) is a permanent registration system started by the Rajasthan Public Service Commission, where candidates need to register only once using their SSO ID. After completing the OTR, applicants can easily apply for any RPSC recruitment without filling in their details repeatedly. This process saves time and ensures accuracy in applications, while also allowing candidates to update their profile whenever required.

RPSC Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Vacancy 2025
RPSC has announced 12 vacancies for the post of Junior Legal Officer (JLO) in the Jaipur Development Authority (JDA) through notification number 11/Exam/JLO/JDA/EP-I/2025-26. Interested and eligible Law graduates can apply online between 27 August 2025 to 25 September 2025. The recruitment process includes a written examination and document verification. Candidates must carefully read the notification for eligibility, application fees, and reservation details before applying.

AFCAT 2 2025 Admit Card – Indian Air Force
Indian Air Force has released the AFCAT 02/2025 notification for the recruitment of Commissioned Officers in Flying and Ground Duty (Technical and Non-Technical) branches. Eligible candidates with a graduate or engineering degree can apply online through the official website. The application process is expected to start in June 2025, with the last date likely in July 2025. The selection will be based on a written exam, AFSB interview, and medical test. Candidates must pay ₹250 as the application fee. For complete details, visit afcat.cdac.in.

RPSC Senior Teacher Grade IV Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the notification for Senior Teacher Recruitment 2025 under the Secondary Education Department. A total of 6500 vacancies are available in 10 subjects including Hindi, English, Maths, Science, Sanskrit, and more. Eligible candidates can apply online from 19 August to 17 September 2025. Candidates from all states can apply, but reservation and age relaxation are applicable only for Rajasthan domicile candidates.
