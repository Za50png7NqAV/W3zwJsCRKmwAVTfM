Indian Navy Civilian INCET Vacancy 2025 Online Form

JPSC Assistant Public Prosecutor APO Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has released the notification for 134 vacancies of Assistant Public Prosecutor (APO) for the year 2025. Candidates holding a Bachelor’s Degree in Law (LLB) from any recognized university in India are eligible to apply. The selection process will include written examinations and interviews. Interested candidates can apply online within the specified application dates. Category-wise vacancies are available for UR, BC-I, BC-II, EWS, SC, and ST candidates. For complete eligibility criteria and application details, refer to the official notification.
