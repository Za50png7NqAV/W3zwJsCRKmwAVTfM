    Indian Coast Guard Navik GD Branch Recruitment 2025

    Join Indian Coast Guard (ICG)

    Short Details of Notification Coast Guard Advt No. : 02/2025 CGEPT

    Important Dates

    • Application Begin: 11/02/2025
    • Last Date for Registration: 25/02/2025 upto 11
    • Exam Date: April 2025
    • Admit Card Available: Before Exam

    Application Fee

    • UR / OBC / EWS: 300/-
    • SC / ST: 0/-

    • Pay the Exam Fee Through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking Fee Mode Only.

    Eligibility

    • Age Limit CGEPT 02/2025

    • Minimum Age: 18 Years.
    • Maximum Age: 22 Years.
    • Age Between: 01/09/2003 to 31/08/2007 for ICG Navik GD Exam CGEPT 02/2025

    Age relaxation as per rules of Coast Guard Advt No. : 02/2025 CGEPT.

    Vacancy Details

    • Total Vacancy: 300 Post

    • -------------------

    • Navik General Duty GD: 260 Post
    • Navik Domestic Branch DB: 40 Post

    • -------------------

    • Coast Guard Navik CGEPT 02/2025

    Education-Wise Details

    Post Name Total Post Coast Guard Navik Eligibility
    Navik General Duty (GD) 260 10+2 Intermediate Exam with Physics / Mathematics as a Subject.
    Navik Domestic Branch (DB) 40 Class 10th Matric Exam Passed from Any Recognized Board in India.

    How to Apply

    📢 Important Instructions for Candidates
    ✅ for Applying Join Indian Coast Guard (ICG)

    Go to official Site and Read Instructions and notification for Candidates from official Website before Apply .

    ✅ Read the Notification

    Carefully go through the official notification to understand the recruitment process and requirements.

    ✅ Document Preparation
    • Verify your eligibility and gather essential documents like ID proof, address details, and basic details.
