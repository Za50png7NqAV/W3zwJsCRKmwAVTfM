👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to the South Western Railway RRC official website (or direct application link if provided).

  • Read the Notification Carefully:

    Download and read the official notification to check eligibility, trade-wise vacancies, and instructions.

  • Complete the Registration:

    Click on the "Apply Online" link and register using your basic details like name, mobile number, and email ID.

  • Fill the Online Application Form:

    Log in and enter your personal details, educational qualification, ITI trade, division preferences, etc.

  • Upload Required Documents:

    Upload scanned copies of 10th Marksheet, ITI Certificate, Photograph & Signature, Category/PH Certificate (if applicable)

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Pay the fee (₹100 for Gen/OBC/EWS) using Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.

  • Submit and Print:

    After successful submission, download and print the application form for future reference.
