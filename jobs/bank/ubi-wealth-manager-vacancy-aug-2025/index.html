The application process will be open from 05 Aug to 25 Aug 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . 👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit Official Website:

    www.unionbankofindia.co.in → Recruitments → Careers section.

  • Open Link:

    Click “RECRUITMENT OF WEALTH MANAGERS (SPECIALIST OFFICERS)” → “CLICK HERE TO APPLY ONLINE”.

  • Register:

    Click “New Registration”, enter details, get Registration Number & Password.

  • Fill Form:

    Enter personal, education, and experience details.

  • Upload Documents:

    Photo, signature, thumb impression, handwritten declaration.

  • Pay Fee:

    ₹177 (SC/ST/PwBD) or ₹1,180 (Others) online.

  • Submit:

    Final submit, download receipt, and print application form.
