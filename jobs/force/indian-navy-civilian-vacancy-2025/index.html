Q. What is INCET in the Indian Navy??

INCET stands for Indian Navy Civilian Entrance Test, conducted for recruitment to Group B and C civilian posts.

Q. How many vacancies are released in INCET 01/2025??

A total of 1097 civilian posts have been released under Group B and Group C categories.

Q. Who can apply for Indian Navy INCET 2025??

Male and Female Indian citizens who meet the required educational qualifications and age limits can apply.

Q. What is the minimum qualification required??

The minimum qualification varies by post: 10th / 12th / ITI / Diploma / Graduate ‚Äì depending on the post applied for.

Q. What is the age limit for INCET posts??

The minimum age is 18 years. The upper age limit varies between 25 to 45 years depending on the post. Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules.

Q. What is the selection process??

Selection is based on a Computer-Based Written Exam, followed by Document Verification and Medical Exam.

Q. Is there any application fee??

Yes, the examination fee must be paid through online mode only (Net banking, UPI, cards etc.).

Q. Can I apply for more than one post??

Yes, but only if you meet the eligibility criteria for each post. Separate applications may be required.
