Rajasthan Police Constable Vacancy 2025 – Notification PDF, eligibility, Apply Link

Last Update: 06/06/2025 | 11:40 AM

Description: Govt of Rajasthan – Police Department has released a notification क्रमांक-5( )पु.फो./कानि.भर्ती–25/2024/1360 of Rajasthan Police RP Constable Recruitment 2025. The application process will be open from 28 April to 25 May 2025. Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including 12th Pass. As per the official notification, . Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.
