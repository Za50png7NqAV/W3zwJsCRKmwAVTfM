    IOB Local Bank Officer Bharti 2025 – ऑनलाइन फॉर्म, योग्यता, वेतन

    Last Update: 12/05/2025 | 04:51 PM

    Description: Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) has released a notification HRDD/RECT/01/2025-26 of इंडियन ओवरसीज बैंक लोकल बैंक अधिकारी भर्ती 2025. The application process will be open from 12 May to 31 May 2025. Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including Bachelor Degree . As per the official notification, there are 400 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

    Notification : HRDD/RECT/01/2025-26 (Dated: 12 May 2025)
