Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB)

DSSSB Post Code Wise Admit Card Out – Check Now

📝 Short Info

Exam Name DSSSB Various Post Code Recruitment Exam 2025
Admit Card Release Date 28 June 2025
Exam Date 4 July to 9 July 2025
Exam Mode Offline / CBT (Computer Based Test)
Exam Centers Across Delhi NCR
Official Website: https://dsssb.delhi.gov.in
👨‍💻 How to do:
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to dsssb.delhi.gov.in
  • Click on the Admit Card Link: "Download E-Admit Card for Exams Scheduled from 23 June to 09 July 2025"
  • Enter Login Details: Application Number / Roll Number, Date of Birth, Password (if required) Click on ‘Login’ or ‘Submit’
  • Download the Admit Card: Your admit card will appear on the screen. Click "Download" and save the PDF. Take a printout for the exam.
📄 Importent Documents/Details
✓Application Number
✓User Password
🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. When was the DSSSB Admit Card 2025 released??

A: The admit card was released on 28 June 2025 for exams scheduled from 23 June to 9 July 2025.

Q. How can I download my DSSSB admit card??

A: Visit https://dsssb.delhi.gov.in → Click the admit card link → Enter your login details → Download the PDF.

Q. What details are required to login??

A: You need your Application Number / Roll Number, Date of Birth, and sometimes your Password.

Q. What should I do if I forget my application number??

A: Use the “Forgot Application No.” or “Recover Password” option on the login page or check your email/SMS from DSSSB.

Q. Is it mandatory to carry a printout of the admit card??

A: Yes, a printed copy of the admit card is mandatory along with a valid photo ID proof.

Q. Can I show the admit card on my mobile at the exam center??

A: No, only printed admit cards are allowed at the exam center.

Q. What should I do if there’s an error in my admit card??

A: Contact DSSSB helpline or email support immediately before your exam date.

Q. What is the official website of DSSSB??

A: dsssb.delhi.gov.in
