🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ When is the RRB ALP CBAT 2025 exam scheduled??

Ans: The CBAT exam is scheduled for 15th July 2025.

❓ Who is eligible to appear for the CBAT stage??

Ans: Only those candidates who have qualified the CBT-2 stage of RRB ALP (CEN 01/2024) are eligible to appear in CBAT.

❓ What is the full form of CBAT??

Ans: CBAT stands for Computer-Based Aptitude Test. It is the final stage of the RRB ALP selection process.

❓ When will the admit card be available for download??

Ans: Admit cards are expected to be available from 11 July 2025, 4 days before the exam.

❓ How to download the RRB ALP CBAT admit card??

Ans: Visit your RRB's official website, log in using your Registration Number and Date of Birth, and download the admit card from the provided link.

❓ Will there be any negative marking in CBAT??

Ans: No, there is no negative marking in CBAT. However, only candidates who score a minimum of 42 marks in each test battery (unreserved) will qualify.

❓ What is the qualifying criteria for CBAT??

Ans: A minimum score of 42 marks in each test is required (no relaxation for SC/ST).

❓ Where can I check the exam city and timing??

Ans: The Exam City Intimation Slip will be available from around 5 July 2025 on the RRB website.

❓ What if I forget my registration number??

Ans: Use the “Forgot Registration” option on the login page or check your registered email/SMS for previous communication.
