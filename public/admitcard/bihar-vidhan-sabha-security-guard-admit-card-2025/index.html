📝 Short Info

Post Detail : Security Guard
Exam Name : Bihar Vidhan Sabha Security Guard Exam 2025
Pre Admit Card Release Date : 05 July 2025
Exam Date : 13 July 2025
Result Declared : After exam completion
Exam Mode : Offline / CBT
Exam Centers : Across Bihar
Total Posts : 69
👨‍💻 How to do:

    1. Visit the Official Website:
    Go to vidhansabha.bih.nic.in

    2. Navigate to the Admit Card Section:
    Look for a link titled “Recruitment”, “Career”, or “Admit Card 2025” on the homepage.

    3. Select the Security Guard Admit Card Link:
    Click on the relevant link, such as “Download Security Guard Admit Card 2025”.

    4. Enter Your Credentials:
    Provide your Registration Number (or Application Number) and Date of Birth.

    5. Submit and View:
    Hit Submit or Login—your admit card will be displayed on the screen.

    6. Download & Print:
    Click Download PDF, Print at least two copies for future reference

📄 Importent Documents/Details
✓Application ID/ Mobile Number
✓Date of Birth
🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. When will the Bihar Vidhan Sabha Security Guard Admit Card 2025 be released??

Ans: The admit card is expected to be released in July 2025. Keep checking the official website for updates.

Q. Where can I download the admit card??

Ans: You can download it from the official website: https://vidhansabha.bih.nic.in

Q. What details are required to download the admit card??

Ans: You need your Registration Number or Application ID and Date of Birth/Password.

Q. What should I do if I forget my registration number??

Ans: Check your email/SMS for the registration details or use the ‘Forgot Registration’ option on the admit card page (if available).

Q. Will the admit card be sent by post??

Ans: No, the admit card will be available online only. It will not be sent by post.

Q. What should I carry to the exam center along with the admit card??

Ans: You must carry- Printed admit card, A valid photo ID (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, etc.), Passport-sized photograph (if mentioned)

Q. What to do if there is an error in my admit card??

Ans: Immediately contact the Bihar Vidhan Sabha recruitment helpdesk for correction.

Q. What is the mode of the examination??

Ans: The exam will be conducted in Offline/CBT (Computer-Based Test) mode.
