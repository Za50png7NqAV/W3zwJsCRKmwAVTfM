📝 Short Info

Exam Name : DFCCIL MTS, Executive & Junior Manager Recruitment 2025
Exam City Details : 26 June 2025

Exam Date: 10 July to 11 July 2025 (Stage-I)
Result Declared : To be announced
Exam Mode : Computer Based Test (CBT)

Total Posts : 642 Post
Official Website: https://dfccil.com
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit Official Website:
    Go to the official DFCCIL website – dfccil.com

    Navigate to Career Section:
    On the homepage, click on the “Careers” or “Latest News” section.

    Find Admit Card Link:
    Look for the link titled “Download Admit Card for MTS / Executive / Junior Manager Exam 2025”

    Login Page Opens:
    Click the link – You will be redirected to the candidate login page.

    Enter Login Credentials:
    Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth / Password

    Submit & View:
    Click on the “Login” or “Submit” button.

    Download Admit Card:
    Your admit card will appear on the screen. Check all the details carefully.

    Print a Copy:
    Download the PDF and take a printout for the exam day.

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. 1. When will the DFCCIL exam 2025 be conducted??

👉 The exam is expected to be conducted in August 2025 as per the official notification.

Q. 2. What is the mode of DFCCIL exam??

👉 The exam will be conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode.

Q. 3. When will the DFCCIL admit card 2025 be released??

👉 The admit card will be released a few days before the exam on the official website – https://dfccil.com.

Q. 4. How can I download my DFCCIL admit card??

👉 You can download it by logging in using your registration number and date of birth on the official portal.

Q. 5. What should I carry to the exam center??

👉 Admit card printout, one photo ID proof (Aadhar, Voter ID, etc.), and a recent passport-size photo.

Q. 6. Can I change my exam center after submitting the form??

👉 No, once submitted, the exam center cannot be changed.

Q. 7. Is there negative marking in the DFCCIL CBT??

👉 Yes, there is likely to be negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer (subject to post and notification).

Q. 8. What is the official website for DFCCIL updates??

👉 dfccil.com
