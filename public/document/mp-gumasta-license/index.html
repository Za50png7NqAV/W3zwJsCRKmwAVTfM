📝 Short Info

Definition : Gumasta Licence is a legal registration that provides government recognition to a shop or business.
Other Name : It is also known as Shop & Establishment Registration.
Who Needs It : Required for shop owners, offices, small businesses, startups, and companies.
Purpose : Ensures that the business operates legally and follows labour laws.
Benefits : Helps in opening a business bank account, GST registration, and accessing government schemes.
Application Mode : Available online in most states through the labour department portal.
Documents Required : Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, photograph, and address proof.
Application Fees : Usually ranges from ₹100 to ₹1000 depending on the state and business type.
Processing Time : Licence is generally issued within 3 to 7 days after application.
Renewal : Renewal may be required periodically as per state rules.

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ What is a Gumasta Licence??

It is a legal registration required to run a shop or business under the Shop & Establishment Act.

❓ Is Gumasta Licence mandatory in MP??

Yes, it is mandatory for all shops, offices, and commercial establishments in Madhya Pradesh.

❓ Who can apply for Gumasta Licence??

Any individual, business owner, startup, or company operating a shop or office can apply.

❓ How can I apply online in MP??

You can apply through the MP Labour Department Portal by filling the online form.

❓ What is the application fee??

The fee ranges from ₹100 to ₹1000 depending on the business type.

❓ How many days does it take to get the licence? ?

Usually, it takes 3 to 7 working days after submission.

❓ Is renewal required for Gumasta Licence??

Yes, renewal may be required as per state rules.

❓ Can I run a business without this licence??

No, running a business without registration can lead to legal issues or penalties.

❓ Is this licence required for GST registration??

Yes, it is often required as a supporting document for GST and bank account opening.
