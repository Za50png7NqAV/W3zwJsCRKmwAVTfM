Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME)

MSME Udyam Registration Online – Step-by-Step Guide (Free & Easy)

Ad
Advertisement

📝 Short Info

Scheme Name : Udyam Registration
Launched By : Ministry of MSME, Government of India
Purpose : MSME (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises)
Who Can Apply : Proprietor, Partnership Firm, Company, LLP, etc.
Mode of Registration : Online (Self Declaration)
Fee : ₹0 (Completely Free)
Documents Required : Aadhaar, PAN, Bank Details, Business Info
Major Benefits : Subsidy, Easy Loans, Tax Benefits, Govt Tender Preference
Eligibility : Based on Investment & Turnover (As per MSME norms)
Certificate Issued : Udyam MSME Certificate (With Unique Udyam Number)
📄 Importent Documents/Details
✓Aadhaar Card
✓PAN Card
✓Mobile Number & Email ID
✓Bank Account Details
✓Business Address
✓Business Activity Details
✓Investment & Turnover Info
📌 Key Point ℹ Short Info
What is Udyam Registration? It is a government registration for MSMEs (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises).
Who can apply? Any business owner, startup, firm, company, or self-employed individual.
Eligibility Must fall under Micro, Small, or Medium category based on investment & turnover.
Required Documents Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Mobile Number, Bank Details.
Registration Fee Completely Free (₹0)
Registration Process Apply online on the official portal with OTP verification.
Certificate Udyam Certificate is issued online after successful registration.
Benefits Govt subsidy, low-interest loans, tax benefits, tender priority, scheme access.
Renewal Required? No, it is a one-time registration.
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit Official Website :
    Go to udyamregistration.gov.in

    Select Option :
    Click on “For New Entrepreneurs who are not Registered yet as MSME”

    Enter Aadhaar Number :
    Fill in your 12-digit Aadhaar number

    Enter Name as per Aadhaar :
    Exactly match your name with Aadhaar

    Validate Aadhaar :
    Click on “Validate & Generate OTP”

    Enter OTP :
    Enter OTP received on Aadhaar-linked mobile number

    Enter PAN Details :
    Fill your PAN number (mandatory for businesses)

    Enter Business Name :
    Provide the name of your business/enterprise

    Select Organization Type :
    Proprietorship, Partnership, Company, etc.

    Enter Business Address :
    Fill complete address of your business

    Enter Mobile & Email :
    Provide valid contact details

    Choose Business Activity :
    Select manufacturing or service

    Enter NIC Code :
    Choose your business activity NIC code

    Enter Employee Count :
    Total number of employees

    Enter Investment Details :
    Total investment in plant & machinery

    Enter Turnover Details :
    Annual turnover of the business

    Enter Bank Details :
    Account number and IFSC code

    Check Declarations :
    Tick required self-declarations

    Submit Form :
    Click “Submit and Get Final OTP”

    Enter Final OTP :
    Confirm and validate with OTP

    Get Certificate :
    Udyam Registration Number and Certificate will be generated

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ What is Udyam Registration??

A: It is an official government registration for MSMEs (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) to get recognition and benefits under the Ministry of MSME.

❓ Is Udyam Registration free of cost??

A: Yes, the registration on the official portal udyamregistration.gov.in is 100% free.

❓ Who can apply for Udyam Registration??

A: Any individual, proprietor, partnership firm, company, or LLP running a small business can apply.

❓ Is Aadhaar mandatory for registration??

A: Yes, Aadhaar is mandatory for the owner/partner/director depending on the business type.

❓ Is PAN and GSTIN required??

A: PAN is mandatory. GSTIN is required if applicable under the GST law.

❓ Do I need to upload any documents??

A: No documents are required to be uploaded. The registration is based on self-declaration.

❓ What are the benefits of Udyam Registration??

A: Access to government schemes, subsidies, loans at lower interest, tax benefits, and priority in government tenders.

❓ How can I download my Udyam Certificate??

A: After successful registration, the certificate is generated instantly and can be downloaded from the portal using your Udyam Number.

❓ Is it necessary to renew Udyam Registration every year??

A: No, there is no need for renewal. It is a one-time registration.

❓ What if I already had Udyog Aadhaar??

A: You must migrate to the new Udyam Registration system on the official website.
