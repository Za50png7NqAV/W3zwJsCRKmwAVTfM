‚ùì What is Udyam Registration??

A: It is an official government registration for MSMEs (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) to get recognition and benefits under the Ministry of MSME.

‚ùì Is Udyam Registration free of cost??

A: Yes, the registration on the official portal udyamregistration.gov.in is 100% free.

‚ùì Who can apply for Udyam Registration??

A: Any individual, proprietor, partnership firm, company, or LLP running a small business can apply.

‚ùì Is Aadhaar mandatory for registration??

A: Yes, Aadhaar is mandatory for the owner/partner/director depending on the business type.

‚ùì Is PAN and GSTIN required??

A: PAN is mandatory. GSTIN is required if applicable under the GST law.

‚ùì Do I need to upload any documents??

A: No documents are required to be uploaded. The registration is based on self-declaration.

‚ùì What are the benefits of Udyam Registration??

A: Access to government schemes, subsidies, loans at lower interest, tax benefits, and priority in government tenders.

‚ùì How can I download my Udyam Certificate??

A: After successful registration, the certificate is generated instantly and can be downloaded from the portal using your Udyam Number.

‚ùì Is it necessary to renew Udyam Registration every year??

A: No, there is no need for renewal. It is a one-time registration.

‚ùì What if I already had Udyog Aadhaar??

A: You must migrate to the new Udyam Registration system on the official website.
