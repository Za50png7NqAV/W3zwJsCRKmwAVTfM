Q. 1. What is Apaar ID or ABC ID??

Apaar ID (also known as Academic Bank of Credits ID or ABC ID) is a 12-digit unique identification number issued by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. It is introduced under the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 to digitally store students‚Äô academic records, certificates, and credit information for easy access and transfer.

Q. 2. Who can apply for Apaar ID??

All students from Class 1 to higher education (school and college level) across India are eligible to apply for an Apaar ID.

Q. 3. Is Apaar ID mandatory??

Yes, many schools, colleges, and universities are now making it mandatory for students to have an Apaar ID for academic record linking and digital identity purposes.

Q. 4. How to apply for Apaar ID??

Students can apply online through DigiLocker, the ABC Portal (abc.gov.in), or the UMANG App. Aadhaar-linked mobile number is required for OTP verification.

Q. 5. What documents are required to generate Apaar ID??

To create an Apaar ID, students need a valid Aadhaar card (linked with mobile), their school or college ID, and basic educational information like class and institution name.

Q. 6. Is Aadhaar mandatory for Apaar ID??

Yes, Aadhaar is mandatory since it is used for identity verification through OTP and to match official student records.

Q. 7. Is there any fee to generate Apaar ID??

No, generating the Apaar ID is completely free of cost. There is no charge for registration or download.

Q. 8. How can I download my Apaar ID card??

Once generated, students can download their Apaar/ABC ID Card from DigiLocker under the ‚ÄúIssued Documents‚Äù section as a PDF.

Q. 9. Can I update my Apaar ID details later??

Some academic details may be updated by your institution. However, any error related to Aadhaar (like name or date of birth) must be corrected via the UIDAI portal.

Q. 10. Will Apaar ID be useful for exams or admissions??

Yes, Apaar ID will play a vital role in examinations, admissions, credit transfers, and creating a centralized academic profile as part of the NEP 2020 framework.
