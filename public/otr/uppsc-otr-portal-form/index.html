Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC)

UPPSC OTR Registration Online Form 2025

Ad
Advertisement

📝 Short Info

Full Form : Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission – One Time Registration
Purpose : Single time profile creation for all UPPSC exams
Website : otr.pariksha.nic.in
Who Can Register : All aspirants of UPPSC exams
Fee : Free of cost
Benefits : Saves time, avoids repetitive data entry, ensures accuracy
Edit Option : Profile can be updated anytime after login
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit the Official Website:
    Go to otr.pariksha.nic.in

    Click on New Registration:
    On the homepage, click on “New Registration”

    Enter Basic Details:
    Fill in your name, DOB, gender, mobile number, and email ID

    Verify OTP:
    Enter OTP received on your mobile and email

    Create Login Credentials:
    Set a username, password, and security question

    Login to OTR Portal:
    Use your new username and password to log in

    Fill Personal Details:
    Enter address, category, ID proof, and other info

    Enter Educational Details:
    Provide 10th, 12th, graduation qualification details

    Upload Documents:
    Upload your photo and signature in the correct format

    Submit and Get OTR Number:
    Preview the form, submit it, and note your OTR number

📄 Importent Documents/Details
✓Aadhaar Card / Any ID Proof
✓Passport Size Photograph
✓Scanned Signature
✓Education Qualification Details
✓Active Mobile Number
✓Valid Email ID
✓Caste Certificate (if applicable)
✓Domicile Certificate (if applicable)

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. What is UPPSC OTR??

✅ UPPSC OTR stands for One Time Registration system by Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission.

Q. Is OTR mandatory for all UPPSC exams??

✅ Yes, OTR is required to apply for most UPPSC recruitments now.

Q. Is UPPSC OTR free of cost??

✅ Yes, the registration is completely free.

Q. Can I edit my OTR profile later??

✅ Yes, you can log in anytime and update your details.

Q. How many times do I need to register??

✅ Only once; you can use the same OTR for all future UPPSC applications.

Q. What documents are needed for OTR??

✅ Passport-size photo, scanned signature, ID proof, and educational details.

Q. Where can I do UPPSC OTR registration??

✅ Visit the official portal otr.pariksha.nic.in

Q. Do I get an OTR number??

✅ Yes, after final submission, an OTR number is sent via SMS and email.

Q. What is the file size for photo and signature??

✅ Photo should be under 200KB and signature under 100KB in JPG/JPEG format.

Q. What if I forget my login details??

✅ You can recover them using your registered email/mobile and security question.
