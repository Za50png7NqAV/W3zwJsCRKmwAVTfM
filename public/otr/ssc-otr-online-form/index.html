Staff Selection Commission (SSC)

SSC One Time Registration (OTR) 2025 – Complete Guide

Ad
Advertisement

📝 Short Info

Full Name : Staff Selection Commission (SSC)
Type : Government Recruitment Agency
Founded : 4 November 1975
Purpose : Create a single profile to apply for multiple SSC exams.
Eligibility : Indian citizen; age and educational qualifications as per post requirements.
Benefits : Single profile for multiple SSC exams
OTP Verification : Mobile & Email mandatory
Official Website : ssc.nic.in
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit Official Website:
    Go to ssc.gov.in

    Open OTR Registration:
    Click on “One Time Registration / OTR” link

    Enter Personal Details:
    Fill name, date of birth, gender, category, and contact details

    Enter Educational Details:
    Provide qualification, year of passing, and marks obtained

    Upload Documents:
    Upload scanned photo, signature, ID proof, and category certificate (if applicable)

    Verify Mobile & Email:
    Enter OTP received on your mobile number and email

    Submit Registration:
    Review all details and submit your OTR form

    Save Registration ID:
    Note your Registration ID and password for future use

    Update Profile (Optional):
    Edit or update profile till last date of registration

    Apply for SSC Exams:
    Use OTR profile to apply for upcoming SSC recruitment

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. Q: What is SSC OTR??

A: SSC OTR (One Time Registration) allows candidates to create a single profile to apply for multiple SSC exams without filling basic details repeatedly.

Q. Q: Who can register for SSC OTR 2025??

A: Any Indian citizen who meets the age and educational qualifications for SSC exams can register.

Q. Q: Can I update my OTR profile??

A: Yes, you can edit or update your profile until the last date of registration.

Q. Q: Do I need a new profile for each SSC exam??

A: No, one OTR profile can be used for all SSC exams.

Q. Q: How will I receive my registration details??

A: After successful registration, a Registration ID and password will be provided. Keep them safe for future applications.

Q. Q: Where can I apply for SSC exams after OTR??

A: Use your OTR profile to apply directly on the SSC official website.
