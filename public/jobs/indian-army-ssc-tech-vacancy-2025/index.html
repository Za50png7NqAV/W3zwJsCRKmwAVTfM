🙋‍♀️ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) -
Q. What is the full form of SSC Tech??

A: SSC Tech stands for Short Service Commission (Technical), a mode of entry into the Indian Army for engineering graduates.

Q. Who can apply for SSC Tech 2025??

A: Unmarried male and female candidates with a B.E./B.Tech degree in relevant engineering disciplines can apply. Widows of Defence Personnel can also apply under a separate category.

Q. What is the last date to apply for SSC Tech 66th Entry 2025??

A: The last date to apply online is 14 August 2025.

Q. Is there any application fee for SSC Tech 2025??

A: No, there is no application fee for this entry.

Q. Where will the training be conducted??

A: Training will be conducted at Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai.

Q. What is the duration of training??

A: The training period is 49 weeks.

Q. Can final year engineering students apply??

A: Yes, final year students can apply, provided they can produce the degree certificate before joining the academy.
