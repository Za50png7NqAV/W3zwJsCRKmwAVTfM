RBI Grade B Recruitment 2025 Notification Out – Apply Online for 120 Vacancies, Eligibility & Exam Dates

Last Update: 11/09/2025 | 09:39 AM

What's Update :
  • Application Started from 10/09/2025

Description: Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: RBISB/BA/03/2025-26 of Officers in Grade ‘B’ (General/DEPR/DSIM). The application process will be open from 10 Sep to 30 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 120 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

Notification : Advt. No.: RBISB/BA/03/2025-26 (Dated: 10 September 2025)

  • Begin Date : 10/09/2025

  • Payment Last Date : 30/09/2025

  • Phase-I Online Exam (Grade ‘B’ DR – DSIM): 19/10/2025
  • Phase-II Online Exam (Grade ‘B’ DR – General): 06/12/2025
  • Phase-II Online Exam (Grade ‘B’ DR – DEPR & DSIM): 07/12/2025

💸 Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹850
  • SC / ST / PwBD: ₹100
  • RBI Staff: ₹0
  • Payment can be made online through UPI, net banking, debit/credit card, etc.
  • For the correct information regarding application fees, please refer to the official notification.
