Bihar Swastha Vibhag Lab Technician Recruitment 2025 – Apply for 1075 Posts, Eligibility & Salary Details

Last Update: 01/09/2025 | 11:27 AM

What's Update :

Description: State Health Society (SHS), Bihar has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: 09/2025 of SHS Laboratory Technician & Senior Technician. The application process will be open from 01 Sep to 15 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 1075 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.
