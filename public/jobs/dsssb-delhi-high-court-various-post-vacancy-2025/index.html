Description: Delhi Subordinate Service Selection Board (DSSSB) has released a Official Notification Advt No.: 03/2025 of DSSSB Attendant. The application process will be open from 26 Aug to 24 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 334 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit Official Website:

    Go to dsssbonline.nic.in.

  • New Registration:

    Click on New Registration and fill in your basic details like name, mobile number, and email ID.

  • Login:

    Use the registration number and password received on your email/mobile to log in.

  • Select Advertisement:

    Choose Advt No. 03/2025 and the post you want to apply for.

  • Fill Application Form:

    Enter personal, educational, and other required details carefully.

  • Upload Documents:

    Upload scanned copies of your photo, signature, and other required certificates in the prescribed format.

  • Pay Application Fee:

    Pay ₹100 (if applicable) online via debit card, credit card, net banking, or UPI.

  • Preview Form:

    Check all details before submitting—once submitted, changes may not be allowed.

  • Submit Application:

    Click Final Submit to complete the process.

  • Print Application:

    Download and print the confirmation page for future reference.
