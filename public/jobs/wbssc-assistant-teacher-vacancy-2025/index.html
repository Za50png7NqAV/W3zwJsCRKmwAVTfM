👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to the official WBSSC portal www.westbengalssc.com

  • Read the Latest Notification:

    Carefully read the Assistant Teacher Recruitment 2025 notification and check eligibility.

  • Go to the Candidate Online Form Section:

    Click on “Apply Online” or navigate to the “Candidate Login/Registration” page.

  • Complete the Registration:

    Register yourself with a valid mobile number and email ID.

  • Fill Out the Application Form:

    Enter personal details, qualification, and post preference correctly.

  • Upload Required Documents:

    Upload scanned copies of your photo, signature, and qualification certificates.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Make the payment through Debit Card, Credit Card, UPI, or Net Banking.

  • Preview and Submit the Form:

    Verify all details and submit the application.

  • Print the Application Form:

    Download and print the submitted application for future reference.
