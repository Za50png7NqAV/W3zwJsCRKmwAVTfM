👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to the official website of MPPTCL- www.mptransco.in Or directly visit the MPOnline portal- www.mponline.gov.in

  • Find the Recruitment Notification:

    Look for the "MPPTCL Recruitment 2025" link under the latest announcements or “Careers” section.

  • Read the Notification Carefully:

    Download and go through the complete notification to understand eligibility, post details, and instructions.

  • Click on the Online Application Link:

    Click on “Apply Online” for the respective recruitment.

  • Register Yourself:

    If you are a new user, complete the one-time registration by providing your basic details (name, mobile number, email, etc.).

  • Login to the Portal:

    Use the registered credentials to log in and access the application form.

  • Fill in the Application Form:

    Provide personal details, academic qualifications, address, category, and post preference.

  • Upload Required Documents:

    Upload scanned copies of Required Documents.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Pay the fee online via Online Mode or MP Online Authorized Kiosk

  • Submit the Application:

    Review all the details carefully and click on the Submit button.

  • Print the Application Form:

    After successful submission, download and print the filled application form for future reference.
