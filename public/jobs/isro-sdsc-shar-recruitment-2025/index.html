ISRO SDSC SHAR Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online for 141 Scientist, Engineer & Technician Posts

Last Update: 18/10/2025 | 11:25 AM

What's Update :
  • Application Started form 16/10/2025

Description: Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: SDSC SHAR/RMT/01/2025 of Scientist, Engineer & Technician. The application process will be open from 16 Oct to 14 Nov 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 141 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the official website :

    www.shar.gov.in or www.apps.shar.gov.in

  • Click on the “Apply Online” link :

    choose the desired post code from the list.

  • Fill in your details :

    enter your name, date of birth, email ID, and mobile number correctly.

  • Upload documents :

    attach recent photograph, signature, and required certificates in the given format.

  • Verify details :

    check all entered information carefully before submission.

  • Submit application :

    after confirming, click “Submit” to complete registration.

  • Note registration number :

    a unique registration number will be generated after submission — keep it safe.

  • Pay application fee :

    pay the prescribed processing fee online before 14/11/2025 (0500 PM).

  • Download confirmation :

    save or print the personalized registration confirmation form for future reference.

  • Keep checking updates :

    follow www.shar.gov.in for admit card, exam date, and further notices.
