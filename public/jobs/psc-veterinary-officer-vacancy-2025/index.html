👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to the Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) website rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.

  • Read the Official Notification:

    Download and read the notification (No. 04/EXAM/V.O./RPSC/EP-I/2025-26) carefully to check eligibility, age, and important details.

  • Complete One Time Registration (OTR):

    If you are a new user, register yourself on the SSO (Single Sign-On) portal of Rajasthan.

  • Login and Fill the Application Form:

    After registration, log in using your SSO ID and find the Veterinary Officer Recruitment 2025 link.

  • Fill Personal and Educational Details:

    Enter all details carefully, including academic qualifications and category details.

  • Upload Documents:

    Upload a passport-size photo, signature, and required certificates in the prescribed format.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Pay the fee online through debit card, credit card, net banking, or UPI as per your category.

  • Preview and Submit the Form:

    Review the application details and submit the form.

  • Take a Printout:

    Save and print the submitted application form for future reference.
