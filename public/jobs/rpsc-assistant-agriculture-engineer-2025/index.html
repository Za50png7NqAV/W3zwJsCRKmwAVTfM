RPSC Assistant Agriculture Engineer Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online for 281 Posts

Last Update: 28/07/2025 | 09:13 AM

What's Update :
  • As per the notification, this will start from 28 July 2025.

Description: Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released a Official Notification Adv. No.: 03/EXAM/A.En.(Agriculture)/RPSC/EP-I/2025-26 of RPSC Assistant Agriculture Engineer. The application process will be open from 28 Jul to 26 Aug 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 281 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

Notification : Adv. No.: 03/EXAM/A.En.(Agriculture)/RPSC/EP-I/2025-26 (Dated: 17 July 2025)

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in

  • Click on "Apply Online":

    Navigate to the "Apply Online" section or visit the SSO Portal sso.rajasthan.gov.in

  • Login/Register on SSO Portal:

    If you're a new user, create an account using your basic details. Existing users can log in with their SSO ID and password.

  • Find the Application Form:

    After login, go to "Recruitment Portal" and click on "Assistant Agriculture Engineer 2025" under ongoing recruitments.

  • Fill the Application Form:

    Enter your personal details, education qualifications, and other required information. Upload scanned documents, including your photograph and signature.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Use online payment methods like Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI to pay the fee. Keep a copy of the payment receipt.

  • Submit and Print:

    After reviewing all details, submit the form. Download and print a copy of the final application form for future reference.
