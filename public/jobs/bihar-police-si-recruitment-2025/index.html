Bihar Police SI Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online for 1799 Sub Inspector Vacancies

Last Update: 26/09/2025 | 11:18 AM

What's Update :
  • Application Started from 26/09/2025

Description: Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: 05/2025 of Bihar Police Sub Inspector. The application process will be open from 26 Sep to 26 Oct 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 1799 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit official website :

    bpssc.bihar.gov.in and click on Advt. 05/2025 apply link

  • Registration :

    Enter name, DOB, mobile number and email ID to get ID & password

  • Login :

    Use registration ID and password to access the application form

  • Fill Details :

    Provide personal info, category and educational qualification

  • Upload Documents :

    Attach recent photo and signature in required size/format

  • Fee Payment :

    Pay ₹100 online via Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card or UPI

  • Preview Form :

    Check all details carefully and correct mistakes if any

  • Final Step :

    Submit application and print acknowledgement slip for future reference
