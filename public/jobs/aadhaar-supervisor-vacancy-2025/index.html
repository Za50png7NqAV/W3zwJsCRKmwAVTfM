üë®‚Äçüíª How to Apply

  • Visit the Official CSC Job Portal:

    Open this website in your browser career.csccloud.in

  • Select Your State Vacancy:

    Find your state from the list (e.g. Bihar, UP, MP) and click on the "Click Here" button.

  • Open the Vacancy Page:

    The link will open a page with complete details of the Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor post for your selected state.

  • Login or Register:

    If you are a CSC VLE, login using your CSC ID and password. If not, you may need to get UIDAI certification via NSEIT first.

  • Fill the Application Form:

    Enter personal details like name, mobile number, address, etc. as asked.

  • Upload Required Documents:

    Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Certificate (UIDAI), Photo ID (like Aadhaar or PAN), Recent passport size photo

  • Submit the Application:

    After reviewing all the information, click on Submit button to complete your application.

  • Confirmation Message:

    You will get an SMS or email confirmation (if required) for next steps or approval.
