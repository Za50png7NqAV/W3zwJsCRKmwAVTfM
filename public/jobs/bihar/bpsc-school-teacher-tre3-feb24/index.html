    BPSC Bihar School Teacher TRE 3.0 Recruitment 2024

    Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC)

    Short Details of Notification Bihar Teacher Bharti TRE 3 Advt No. 22/2024:

    Important Dates

    • Application Begin : 10/02/2024
    • Last Date for Apply Online : 26/02/2024
    • Pay Exam Fee Last Date : 26/02/2024
    • Exam Date :15 March 2024 (Cancelled)
    • Exam Date :16 March 2024 (Postponed)
    • Re Exam Date :19-22 July 2024
    • Admit Card Available :09/07/2024
    • Question Booklet Available :10/08/2024
    • OMR Sheet Available :15-22 August 2024
    • Class 1-5 Answer Key :28/08/2024
    • Class 6-8 Answer Key :30/08/2024
    • Other Class Answer Key :06/09/2024
    • OMR / Response Sheet Available :18/09/2024
    • Result Available :15 November 2024
    • District Allocation List: Available :10/01/2025
    • Marks Available :21 January 2025

    Application Fee

    • General / OBC/ Other State : 750/-
    • SC / ST / PH : 200/-
    • Female Candidate (Bihar Dom.) : 200/-
    • Pay the Exam Fee Through Online / Offline Fee Mode Only

    Eligibility

    • Age Limit as on 01/08/2023
    • Minimum Age : 18 Years for Primary Teacher
    • Minimum Age : 21 Years for TGT / PGT Teacher
    • Maximum Age : 37 Years for Male & 40 Years for Female

    Age relaxation as per rules of Bihar Teacher Bharti TRE 3 Advt No. 22/2024:.

    Vacancy Details

    • Total Vacancy : 86391 Post
    • Bihar School Teacher TRE 3 Exam 2024

    Education-Wise Details

    • Master Degree with 55% Marks and B.Ed – Med 3 Year Degree

    Carefully go through the official notification to understand the recruitment process and requirements.

    • Verify your eligibility and gather essential documents like ID proof, address details, and basic details.
