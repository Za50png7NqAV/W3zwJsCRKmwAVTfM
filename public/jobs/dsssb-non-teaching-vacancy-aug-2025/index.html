DSSSB Non-Teaching Recruitment 2025: Apply Online for 615 Group B & C Posts | Post-wise Eligibility & Vacancy

Last Update: 18/08/2025 | 09:24 AM

What's Update :
  • Application process is open from 18 August 2025 onwards.

Description: Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released a Official Notification Advt. No. : 02/2025 of Delhi DSSSB Various Post. The application process will be open from 18 Aug to 16 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 615 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to the DSSSB online portal dsssbonline.nic.in

  • Register as New User:

    Click on "Click for New Registration" and fill required details

  • Login:

    Use your registered User ID and Password to log in

  • Select Advertisement:

    Choose Advt. No. 02/2025 and your desired Post Code

  • Fill Application Form:

    Enter personal, educational, and experience details carefully

  • Upload Documents:

    Upload scanned photo, signature, and other required certificates

  • Pay Application Fee:

    Pay ₹100 online if applicable (Fee exempted for SC/ST/PwBD/Women)

  • Final Submission:

    Review the form and click on Submit

  • Print Application:

    Download and print the application form and fee receipt for records
