  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to www.ibps.in

  • Click on “CRP Clerical” section:

    Select the link for “Common Recruitment Process for Clerks – XV”

  • Click on “Apply Online”:

    Open the online application link for IBPS Clerk 2025.

  • Register Yourself:

    Click on “New Registration” and fill in your basic details.

    Enter personal details, qualification, and communication info carefully.

  • Upload Required Documents:

    Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, thumb impression, and handwritten declaration in the prescribed format.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Pay the applicable fees through debit/credit card, net banking, or UPI.

    Preview the application and submit it only after verifying all details.

    Save and print the submitted form and payment receipt for future reference.
