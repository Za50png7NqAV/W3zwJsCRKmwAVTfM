HP JBT Teacher Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online, Eligibility, Age Limit & Last Date

Last Update: 14/08/2025 | 10:01 AM

What's Update :

Description: Himachal Pradesh Rajya Chayan Aayog (HPRCA) has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: 02/2025 of Junior Basic Teacher. The application process will be open from 14 Aug to 17 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 600 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

Age Limit as On 01/01/2025

HP JBT Teacher Recruitment 2025 – Post Details

Post Name Total Posts Qualification
Junior Basic Teacher (Job Trainee) 600 10+2 / Senior Secondary (50% marks) with JBT / D.El.Ed. / 4-yr B.El.Ed. OR Graduation with 2-yr JBT / D.El.Ed. OR PG with integrated B.Ed.-M.Ed.
AND TET pass (HPBOSE or authorized agency). SC/ST/OBC/PWD: 5% relaxation.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to hprca.hp.gov.in

  • Register/Login:

    If you are a new user, click on New Registration and fill in basic details (Name, Email, Mobile Number, etc.).

  • Login if Registered:

    If you already have an account, just login using your credentials.

  • Select the Post:

    Choose Junior Basic Teacher (Job Trainee) from the active recruitment list.

  • Enter Personal Details:

    Fill in Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Category, etc.

  • Enter Education Details:

    Provide your educational qualifications and TET details.

  • Upload Documents:

    Upload photo, signature, certificates, and TET proof in the prescribed format.

  • Pay Application Fee:

    Pay ₹800 (for all categories) online via Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking/UPI.

  • Correction Fee (if needed):

    Pay ₹100 to make corrections (if applicable).

  • Review Application:

    Check all entered details carefully before final submission.

  • Submit Application:

    Submit the form online and take a printout for reference.

  • Prepare for Verification:

    Keep the printed application form and original documents ready for document verification by HPRCA.
