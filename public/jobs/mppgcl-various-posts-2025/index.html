MPPGCL Recruitment 2025: Apply Online for 346 AE, JE, Assistant & Other Posts

Last Update: 01/09/2025 | 04:16 PM

Description: Madhya Pradesh Power Generating Company Limited (MPPGCL) has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: 3233 of MPPGCL AE, JE, Assistant & Other Posts. The application process will be open from 23 Jul to 02 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 346 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • ✅ Visit the official website:

    www.mppgcl.mp.gov.in

  • ✅ Read the latest notification:

    Download and read the official PDF notification to check eligibility, important dates, and instructions.

  • ✅ Go to the Candidate Online Form section:

    Click on “Apply Now” or “Candidate Login” on the homepage.

  • ✅ Complete the registration:

    Click on “New Registration” and enter your name, mobile number, email, and other basic details.

  • ✅ Fill out the application form:

    Log in with your credentials, complete personal, educational, and category details.

  • ✅ Pay the application fee:

    Pay using Debit Card / Credit Card / UPI / Net Banking via SBI ePay gateway.

  • ✅ Print the application form:

    After final submission, download and print your filled form and payment receipt for future use.
