👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the official website:

    Go to agnipathvayu.cdac.in

  • Read the latest notification:

    Download and read the official notification PDF carefully to check eligibility, age limit, exam pattern, and important dates.

  • Go to the Candidate Online Form section:

    Click on “Candidate Login” and select Intake 02/2026 link for new registration or login.

  • Complete the registration:

    Click on “New User? Register”, Enter your mobile number, email ID, and create a password, Verify OTP sent to your mobile/email

  • Fill out the application form:

    Login with your credentials Enter personal details, educational qualification, and communication address Select your exam city preferences Upload passport size photograph, signature, and other documents as per the instructions

  • Pay the application fee:

    Pay the fee through Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI Application Fee ₹550 + GST

  • Print the application form:

    After final submission, download and print the filled application form,Keep a copy for future reference.
