👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to jssc.jharkhand.gov.in (Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission official site).

  • Read the Latest Notification:

    Carefully go through the ANM (Regular & Backlog) Recruitment 2025 notification for eligibility and important details.

  • Go to the Candidate Online Form Section:

    Click on the "Online Application for JANMCE-2025" link available on the homepage or recruitment page.

  • Complete the Registration:

    Register yourself with a valid mobile number and email ID to generate a unique registration ID and password.

  • Fill Out the Application Form:

    Log in and fill all the required personal, educational, and eligibility details accurately.

  • Upload Documents:

    Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and relevant certificates.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Pay the fee online via debit/credit card, UPI, or net banking as per your category.

  • Print the Application Form:

    After successful submission, download and print the filled application form and payment receipt for future reference.
