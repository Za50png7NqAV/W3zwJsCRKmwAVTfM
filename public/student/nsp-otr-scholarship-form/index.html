Point Details
What is NSP OTR? NSP OTR stands for One Time Registration on the National Scholarship Portal.
Why is it necessary? It is mandatory for students to apply for any scholarship under NSP from 2024-25 onward.
Purpose To simplify and unify the registration process and prevent duplicate or fake applications.
Who needs to register? All new applicants (Fresh students) applying for scholarships through NSP.
When to register? Before applying for any scholarship scheme on NSP.
Benefits Single ID for lifetime, better transparency, Aadhaar-based verification.
Documents required Aadhaar Card, Mobile Number, and basic student details.
Registration mode Online through NSP official website or NSP OTR Mobile App.
Verification method Aadhaar authentication via OTP or biometric.
OTR ID use This ID is used to log in and apply for all future scholarships on NSP.

‚ùì What is NSP OTR??

A. NSP OTR (One Time Registration) is a mandatory process to generate a unique ID before applying for any scholarship on the National Scholarship Portal.

‚ùì Is OTR compulsory for all students??

A. Yes, from academic year 2025 onwards, OTR is mandatory for all students applying for scholarships on NSP.

‚ùì How many times do I need to register for OTR??

A. Only once. OTR is a one-time process for lifetime use.

‚ùì What documents are required for OTR??

A. Only your Aadhaar number linked with an active mobile number is required.

‚ùì Can I complete OTR without an Aadhaar-linked mobile number??

A. No, Aadhaar-linked mobile number is mandatory to receive OTP for verification.

‚ùì What is the Reference Number generated after registration??

A. It is a temporary ID you get after submitting basic details. You must log in to the NSP OTR mobile app to complete Aadhaar authentication and receive your final OTR ID.

‚ùì How do I get the NSP OTR mobile app??

A. Download it from the Google Play Store by searching "NSP OTR".

‚ùì What if I lose my Reference Number or OTR ID??

A. You can recover it using the ‚ÄúForgot OTR ID‚Äù option on the portal or contact the NSP support team.

‚ùì Can I edit my details after submitting the OTR form??

A. No, once Aadhaar verification is complete, details cannot be edited. Be careful during entry.

‚ùì Without OTR, can I fill the scholarship application form??

A. No, you cannot access or submit any scholarship form on NSP without a valid OTR ID.
