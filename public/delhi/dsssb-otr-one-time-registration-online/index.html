Q. What is DSSSB One-Time Registration (OARS)??

A: It is a system to create a unique login ID and password for applying to multiple DSSSB posts without entering personal and academic details repeatedly.

Q. Who can register for DSSSB OARS??

A: Any candidate meeting the eligibility criteria of posts under DSSSB can register, provided they have Class 10 certificate and valid ID proof.

Q. What documents are required for registration??

A: Class 10 certificate, valid ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport), photograph, signature, left & right thumb impressions, and category certificate if applicable.

Q. Can I edit my details after registration? ?

A: No, once submitted, registration details cannot be edited. Verify everything before final submission.

Q. How do I recover my Registration Number or Password??

A: Visit the DSSSB login page, click ‚ÄúForgot Registration No./Password,‚Äù enter your DOB, Class 10 Roll Number, and Passing Year, and follow instructions to reset.

Q. Is there a validity period for the One-Time Registration??

A: Registration remains valid until you apply for posts or until DSSSB updates its system.
