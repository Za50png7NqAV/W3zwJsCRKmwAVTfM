MP Anganwadi Bharti 2026: Apply Online for 2548 Worker & Assistant/Helper Posts

Last Update: 01/07/2026 | 06:46 PM

What's Update :

Description: Women and Child Development, Govt of Madhya Pradesh has released a Official Notification Notification No.: क्र./मवावि/SAP-2/2026/2523 of Madhya Pradesh Anganwadi Worker & Helper Bharti Phase 1. The application process will be open from 01 Jul to 13 Jul 2026 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 2548 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit Official Website:

    Open the MP Online Selection Portal.

  • Open Recruitment Link:

    Click on the MP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 application link.

  • Registration:

    Register using your valid mobile number and email ID.

  • Select Post:

    Choose the Anganwadi Worker or Anganwadi Assistant post.

  • Fill Application Form:

    Enter your personal, educational, and communication details carefully.

  • Select Vacancy Area:

    Choose the vacancy of your same Gram Panchayat (Rural) or same Ward (Urban).

  • Upload Documents:

    Upload your photograph, signature, and other required documents in the prescribed format.

  • Pay Application Fee:

    Complete the online fee payment through the available payment options.

  • Submit Application:

    Submit the completed online application form.

  • Print Application:

    Download and print the final submitted application form for future reference.
