‚ùì What is the selection process for this scholarship program??

The selection for the SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025‚Äì26 is based on academic merit and financial background and involves a multi-stage process: 1) Initial shortlisting of applications based on academic performance and family income. 2) Telephonic interviews with shortlisted candidates. 3) Document verification for final selection.

‚ùì How will I receive the scholarship funds if selected for this program??

The scholarship amount will be directly transferred into the bank account of the selected scholars through secure digital payment.

‚ùì Will I receive this scholarship for subsequent years of study??

SBI Foundation aims to provide continuous financial support to students selected under this scholarship till the completion of their ongoing course, subject to renewal conditions.
