📝 Short Info

Post Details : SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025
Application Date : 19 September 2025 to 15 November 2025
Department : SBI Foundation (CSR initiative of State Bank of India)
Scholarship Type : Education Scholarship (Financial Assistance for Students)
Benefits : Financial aid up to ₹20 lakh per year for meritorious students from Class 9 to Postgraduate level
Educational Qualification : Minimum 75% marks or 7.0 CGPA in the previous class (10% relaxation for SC/ST students)
Class / Level : Class 9, 10, 11, 12, Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Professional, and Medical students
Reservation : 50% seats reserved for female candidates and special quota for SC/ST categories
📄 Importent Documents/Details
✓Marksheet of the previous class
✓Aadhaar card
✓Income certificate / Form 16 / Salary slip
✓Admission proof / College ID / Bonafide certificate
✓Current year fee receipt
✓Passport-size photograph
✓Copy of bank passbook
✓Caste certificate (if applicable)

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ What is the selection process for this scholarship program??

The selection for the SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025–26 is based on academic merit and financial background and involves a multi-stage process: 1) Initial shortlisting of applications based on academic performance and family income. 2) Telephonic interviews with shortlisted candidates. 3) Document verification for final selection.

❓ How will I receive the scholarship funds if selected for this program??

The scholarship amount will be directly transferred into the bank account of the selected scholars through secure digital payment.

❓ Will I receive this scholarship for subsequent years of study??

SBI Foundation aims to provide continuous financial support to students selected under this scholarship till the completion of their ongoing course, subject to renewal conditions.

❓ How to apply for the SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025–26??

To apply, visit the official Buddy4Study website – https://www.buddy4study.com. Register or log in, complete your profile, go to the SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship section, fill out the form, upload required documents, and submit your application online.

❓ How can I check my scholarship status??

After submitting your application, log in to your Buddy4Study account. Go to the “Applied Scholarships” section to view your current application status and updates.

❓ Is an SBI bank account mandatory to apply for this scholarship??

No, it is not mandatory to have an SBI bank account. You may provide details of any valid bank account for receiving the scholarship funds.

❓ Which document can I submit instead of a fee receipt if I study in a government school where no fees are paid??

If your government school doesn’t charge fees, you can upload a Letter or Certificate from the school head confirming your enrollment and that no fees are collected.

❓ Are diploma students eligible to apply for this scholarship??

No, diploma students are not eligible for the SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025–26. The scholarship is open only to: Students from Classes 9 to 12, and Those pursuing Undergraduate or Postgraduate courses in Top 300 NIRF-ranked institutions, IITs, or IIMs.

❓ My college is not in the Top 300 NIRF list. Can I apply for the Postgraduate scholarship??

No, to apply for the UG/PG scholarship, your institution must be among the Top 300 NIRF-ranked colleges/universities. For study abroad scholarships, you must be enrolled in a Top 200 university in the QS or THE World Rankings (2024–25).

❓ I have a gap of 2 years after 12th/Graduation. Can I apply??

Yes, a gap of up to 2 years after 12th or graduation does not disqualify you, provided you currently meet all other eligibility conditions.

❓ I am an orphan and don't have an earning member in my family. What document should I provide under the income certificate section??

You can submit a certificate from the local government authority or a recognized institution confirming your orphan status, along with a no-income affidavit or a letter describing your financial condition.

❓ When will the scholarship results be announced??

The final selection list is usually released within 6–8 weeks after the last date of application, depending on the number of applicants.

❓ Whom should I contact for any technical or application-related issues??

You can contact the Buddy4Study helpline or SBI Foundation support team at sbiashascholarship@sbifoundation.co.in 📞 011-430-92248 (Ext-303)
