📝 Short Info

Scholarship Name : Azim Premji Scholarship 2025
Organizer : Azim Premji Foundation
Purpose : To support girl students from disadvantaged backgrounds in higher education
Benefit : ₹30,000 per year (for full duration of 1st UG/Diploma course – 2 to 5 years)
Eligibility : Must have passed Class 10 & 12 from a government school/college || Must be admitted in 1st year of UG/Diploma (2025–26 session)
Eligible States/UTs : 20 (including Bihar, MP, UP, Rajasthan, Odisha, Assam, etc.)
Application Start Date : 10 September 2025
Documents Required : Class 10 & 12 Mark Sheets, Aadhaar, Admission Proof, Photo, Bank Statement, Signature
Application Fee : None (free of cost)

Azim Premji Scholarship Eligibility 2025
Eligibility Criteria Details
Gender Only Girl Students
Background From disadvantaged / economically weaker families
Education Must have passed Class 10 & 12 as a regular student from a Government school/college
Course First-year admission in Undergraduate Degree/Diploma (2–5 years)
College/University Recognized Government or credible private institution in India
Mode of Study Regular only (Distance learning not allowed)
Renewal Must maintain satisfactory academic performance
Eligible States / UTs Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Puducherry, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Telangana, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand
Not Eligible Distance learning, second degree, or postgraduate courses

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ Q. Who can apply for the Azim Premji Scholarship? ?

A. Girl students from disadvantaged backgrounds who meet the eligibility criteria can apply.

❓ Q. What is the amount of the scholarship? ?

A. The scholarship provides INR 30,000 annually for the full duration of the first undergraduate degree or diploma.

❓ Q. Is the scholarship available for all courses? ?

A. Yes, it supports the first undergraduate degree or diploma course in any recognized institution.

❓ Q. Do I need to renew the scholarship every year? ?

A. Yes, the scholarship must be renewed annually by providing proof of continued enrollment and good academic performance.

❓ Q. What documents are required for application? ?

A. Class 10 & 12 marksheets, Aadhaar card, admission proof, recent passport-size photo, self-signed bank statement, and scanned signature.

❓ Q. How can I apply for the scholarship? ?

A. Go to the official site ~~ 👉 azimpremjifoundation.org and fill out the online application form.

❓ Q. Is there any application fee? ?

A. No, there is no application fee to apply for the scholarship.

❓ Q. When is the last date to apply? ?

A. The last date is mentioned in the official notification on the foundation’s website.
