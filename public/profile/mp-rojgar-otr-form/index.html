Department of Skill Development and Employment, Govt. of MP

Madhya Pradesh Rojgar Panjiyan 2025

Ad
Advertisement

📝 Short Info

Portal : mprojgar.gov.in – Official MP Employment Portal
Eligibility : Resident of Madhya Pradesh
Minimum qualification : 10th pass
Validity : Valid for 3 years
Features : Job fair updates, registration print, vacancy alerts, experience upload
Offices : District employment offices available across MP
Fees : Entire process is Free of cost
Access : Accessible via both mobile and desktop devices
Renewal : Update profile and renew online through portal login
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit the official website:
    Go to mprojgar.gov.in

    Click on Job Seeker Registration:
    Choose “Job Seeker” from the homepage

    Select New Registration:
    Click on “New Registration” if you don’t already have an account

    Enter personal details:
    Fill in your name, Aadhaar number, mobile number, email, and create a password

    Verify mobile number with OTP:
    Enter the OTP received on your mobile for verification

    Login to your account:
    Use your mobile number and password to log in

    Fill out the profile form:
    Enter educational qualification, skills, experience, and address details

    Upload required documents:
    Upload scanned copies of Aadhaar, certificates, and photo

    Submit the application:
    Review all details and click on “Submit” to complete registration

    Print the registration certificate:
    Go to your dashboard and click on “Print Registration”

Aspect Description
Job Notifications Registered candidates receive timely updates on government and private job openings.
Participation in Job Fairs Only registered individuals can take part in employment/job fairs organized by the government.
Access to Government Schemes Employment registration is required to apply for schemes like self-employment and apprenticeship programs.
Official Record Inclusion Helps the government maintain accurate unemployment statistics and plan new policies.
Proof of Registration The registration certificate is considered valid for various official purposes.
Renewal Keeps Data Active Regular renewal ensures your employment profile remains active and up-to-date.
Online Convenience The entire process is digital, reducing the need for physical visits to employment offices.
🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. What is MP Rojgar Panjiyan??

A: It is the Employment Registration Portal of Madhya Pradesh where job seekers can register themselves to get job opportunities and participate in employment fairs.

Q. Who can apply for MP Rojgar Panjiyan??

A: Any resident of Madhya Pradesh who is 14 years or older and has at least passed Class 10th is eligible to register.

Q. Is it mandatory to have an Aadhaar card for registration??

A: Yes, a valid Aadhaar number is mandatory for verification during the registration process.

Q. Is there any application fee??

A: No, the registration process is completely free of cost.

Q. What is the validity of the registration??

A: The registration remains valid for 3 years. After that, it needs to be renewed.

Q. How can I renew my registration??

A: You can log in to your account on the portal and select the “Renew Registration” option.

Q. Will I get job offers directly after registration??

A: No, registration makes you eligible for job alerts and participation in employment fairs, but selection depends on job criteria and employer requirements.

Q. Can I update my information after registration??

A: Yes, you can log in anytime and update your details such as address, qualification, or mobile number.

Q. Can I register offline??

A: No, the process is entirely online through the official portal: https://mprojgar.gov.in

Q. Is there a helpline for technical issues??

A: Yes, you can find contact/helpline details on the official website under the “Contact Us” section.
