📝 Short Info

Name : MPTAAS – Madhya Pradesh Tribal Affairs Automation System
Purpose : To determine eligibility for Post Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC)
Benefit : Direct scholarship payment to bank account via DBT
Process : Online profile registration on the official portal
e-KYC : Verification via Aadhaar OTP or biometric
Without Profile : You cannot apply for any scholarship under MPTAAS
📄 Importent Documents/Details
✓Samagra ID
✓Aadhaar Card
✓Caste Certificate
✓Domicile Certificate
✓Income Certificate
✓Mobile Number
🎓 Scholarship Name 🧒 Eligibility
Post Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC) For SC/ST/OBC students from Class 11 to PhD
Higher Education Scholarship For SC/ST students enrolled in Engineering, Medical, Law, Management, etc.
Overseas Scholarship For SC/ST students pursuing Master's or PhD abroad with strong academic record
Civil Services Coaching Scholarship For SC/ST students preparing for UPSC/MPPSC/SSC exams
Laptop/Tablet Scheme For meritorious SC/ST/OBC students in specific courses
Education Incentive Scheme (Shiksha Protsahan Yojna) For SC/ST students with top ranks in Board/College exams
Pre-Matric Scholarship For SC/ST/OBC students from Class 1 to 10
Topic Description
Introduction MPTAAS (Madhya Pradesh Tribal Affairs Automation System) is a digital profile required for SC/ST/OBC students to avail government scheme benefits.
Purpose To provide transparent and quick access to scholarships, financial aid, and government schemes.
Benefits Easy application for scholarships, online tracking, direct payment to bank accounts, etc.
Eligibility Students belonging to SC/ST/OBC categories of Madhya Pradesh, within prescribed income limits, enrolled in recognized institutions.
Required Documents Samagra ID, caste certificate, domicile certificate, Aadhaar card, bank passbook, passport-size photo.
Where It Is Useful Post Matric Scholarship, financial assistance, scheme benefits, bank payments, document verification.
How to Update Profile Login to your account, update necessary details, and re-upload documents if needed.
Important Notes Do not provide false information, link your bank account with Aadhaar, regularly check application status.
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit the official website:
    Go to www.tribal.mp.gov.in/MPTAAS

    Click on "New Beneficiary Profile Registration":
    On the homepage, find and select this option under the scholarship section.

    Enter your personal details:
    Fill in your Samagra ID, Aadhaar number, name, caste, address, etc.

    Upload required documents:
    Aadhaar card, caste certificate, domicile, bank passbook, passport size photo.

    Verify mobile number via OTP:
    Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number to continue.

    Set login password:
    Create a secure password to access your profile in the future.

    Complete e-KYC process:
    Use Aadhaar OTP or biometric for identity verification (if prompted).

    Submit the registration form:
    Double-check all details, then click Submit.

    Print the confirmation:
    Save or print the registration acknowledgment for future use.

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ What is MPTAAS??

Ans: MPTAAS stands for Madhya Pradesh Tribal Affairs Automation System. It is an online portal for SC/ST/OBC students of Madhya Pradesh to apply for various scholarships.

❓ Is MPTAAS a scheme??

Ans: No, it is not a scheme. It is a digital platform used to apply for post-matric scholarship schemes and maintain student profiles.

❓ Why is MPTAAS profile registration important??

Ans: Without registering your profile on MPTAAS, you cannot apply for any post-matric scholarship under the tribal or social welfare department.

❓ Who is eligible to register on MPTAAS??

Ans: Students who are residents of Madhya Pradesh and belong to Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or Other Backward Class (OBC) studying in recognized institutions.

❓ What should I do after profile registration??

Ans: After successful registration, log in to your account and apply for the “Post Matric Scholarship” under the scholarship section.

❓ Is the profile registration a one-time process??

Ans: Yes, profile creation is done once. However, you must log in every year to apply or renew your scholarship application.

❓ What is the official website of MPTAAS??

Ans: https://www.tribal.mp.gov.in/MPTAAS

❓ What if I forget my password??

Ans: Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page and reset it using your registered mobile number or email.

❓ What if I don’t have a Samagra ID??

Ans: Visit your nearest CSC center or apply online at https://samagra.gov.in to get a Samagra ID.
