Rajasthan RCRB Various Post Result 2025 Out

📝 Short Info

Name of the Examination: Rajasthan Co-operative Bank
Post Name : Clerk, Junior Assistant, Salesman, Manager & Other Posts
Result Date : 24 June 2025
Result Type : Merit List & Cutoff
Job Location : Rajasthan
Download Mode : Online PDF
👨‍💻 How to do:

    ✅ Visit the Official Website:
    Go to the official website rajcrb.rajasthan.gov.in

    🔍 Find the Result Section:
    On the homepage, look for a link that says “Result for Various Posts 2025” or check the Latest News / Notifications section.

    📄 Click on the Relevant Link:
    Click on the link mentioning the post name (e.g., Clerk, Junior Assistant, etc.) to open the result page.

    🧑‍💻 Download the Result PDF:
    The result will be displayed in PDF format containing roll numbers or names of selected candidates.

    🔍 Search Your Roll Number:
    Use Ctrl + F and enter your Roll Number or Name to check your result.

    💾 Save or Print:
    Download and save the result PDF for future reference. You can also take a printout if required.

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. 1. For which posts has the RCRB Result 2025 been declared??

👉 The result has been declared for Clerk, Junior Assistant, Manager, Salesman, and other various posts.

Q. 2. When was the Rajasthan RCRB Result 2025 released??

👉 The result was released in June 2025 on the official website.

Q. 3. Where can I download the RCRB result??

👉 You can download the result PDF from the official website: rajcrb.rajasthan.gov.in

Q. 4. What details are mentioned in the result??

👉 The result PDF contains the roll numbers or names of selected candidates, along with post-wise selection details.

Q. 5. What if my name is not in the merit list??

👉 If your name or roll number is not in the list, it means you have not been selected. You can try in future recruitments.

Q. 6. Is the cutoff also released with the result??

👉 Yes, the cutoff marks for each post/category are usually released along with or separately on the official website.

Q. 7. When will the document verification take place??

👉 The date and venue for document verification will be announced separately or mentioned in the selection list.
