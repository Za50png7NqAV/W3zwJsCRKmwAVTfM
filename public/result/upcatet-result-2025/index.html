Uttar Pradesh Combined Agriculture and Technology Entrance Test (UPCATET)

UPCATET Result 2025 – Out

📝 Short Info

Exam Name : UPCATET 2025 (Uttar Pradesh Combined Agriculture & Technology Entrance Test)
Conducted By : Chandra Shekhar Azad University of Agriculture & Technology, Kanpur
Exam Date : 11 June to 12 June 2025
Result Release Date : June 24, 2025
Courses Offered : UG, PG, PhD (Agriculture & Allied Fields)
Official Website : upcatet.net
Next Step : Counselling & Admission Process
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit the official website:
    Go to upcatet.net

    Find the result link:
    On the homepage, look for the “UPCATET Result 2025” or “Download Entrance Result / Scorecard” link and click it

  • scroll.in

    • Log in with your credentials:
    Select your Programme and Group of Paper, Enter your 6-digit Registration/User ID or roll number, Provide your Password, and complete the security/captcha answer

    View your result:
    Upon clicking Submit or Login, your Scorecard or Rank Card will appear, showing- Name, roll number, programme & exam centre, Subject‑wise marks, total scores, group and category rank, Qualification status

    Download & print:
    lick Download PDF to save a copy Print multiple copies for future reference during counselling/admissions

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. 1. When was the UPCATET 2025 result declared??

The result was released on June 24, 2025 on the official website upcatet.net.

Q. 2. Where can I check my UPCATET 2025 result??

You can check your result on the official website: upcatet.net by logging in with your registration credentials.

Q. 3. What details are required to download the result??

You will need: Programme & Group of Paper, Registration/User ID, Password, Captcha/security answer

Q. 4. What is mentioned in the scorecard??

The scorecard includes: Candidate's name, roll number, programme, Subject-wise marks and total marks, Category and group-wise rank, Qualification status

Q. 5. What is the minimum qualifying mark in UPCATET??

There is no fixed cutoff; admission is based on merit and rank. However, higher scores (e.g., 400+ for UG) improve chances of selection.

Q. 6. When will UPCATET 2025 counselling begin??

Counselling for UG, PG, and PhD courses is expected to start from July 21, 2025 (tentative). Keep checking the website for updates.
