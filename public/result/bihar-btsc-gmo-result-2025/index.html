👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit the Official Website:
    Go to btsc.bih.nic.in or pariksha.nic.in, the official result portal.

    Find the Result SectionL:
    On the homepage, look for the link titled “BTSC GMO Result 2025” under the latest announcements or result section.

    Click on the Result Link:
    Click the relevant link for General Medical Officer Result 2025.

    Download the PDF:
    A PDF file containing the list of shortlisted candidates will open. Click the download icon or press Ctrl + S to save the file.

    Search Your Roll Number:
    Open the downloaded PDF and use Ctrl + F to search your Roll Number/Name.
