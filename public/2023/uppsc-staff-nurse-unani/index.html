    UPPSC Staff Nurse (Unani) Male / Female Recruitment 2023

    Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC)

    Short Details of Notification UPPSC Staff Nurse Exam A8/E-1/2023

    Important Dates

    • Application Begin:04/12/2023
    • Last Date for Apply Online:01/01/2024
    • Pay Exam Fee Last Date:01/01/2024
    • Correction Last Date:11/01/2024
    • Exam Date:08/09/2024
    • Admit Card Available:29/08/2024
    • Pre Exam Result Available:17/10/2024
    • Mains Exam Date:16/02/2025
    • Admit Card Available:12/02/2025

    Application Fee

    • General / OBC / EWS:125/-
    • SC / ST:65/-
    • PH Candidates:25/-

    • Pay the Examination Fee Through Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking or Pay Offline Through E Challan

    Eligibility

    • Age Limit as on 01/07/2023

    • Minimum Age:21 Years
    • Maximum Age:40 Years

    Age relaxation as per rules of UPPSC Staff Nurse Exam A8/E-1/2023.

    Vacancy Details

    • Details Vacancy : 27 Post

    Education-Wise Details

    UPPSC Staff Nurse Unani Recruitment 2023
    Post Name Staff Nurse (Unani) - Male/Female
    Total Posts 27
    Gender-wise Distribution Male: 02 Posts
    Female: 25 Posts
    Eligibility Criteria 1. Class 10th with Science and Intermediate Exam Passed
    2. Diploma in Medical and Surgical Nursing Unani (Registrable with the Ayurvedic and Unani Tibbi Medicine Board of UP)
    3. Diploma in Midwifery Unani (Registrable with the Ayurvedic and Unani Tibbi Medicine Board)
    4. Registration Certificate as Unani Nurse and Midwife Dhatri from Ayurvedi and Unani Tibbi Medicine Board of UP
    Additional eligibility details to be announced
