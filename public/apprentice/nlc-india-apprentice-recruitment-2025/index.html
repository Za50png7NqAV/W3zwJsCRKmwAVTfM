NLC India Limited Apprenticeship 2025 – Apply Online for 163 Trade, Diploma & Graduate Posts

Last Update: 04/10/2025 | 05:22 PM

What's Update :

Description: NLC India Limited has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: NLCIL-BP/01/2025 of NLC Apprentice. The application process will be open from 03 Oct to 23 Oct 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 163 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Register on NATS/NAPS portal :

    Update your apprentice profile completely.

  • Visit:

    www.nlcindia.in Career - Trainee & Apprentices - Advt. No. BP 01/2025 Open application link.

  • Fill the online application form :

    Enter all required personal, academic and registration details.

  • Submit application :

    Take a printout of the registration cum application form.

  • Attach documents :

    Enclose self-attested copies of certificates, ID and proofs.

  • Send application form :

    By Post to NLC Barsingsar Project Office OR submit at BTPS Gate No. 2 Drop Box.

  • Last Date :

    Ensure form and documents reach on or before 30/10/2025, 5 PM.
