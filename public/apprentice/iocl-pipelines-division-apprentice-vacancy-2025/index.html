IOCL Pipelines Division Apprentice Recruitment 2025 | Apply Online for 537 Vacancies

Last Update: 19/09/2025 | 10:34 AM

What's Update :
  • Last Date Extended from 18/09/2025 to 28/09/2025

Description: Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: PL/HR/ESTB/APPR(2025)-2 of IOCL Pipelines Division Apprentice. The application process will be open from 29 Aug to 28 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 537 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

Post Name Qualification Requirement
Technician Apprentice (Mechanical) 3-year full-time Diploma in Mechanical Engineering / Automobile Engineering

Technician Apprentice (Electrical) 3-year full-time Diploma in Electrical Engineering / Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Technician Apprentice (Telecommunication & Instrumentation) 3-year full-time Diploma in any of: Electronics & Communication / Electronics & Telecommunication / Electronics & Radio Communication / Instrumentation & Control / Instrumentation & Process Control / Electronics Engineering

Trade Apprentice (Assistant – Human Resource) Full-time Bachelor’s Degree (any discipline) from a Govt. recognized Institute/University
Trade Apprentice (Accountant) Full-time Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce (B.Com) from a Govt. recognized Institute/University
Data Entry Operator (Fresher) Minimum 12th Pass (but below Graduate)
Domestic Data Entry Operator (Skill Certificate Holders) Minimum 12th Pass (but below Graduate) and Skill Certificate of “Domestic Data Entry Operator” (training < 1 year) issued by an NSQF/central Govt. recognized awarding body

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit Website :

    Go to the official IOCL website www.iocl.com and open the “Careers - Apprenticeships” section.

  • Read Notification :

    Download and read the detailed advertisement Advt. No. PL/HR/ESTB/APPR (2025)-2 carefully.

  • Registration on Portal :

    Register yourself on NAPS (for Trade Apprentice) / NATS (for Technician Apprentice) portal.

  • Apply Online :

    Click on the “Apply Online” link under Pipelines Division Apprentice Recruitment 2025.

  • Fill Details :

    Enter your personal details, educational qualification, trade preference, and upload required documents.

  • Upload Documents :

    Upload scanned copies of photograph, signature, qualification certificates, caste/PwBD certificate (if applicable).

  • Final Submit :

    Re-check all details carefully and submit the form.

  • Print Application :

    Take a printout of the application form for future reference during document verification.
