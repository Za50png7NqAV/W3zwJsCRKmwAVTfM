What's Update :

Description: Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Eastern Railway has released a Official Notification RRC-ER/Act Apprentices/2025-26 of ER Apprentice. The application process will be open from 14 Aug to 13 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 3115 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. 👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Portal:

    Go to the Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Eastern Railway official website.

  • Download the Notification:

    Read the detailed advertisement for eligibility, trades, and instructions.

  • Register Online:

    Create an account using your email, mobile number, and basic details.

  • Login to the Portal:

    Use your registered credentials to access the application form.

  • Fill the Application Form:

    Enter personal, educational, and trade preference details carefully.

  • Upload Documents:

    Submit scanned copies of your photo, signature, 10th and ITI certificates, and other relevant documents.

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Make online payment (₹100 for UR/OBC/EWS; exempted for SC/ST/Women/PwBD).

  • Review Your Application:

    Cross-check all filled details and uploaded documents before final submission.

  • Submit the Form:

    Click on final submit and note down your application/reference number.

  • Save or Print:

    Keep a copy of the submitted application form for future reference.
