Rajasthan SET 2026 – Post Details & Eligibility
Post Name Qualification / Eligibility
Rajasthan SET 2026 Candidates must have a Master's Degree or equivalent examination from a UGC-recognized University/Institution with at least 55% marks (General/EWS) or 50% marks (OBC-NCL/SC/ST/PwD/Third Gender). Candidates appearing in the final year/semester of their Master's Degree or awaiting results are also eligible to apply provisionally.
Note: Candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully for detailed eligibility criteria, subject-wise requirements, reservation benefits, and other important instructions before applying.
