Super 100 Benefits

✅ Student Benefits:

  • 🎓 Free Coaching: Preparation for JEE, NEET, CLAT, etc., from top institutes
  • 🏠 Free Hostel & Meal Facility
  • 📚 Free Study Materials and Test Series
  • 👨‍🏫 Guidance by Experienced Teachers
  • 🧠 Competitive Environment & Motivation
  • 🎯 Structured Preparation Towards Career Goals

🧾 Eligibility:

  • Only students studying in government schools of Madhya Pradesh
  • Currently enrolled in Class 10 (Academic Session 2024–25)
  • Must qualify the designated entrance examination

📝 Selection Process:

  • State-level Entrance Examination
  • Subjects: Mathematics, Science, and Logical Reasoning
  • Selection will be based on merit
  • Selected students will be admitted to coaching institutes
👨‍💻 How to do:

    Visit the Official Portal:
    Go to the official website where the Super 100 application form is available.

    Enter Class 10 Roll Number:
    Enter your Class 10 roll number and the captcha code. Then click the "Search" button.

    Student Details Will Auto-Fill:
    Your name, school name, date of birth, etc., will be auto-filled from the database.

    Enter Mobile Number & Select Exam Center:
    Provide your active mobile number and choose your preferred exam center from the list.

    Upload Required Documents:
    Upload the necessary documents such as your photo, signature, etc.

    Review the Application Form:
    Carefully check all the details filled in the form. Make corrections if needed.

    Pay the Application Fee (if applicable):
    Pay the required fee using available modes like Net Banking, UPI, Debit/Credit Card, or MP Online Kiosk.

    Final Submit and Download Receipt:
    Click the Final Submit button and download/print the application receipt for your records.

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
❓ What is the Super 100 Examination??

Ans: Super 100 is a government scheme in Madhya Pradesh that provides free coaching, hostel, and study facilities to meritorious Class 10 students for preparation of JEE, NEET, and other competitive exams.

❓ Who can apply for the Super 100 Examination 2025??

Ans: Students studying in Class 10 during the academic session 2024–25 in government schools of Madhya Pradesh are eligible to apply.

❓ What is the mode of application??

Ans: The application process is completely online through the official MP Online portal.

❓ Is there any application fee??

Ans: Yes, a nominal fee may be applicable, which can be paid online or through MP Online kiosks.

❓ What documents are required while applying??

Ans: Aadhaar card, Class 9 marksheet, domicile certificate, caste certificate (if applicable), passport-size photo, and signature.

❓ How is the selection done??

Ans: Selection is based on a state-level entrance exam, which tests students’ knowledge in subjects like Mathematics and Science.

❓ What benefits do selected students get??

Ans: Selected students receive free coaching, residential hostel, study materials, and expert mentorship.

❓ What is the last date to apply??

Ans: The last date to submit the application is generally around 20 July 2025 (subject to official announcement).

❓ Can students from private schools apply??

Ans: No, the scheme is only for students of government schools in Madhya Pradesh.

❓ When will the admit card and exam date be announced??

Ans: Admit card and exam date will be announced on the official website after the application deadline.
