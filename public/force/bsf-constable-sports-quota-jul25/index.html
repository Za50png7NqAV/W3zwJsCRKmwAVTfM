👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the official website:

    Go to the BSF Recruitment Portal – rectt.bsf.gov.in

  • Read the notification:

    Carefully read the official notification for eligibility, sports criteria, and instructions.

  • Register yourself:

    Click on the “New Registration” link and complete the registration process using your valid mobile number and email ID.

  • Login to fill the form:

    Use your registered credentials to log in and access the application form.

  • Fill in personal & qualification details:

    Enter your basic details, educational qualification, and sports achievements.

  • Upload documents:

    Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, 10th mark sheet, sports certificate, and other required documents.

  • Pay the application fee:

    Pay the applicable fee online using Net Banking, UPI, Debit/Credit Card.

  • Submit the application:

    Review all the information carefully and click on “Submit”.

  • Print the application:

    Take a printout of the submitted application form for future reference.
