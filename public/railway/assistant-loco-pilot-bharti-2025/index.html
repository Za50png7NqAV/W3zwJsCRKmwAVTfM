RRB ALP Recruitment 2025 ‚Äì Apply Online for Assistant Loco Pilot

Last Update: 10/05/2025 | 08:48 AM

Description: Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released a notification RRB ALP | CEN No.: 01/2025 of RRB Assistant Loco Pilot Recruitment 2025. The application process will be open from 12 April to 19 May 2025. Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including 10th/ITI/Deploma/Graduate. As per the official notification, there are 9970 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date.
