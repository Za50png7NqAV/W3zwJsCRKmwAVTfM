RRC Central Railway Apprentice Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online for 2,418 Vacancies

Last Update: 14/08/2025 | 12:54 PM

What's Update :

Description: Railway Recruitment Cell, Central Railway has released a Official Notification Advt. No.: RRC/CR/AA/2025 of RRC CR Apprentice. The application process will be open from 12 Aug to 11 Sep 2025 . Candidates must meet the required eligibility criteria including . As per the official notification, there are 2418 vacancies available for male and female candidates. Interested candidates are advised to check the important dates and ensure they apply before the last date. For complete information, download the official notification and apply online using the links provided below.

👨‍💻 How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website:

    Go to rrccr.com.

  • Open the Notification:

    Read the RRC CR Apprentice Recruitment 2025 notification carefully.

  • Register Online:

    Click on “Apply Online” and complete the registration by entering basic details.

  • Login to Your Account:

    Use the registration number and password sent to your email/SMS.

  • Fill the Application Form:

    Enter personal details, educational qualification, and trade preference.

  • Upload Documents:

    Passport-size photo (as per size & format mentioned), Signature, 10th mark sheet & ITI certificate, Caste/PwBD certificate (if applicable)

  • Pay the Application Fee:

    Pay online via Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.

  • Preview the Form:

    Check all details carefully before final submission.

  • Submit the Form:

    Click “Submit” and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.
