JNV Class IX Admission Form 2026-27
Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has invited applications for Class IX Lateral Entry Admission 2026-27 against vacant seats through a selection test. The exam will be conducted on 7th February 2026 at designated JNVs of respective districts. Eligible students studying in Class VIII during 2025-26 in Government or Government-recognized schools of the same district, and born between 1st May 2011 to 31st July 2013, can apply online at navodaya.gov.in till 23rd September 2025. Admission will be offered strictly on merit basis and is subject to passing Class VIII along with verification of residence, date of birth, and other required documents.

LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship 2025
The LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship 2025 provides financial support to meritorious students from Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) for pursuing higher education in India. The scheme covers courses after Class 10 and 12, including Medical, Engineering, Graduation, Vocational, Diploma, and ITI programs. Scholarships are of two types: General Scholarship and Special Scholarship for Girls, with amounts ranging from ₹15,000 to ₹40,000 per annum. Selection is merit-based and income-sensitive, and the scholarship is credited directly to the student’s bank account.

HDFC Scholarship 2025-26
HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECSS Scholarship 2025-26 is a financial assistance programme aimed at helping meritorious students from economically weaker sections continue their education without interruption. The scholarship is open for school students (Class 1 to 12), as well as those pursuing diploma, ITI, polytechnic, undergraduate, and postgraduate courses. Under this initiative, eligible students facing financial challenges, family crises, or risk of dropout can receive support of up to INR 75,000. The programme ensures that bright and deserving students get equal opportunities to pursue their academic goals and build a successful future.
