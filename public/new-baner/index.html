SCI Court Master Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Supreme Court of India has invited online applications for 30 posts of Court Master (Shorthand) in 2025. Eligible candidates must be between 30 to 45 years of age and hold a graduate degree with shorthand speed of 120 w.p.m. and computer typing speed of 40 w.p.m., along with at least 5 years of experience in a relevant government or public sector post. The selection process includes a Shorthand Test, Written Test, Typing Speed Test, and Interview. Candidates with a law degree will receive additional weightage. Applications can be submitted online through the official website of the Supreme Court at www.sci.gov.in.
