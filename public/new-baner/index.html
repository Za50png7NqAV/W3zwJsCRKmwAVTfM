MP Police ASI & Subedar Stenographer Exam Date 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Police Headquarters has announced 500 vacancies for Subedar (Stenographer) and Assistant Sub Inspector (Clerk) posts under direct and departmental recruitment. Eligible candidates with 12th pass and computer proficiency (CPCT/Hindi Typing) can apply online via MPESB from 03 October to 17 October 2025. The written exam will start on 10 December 2025. Reservation benefits and age relaxation are applicable only for MP domicile candidates.

MP Police Subedar & Sub Inspector Exam Date 2025
The MP Police Subedar & Sub Inspector Recruitment 2025 offers over 500 vacancies for male and female candidates under the Madhya Pradesh Police Department. The online application starts on 27 October 2025 and ends on 10 November 2025. Applicants must be graduates, meet the prescribed age and physical standards, and pay an online fee of ₹500 (UR) or ₹250 (reserved). The written exam begins on 9 January 2026, followed by PET and document verification.

MPESB Paramedical Result 2025 Declared
MP Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has announced the Paramedical Recruitment 2025 for a total of 464 posts. The recruitment includes vacancies for Physiotherapist, Counselor, Pharmacist Grade-II, and Ophthalmic Assistant. Eligible candidates can apply online from 28 July 2025 to 11 August 2025 through the official portal esb.mp.gov.in. The selection will be based on an online examination scheduled to begin from 27 September 2025. Candidates are advised to check the post-wise qualifications, age limit, and other details before applying.
