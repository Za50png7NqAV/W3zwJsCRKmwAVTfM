Apaar Id / ABC ID card Online Apply & Download
The Apaar ID (also called ABC ID) is a unique 12-digit identification number issued by the Ministry of Education, Government of India under the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. It is designed for school and college students across India to digitally store their academic records, credits, and certificates. The card facilitates seamless credit transfer and academic mobility through platforms like DigiLocker and the Academic Bank of Credits (ABC Portal). Students can apply online using their Aadhaar and basic educational details. The card is available to all students from Class 1 onward and can be downloaded from DigiLocker or the ABC portal after registration.

BSF Constable Sports Quota Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Border Security Force (BSF) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Constable (General Duty) under Sports Quota 2025. A total of 241 vacancies are available for meritorious male and female sportspersons who have participated at national or international levels. Eligible candidates can apply before the last date, 20 August 2025. This is a golden opportunity for young sportspersons to serve in BSF with pride.

BSF Constable Tradesman Vacancy 2025
The Border Security Force (BSF) has released a notification for recruitment to various posts. This opportunity is open to both male and female candidates. The posts include Constable, Head Constable, ASI (Steno), and other technical and non-technical categories. Educational qualifications vary by post, such as 10th pass, 12th pass, or a diploma in the relevant trade. Candidates must meet the prescribed physical standards and go through the selection process, which includes a written examination, physical test, document verification, and medical examination.
