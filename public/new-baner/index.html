BTSC Staff Nurse Exam Dates Announced
The Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) has officially announced the Staff Nurse Computer-Based Test (CBT) schedule under Advt. No. 23/2025. The exam will be conducted on 30 July, 31 July, 1 August, and 3 August 2025 across multiple shifts. Candidates who have applied for over 11,000 Staff Nurse posts should prepare accordingly. The admit card will be released soon on the official website btsc.bihar.gov.in.
