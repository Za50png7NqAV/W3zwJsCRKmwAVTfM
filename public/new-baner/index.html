Join Indian Army Men NCC 124th Course Oct 2026
Indian Army has released the notification for NCC Special Entry Scheme 124th Course (Oct 2026) for Men under Short Service Commission (Non-Technical). A total of 70 vacancies (63 General + 07 Wards of Battle Casualties) have been announced. Unmarried male graduates with minimum ‘B’ Grade in NCC ‘C’ Certificate are eligible to apply. The selection process includes shortlisting based on NCC grading and graduation marks, followed by SSB interview, medical examination, and merit list. Selected candidates will undergo 49 weeks of training at Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai. Online applications will be accepted from 12/02/2026 to 16/03/2026.

SBI Circle Based Officer CBO Vacancy 2026
SBI CBO Recruitment 2026 has been announced by the State Bank of India for 2050 Circle Based Officer (CBO) posts. The online application process will be open from 29 January 2026 to 18 February 2026. Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline and have at least 2 years of experience as an Officer in a Scheduled Commercial Bank or Regional Rural Bank (RRB). The selection process includes an online examination, screening of experience, interview, and local language proficiency test. Selected candidates will be posted in the same circle for which they apply, making this recruitment a great opportunity for experienced banking professionals to join SBI as officers.

Ruk Jana Nahi / Aa Laut Chale (ALC) Dec- 2025 Result
The Madhya Pradesh State Open School (MPSOS) has released the schedule for the Aa Laut Chale (ALC) Dec-2025 Examination. The application process will run from 19 August 2025 to 31 October 2025, giving students enough time to register online. Along with the application, options for Paid, Unpaid, and Duplicate receipts will also be available starting 19 August 2025. This exam provides an important opportunity for students to continue their studies and complete their board education without losing a year.
