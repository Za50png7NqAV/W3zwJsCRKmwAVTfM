Delhi DDA Recruitment 2025- Exam City
The Delhi Development Authority (DDA) has released its Recruitment Notification 2025 for a total of 1732 vacancies across various posts including Deputy Director, Assistant Director, Junior Engineer, Stenographer, Patwari, Mali, and Multi Tasking Staff (MTS). The online application process is scheduled from 06 October 2025 to 05 November 2025, and the Computer Based Test (CBT) is expected in December 2025 – January 2026. Qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10th/12th pass to Graduate/Degree holders in relevant fields.

SSC GD Constable Vacancy 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

RRB Paramedical Staff Application Status 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.
