IB Security Assistant/Executive Admit Card 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has released a notification for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive posts for the year 2025. A total of 4987 vacancies are available across various states and Union Territories. Candidates must have passed 10th standard, possess knowledge of the local language/dialect, and hold a domicile certificate of the region applied for. This is a central government Group 'C' non-gazetted, non-ministerial post, offering job security and growth in the intelligence sector. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal before the last date.

CSBC Bihar Police Recruitment 2025
The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC), Bihar has released Advertisement No. 03/2025 for the recruitment of 4128 vacancies including Prohibition Constable, Jail Warder, and Mobile Squad Constable. Interested applicants must check the official notification for complete details on eligibility, age limit, application fees, and physical standards before applying.
