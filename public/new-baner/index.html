RRB Paramedical Various Posts Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.

Azim Premji Foundation Scholarship 2025 – Online Application Begins
The Azim Premji Scholarship 2025 is a flagship initiative of the Azim Premji Foundation aimed at supporting girl students from disadvantaged backgrounds to continue their higher education. Under this program, eligible students receive ₹30,000 per year for the entire duration of their first undergraduate degree or diploma course (2–5 years) at recognized government or private colleges across India. To qualify, the applicant must have passed Class 10 and 12 as a regular student from a government school or college and secured admission in the first year of a UG or diploma course for the 2025–26 academic session. The scholarship is open to students from select states and union territories including Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Puducherry, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Telangana, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, and Uttarakhand. The application process for the Scholarship Cohort 2025 will begin on 10 September 2025, and candidates must submit documents like a passport-size photograph, Aadhaar card, and signature image. Importantly, there is no application fee, and students are advised to beware of fraud or impostors.

Indian Coast Guard Assistant Commandant AC Exam City & Exam Date 2025
The Indian Coast Guard has released the notification for Assistant Commandant Recruitment 2025. A total of 170 vacancies are available under two main branches: General Duty (GD) with 140 posts and Technical (Engineering/Electrical) with 30 posts. Eligible male candidates with a graduate degree and Physics & Maths at 10+2 level can apply for GD, while candidates with an engineering degree in relevant disciplines can apply for the Technical branch. The application process is online through the official website joinindiancoastguard.cdac.in. This is a prestigious Group ‘A’ Gazetted Officer post under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India.
