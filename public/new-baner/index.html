Chandigarh JBT Teacher Admit Card 2025 Download
Samagra Shiksha, Chandigarh has released a notification for the recruitment of 218 Junior Basic Teacher (JBT) posts for Primary Classes (I to V). Candidates from across India are eligible to apply. The minimum qualification includes a Graduation degree from a recognized university, a 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) recognized by NCTE, and passing the CTET (Paper-I). The salary for the post is fixed at ₹45,260 per month. The application process is online and will begin on 07 August 2025 and end on 28 August 2025 (5:00 PM), while the last date to pay the fee is 30 August 2025 (2:00 PM). The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC/EWS, ₹500 for SC, and exempted for PWD candidates. The selection will be done through a written objective type test of 150 marks, with no interview. Category-wise distribution includes 111 posts for General, 44 for OBC, 41 for SC, and 22 for EWS candidates. Interested applicants must visit the official website ssachd.nic.in to apply and read the full notification.

BSSC Sports Trainer Vacancy 2025
Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has announced 379 vacancies for Sports Instructor/Trainer under Advt. No. 08/2025. Eligible candidates with graduation and diploma/PG diploma in sports coaching along with sports achievements can apply. The selection will be based on a written exam (150 marks) and interview (50 marks). Online applications are open from 09/10/2025 to 11/11/2025 at bssc.bihar.gov.in.

Bihar Vidhan Parishad Vacancy 2025
Bihar Vidhan Parishad Secretariat has released the official notification for 24 vacancies including 09 Driver and 15 Office Attendant posts. Delhi Police Head Constable Ministerial Vacancy 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for Delhi Police Head Constable (Ministerial) Recruitment 2025. A total of 509 vacancies (341 male & 168 female) are available. Candidates with 10+2 qualification can apply online from 29/09/2025 to 20/10/2025. The selection process includes Computer Based Examination, Physical Endurance & Measurement Test (PE&MT), Typing Test, and Computer Formatting Test.
