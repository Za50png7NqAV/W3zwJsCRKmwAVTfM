Canara Bank Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Canara Bank invites online applications for 3500 Graduate Apprentice positions under the Apprentices Act, 1961 for FY 2025-26. Graduates in any discipline from a recognized university are eligible. Candidates must register on the NATS apprenticeship portal before applying on the Canara Bank website. Selection is based on shortlisting, local language test (if applicable), and document verification. Application dates are 23rd September to 12th October 2025, and fees are ₹500 for general/OBC/EWS while SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempted.

Delhi Police Constable Vacancy 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the Delhi Police Constable (Executive) Recruitment 2025 notification for 7565 vacancies. Both male and female candidates can apply online from 22 September to 21 October 2025. The recruitment process includes a Computer-Based Examination, followed by Physical Endurance & Measurement Tests (PE&MT). Eligible candidates must have passed 10+2, and male applicants must hold a valid LMV driving license. The pay scale is Level-3 (₹21,700 – ₹69,100). Reserved categories and women candidates are exempted from the application fee.
