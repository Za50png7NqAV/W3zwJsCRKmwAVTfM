Indian Coast Guard Assistant Commandant AC Vacancy 2025
The Indian Coast Guard has released the notification for Assistant Commandant Recruitment 2025. A total of 170 vacancies are available under two main branches: General Duty (GD) with 140 posts and Technical (Engineering/Electrical) with 30 posts. Eligible male candidates with a graduate degree and Physics & Maths at 10+2 level can apply for GD, while candidates with an engineering degree in relevant disciplines can apply for the Technical branch. The application process is online through the official website joinindiancoastguard.cdac.in. This is a prestigious Group ‘A’ Gazetted Officer post under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India.

RPSC Research Assistant Admit Card 2025
The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the Research Assistant Admit Card 2025 on its official website. Candidates who have applied for the examination can now download their admit card by visiting rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. The admit card contains important details such as exam date, time, center, and instructions. Candidates must carry a valid ID proof along with the admit card to the exam center.

RRB NTPC 10+2 Under Graduate Level Exam Date/ Application Status 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has officially released the exam dates for the NTPC 10+2 (Under Graduate) level posts. The Computer Based Test (CBT 1) will be conducted from 7 August to 8 September 2025. Candidates who have applied for the RRB NTPC UG posts can check the detailed exam schedule on the official RRB websites. This exam is conducted for 12th-pass candidates applying for various undergraduate-level posts in Indian Railways.
