RPSC Exam Calendar 2026
RPSC Exam Calendar 2026 has been officially released by the Rajasthan Public Service Commission, Ajmer. The calendar provides the tentative schedule of various recruitment examinations to be conducted in 2026, including Deputy Commandant, Lecturer, Sub Inspector, Veterinary Officer, Assistant Engineer, Senior Teacher, Junior Legal Officer, Statistical Officer and other posts. The exams will be held between January 2026 and December 2026, with some examinations conducted in CBRT (Computer Based Recruitment Test) mode. Detailed exam programmes, admit cards and shift information will be issued separately by RPSC as per schedule.

IOCL Northern Region Apprentice Online Form 2025
IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2025–26 for the Northern Region offers 501 state-wise apprentice vacancies for Trade, Technician, and Graduate categories. The online application process will be open from 27 December 2025 to 12 January 2026 via the NAPS/NATS portals. Eligible candidates with 12th, ITI, Diploma, or Graduation qualifications can apply for one discipline only. Selection will be merit-based, and shortlisted candidates will undergo document verification and medical examination. The apprenticeship tenure will be 12 months under Indian Oil Corporation Limited – Marketing Division, Northern Region.

NTA UGC NET December 2025 - Admit Card
The UGC NET December 2025 Notification has been released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for determining the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professor and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in Indian universities and colleges. Candidates with a Master’s degree (55% marks for General and 50% for reserved categories) can apply online from 07 October to 07 November 2025 at ugcnet.nta.nic.in. Samagra Portal 2025
The MP Samagra ID Portal (samagra.gov.in) is an official initiative by the Department of Social Justice and Disabled Welfare, Government of Madhya Pradesh. It helps citizens register their families and members under a single database to avail benefits of various state welfare schemes. Through this portal, users can easily apply for a new Samagra ID, print Family or Member ID cards, and update personal details such as name, address, or Aadhaar information online. This digital system ensures transparency, quick access, and smooth delivery of government services to every eligible household in Madhya Pradesh.
