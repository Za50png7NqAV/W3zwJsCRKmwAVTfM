IB Security Assistant/Executive Vacancy 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has released a notification for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive posts for the year 2025. A total of 4987 vacancies are available across various states and Union Territories. Candidates must have passed 10th standard, possess knowledge of the local language/dialect, and hold a domicile certificate of the region applied for. The post offers a Level-3 pay scale (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) along with special allowances. This is a central government Group 'C' non-gazetted, non-ministerial post, offering job security and growth in the intelligence sector. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal before the last date.

RRB Paramedical Various Posts Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.

RSSB Librarian Gr-III Exam City / Admit Card 2025
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has announced important details for the Librarian Grade‑III Exam 2025. The exam city intimation slip was released on July 21, 2025, allowing candidates to check their allotted exam city in advance. The admit card will be available for download starting from July 24, 2025, on the official recruitment portal recruitment.rajasthan.gov.in. The written examination is scheduled to be held on July 27, 2025, in two shifts — the first from 10:00 AM to 12:00 Noon and the second from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Candidates are advised to download their admit cards on time and carefully review the details such as exam center, shift timing, and guidelines to avoid any last-minute issues.
