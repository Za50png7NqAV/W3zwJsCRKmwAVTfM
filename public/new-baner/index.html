MPESB Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025 Online Form
Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the notification for the Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025. Jharkhand SSC ANM Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Female Health Workers (A.N.M.) under the Regular and Backlog vacancies for the year 2025. A total of 3,181 posts are available, including 3,020 regular and 161 backlog vacancies. Only female candidates who are domicile of Jharkhand and have completed 10th (Matric) with at least 45% marks, along with 18 months A.N.M. training and registration in Jharkhand State Nursing Council, are eligible to apply. The application process will be conducted online through the official JSSC website. Interested and eligible candidates must read the official notification thoroughly and apply within the specified dates.

MPESB Candidate Profiling / Profile Panjiyan Form Online
To apply for any government recruitment under the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB), all candidates must complete the Candidate Profile Registration process. This is a one-time mandatory step where applicants register using Aadhaar eKYC, upload personal and academic details, and receive a unique Profile ID. This ID is required for all future MPESB applications. The process is simple, online, and essential for anyone seeking government jobs in Madhya Pradesh.

Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has announced 28 vacancies for various Grade A and B Officer posts under the 2025 recruitment drive.
