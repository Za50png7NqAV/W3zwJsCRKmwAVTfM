Delhi High Court Higher Judicial Service HJS Vacancy 2026
The High Court of Delhi has released the Delhi Higher Judicial Service (DHJS) Recruitment 2026 Notification for 27 vacancies through direct recruitment. Eligible candidates with a Law degree (LLB) and the required judicial or advocacy experience can apply online from 01 July to 15 July 2026. The selection process includes a Preliminary Examination, Mains Examination, and Viva-Voce. Interested candidates should check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application fee, important dates, salary, and other details before submitting the online application form.

UP Anganwadi Worker Bharti 2026
UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026 is a recruitment drive conducted by the Child Development Services & Nutrition Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh, to fill Anganwadi Worker (कार्यकर्त्री) vacancies across various districts. RSSB CET Graduate Level Vacancy 2026
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the Common Eligibility Test (CET) Graduate Level 2026 notification for recruitment to various graduate-level posts in Rajasthan Government departments. Eligible candidates holding a Bachelor's Degree can submit their online applications from 04 July 2026 to 02 August 2026 through the SSO Portal. The CET Graduate Level examination is scheduled to be conducted from 01 December to 03 December 2026. Candidates who qualify the CET will become eligible to apply for various graduate-level recruitments such as Patwari, Village Development Officer (VDO), Junior Accountant, Platoon Commander, Supervisor, Deputy Jailor, Hostel Superintendent Grade-II, and other notified posts as per the respective recruitment rules.
