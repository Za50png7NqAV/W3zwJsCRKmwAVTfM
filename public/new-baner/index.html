SSC GD Constable Vacancy 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

UP Police SI Confidential, ASI Clerk and Accounts Vacancy 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has issued the official notification for Police Sub Inspector (Confidential) and Police Assistant Sub Inspector (Clerk and Accounts) posts for the year 2025. Released under Advertisement No. PRPB-B (Cl.)/2025, the recruitment includes a total of 537 vacancies. Online applications will be accepted from 20/12/2025 to 19/01/2026. Selection of candidates will be based on a written examination, followed by skill test wherever applicable, document verification and medical examination. Physical Standard Test (PST) will be conducted, and no Physical Efficiency Test (PET) is included.

UPPSC Medical Officer, Veterinary & Various Posts Online Form 2025
