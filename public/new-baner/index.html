EMRS Teaching & Non-Teaching Vacancy 2025
The National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) has announced the EMRS Recruitment 2025 (ESSE-2025) notification for a total of 7267 vacancies across teaching and non-teaching posts. The vacancies include Principal, PGT, TGT, Female Staff Nurse, Hostel Warden, Accountant, Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Lab Attendant. The age limit is set between 30 to 50 years, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories. This recruitment provides a major opportunity for aspirants seeking teaching and non-teaching jobs in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) across India.

BSSC Stenographer Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification Advt. No. 07/2025 for the recruitment of 432 Stenographer and Stenotypist Grade-III posts in various departments and district offices. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with stenography (Hindi/English), typing, and basic computer knowledge. The selected candidates will get a salary in Pay Level-4 (₹25,500 – ₹81,100/-). The age limit is 18 to 37 years for UR male, 40 years for UR female, OBC and EBC candidates, and 42 years for SC/ST candidates, with 10 years of additional relaxation for PwD candidates. The application fee is ₹100/- to be paid online. The online registration will be open from 25th September 2025 to 5th November 2025, and the last date for fee payment is 3rd November 2025. Interested candidates can apply online only through the official website bssc.bihar.gov.in
