UPSC EPFO Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the Special Advertisement No. 52/2025 for direct recruitment to a total of 230 vacancies in the Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) under the Ministry of Labour and Employment. These include 156 posts of Enforcement Officer/Accounts Officer (EO/AO) and 74 posts of Assistant Provident Fund Commissioner (APFC). Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university. For the APFC post, qualifications such as a degree in Law, MBA, CA, or equivalent are desirable. The online application process will begin on July 29, 2025, and close on August 18, 2025. Selection will be based on a written examination, and postings will be across India. Interested candidates can apply online at https://upsconline.nic.in.

SBI Probationary Officers PO Mains Admit Card 2025 Out
The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the notification for the recruitment of 541 Probationary Officer (PO) posts, including 500 regular posts and 41 backlog posts. The application process will be conducted online through the official SBI website at sbi.co.in. This is a golden opportunity for graduates seeking a prestigious career in banking with SBI.

RPSC 1st Grade Teacher Agriculture Vacancy 2025
The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has announced the RPSC 1st Grade Teacher (Agriculture) Recruitment 2025 for a total of 500 posts of School Lecturer in the Secondary Education Department. Candidates must have a 4-year Bachelor’s degree in Agriculture or Horticulture, a Postgraduate degree in the relevant subject, and a B.Ed. from an NCTE-recognized institution, along with knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture. The online application process will be open from 4th September to 3rd October 2025, with the written examination expected between 31st May and 16th June 2026. The selection process will be based on a written test, document verification, and medical examination, and the post carries a salary under Pay Matrix Level L-12 with Grade Pay ₹4800.
