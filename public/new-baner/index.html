Oil India Limited Vacancy 2025
Oil India Limited, a Maharatna PSU, has released its Recruitment Notification 2025 for a total of 102 vacancies in Grade A, B, and C posts. The vacancies include positions such as Superintending Engineer, Senior Officers in various disciplines (Chemical, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, IT, Petroleum, HR, Legal, Finance, Public Affairs, Geology, Geophysics, Electronics & Communication, Fire & Safety, HSE, Security, Company Secretary), along with Confidential Secretary and Hindi Officer. Both male and female candidates are eligible to apply. The application process is completely online, starting from 26 August 2025 and closing on 26 September 2025. Candidates will be selected through Computer Based Test (CBT), Personal Interview, and Skill/Proficiency Test as per the post. The recruitment offers attractive pay scales ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹2,20,000 plus allowances, making it a great opportunity for engineering and non-engineering graduates to build a career with Oil India Limited.

RRC SR Apprentices Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Railway Recruitment Cell, Southern Railway (RRC SR) has released the official notification for Apprentice Recruitment 2025, offering a total of 3518 apprenticeship slots across various workshops and divisions including Perambur, Golden Rock, Podanur, Chennai, Salem, Madurai, Palakkad, and Trivandrum. Eligible candidates who have passed 10th (50% marks), 12th with Science (for MLT trades), or ITI in relevant trades can apply online from 25 August 2025 to 25 September 2025. The selection will be based on a merit list prepared from academic and ITI marks. Apprentices will receive a stipend of ₹6,000–₹7,000 per month during training. This engagement is purely for apprenticeship training under the Apprentices Act, 1961, and does not guarantee direct employment, though preference will be given in future railway recruitments to candidates who complete the training successfully.

Federal Bank Associate Officer Vacancy 2025
The Federal Bank has invited applications for the post of Associate Officer (Sales) under its 2025 recruitment drive. The online application window is open from 25/08/2025 to 03/09/2025. Candidates must be graduates with at least 50% aggregate marks in Class X, Class XII/Diploma, and Graduation, and should also hold a valid two- or four-wheeler driving license. The upper age limit is 27 years (born on or after 01/08/1998), and applicants must be domiciled in Gujarat, Telangana, West Bengal, or Delhi NCR. An application fee of ₹350 (non-refundable) is payable online. The selection process consists of a center-based online aptitude test followed by a personal interview, with selected candidates placed on a one-year probation. The role offers a salary package between ₹4.59 and ₹6.19 LPA, along with additional benefits such as medical coverage, NPS, and career progression opportunities.
