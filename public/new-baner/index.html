RPSC School Lecturer Vacancy 2025 Apply Online
The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released a recruitment notification for 3225 vacancies for the post of School Lecturer (Grade-I) in various subjects including Hindi, English, Political Science, Geography, History, Physics, Chemistry, Commerce, Drawing, Physical Education, Music, and Sports Coaching categories like Athletics, Basketball, etc. Candidates must possess a Postgraduate degree in the relevant subject along with B.Ed. or an equivalent teaching qualification. The application process will be conducted online through the official RPSC portal from 14 August 2025 to 12 September 2025. Applicants from all Indian states are eligible to apply; however, only Rajasthan state candidates will receive category-based reservation and fee relaxation. Candidates from other states will be considered under the General category.

Rajasthan Sub Inspector Vacancy 2025 Apply Online
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the official notification for Rajasthan Police SI and Platoon Commander Recruitment 2025-26 under advertisement number 05/Exam/SI-PC/RPSC/EP-I/2025-26. A total of 1015 vacancies have been announced for various posts including Sub Inspector (AP, IB, Sahariya, Scheduled Area) and Platoon Commander (RAC). Candidates holding a graduate degree and having knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 10 August 2025 and the last date to apply is 08 September 2025. Applicants from all states of India can apply for general posts, while certain reserved posts are limited to Rajasthan domicile candidates only.
