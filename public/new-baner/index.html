MPTAAS Profile Registration 2026 - Apply Online for Scholarship form
MPTAAS Profile Registration 2026 is a mandatory process for SC, ST, and OBC students in Madhya Pradesh to apply for Post Matric Scholarships. The profile is created on the Tribal Affairs portal, enabling students to apply for schemes, track status, and receive benefits via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer). Important documents like Aadhaar, Samagra ID, caste certificate, domicile proof, and bank details are required. Once registered, students can log in and apply for scholarships online.
