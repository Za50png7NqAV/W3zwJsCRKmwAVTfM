CUET (UG) 2026 Online Form
CUET (UG) 2026 is a national-level admission test conducted by the National Testing Agency for undergraduate admissions in Central and other participating universities for the academic session 2026–27. Candidates who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 can apply online. The exam is held in CBT mode, allows up to five subject choices, and uses a standardized marking scheme. Admissions are granted by universities based on CUET scores, merit lists, and program-wise eligibility criteria.

UP Police Constable Vacancy 2025
UP Police has released the Constable Recruitment 2025 notification for 32,679 vacancies across multiple units such as Civil Police, PAC, Special Security Force, Female Battalion, Mounted Police and Jail Warder. Applicants must have passed Intermediate (12th) from a recognized board. The prescribed age limit is 18 to 22 years for male candidates and 18 to 25 years for female candidates, with category-wise relaxation as per government norms. The recruitment process consists of a written test followed by PST, PET, document verification and medical examination. The online application window will be open from 31/12/2025 to 30/01/2026, making it one of the largest police recruitments in Uttar Pradesh.

RRB Group D / Level 1 Vacancy 2026
Railway Recruitment Boards have released the indicative notice for Level-1 / Group D Recruitment under CEN 09/2025 to fill around 22000 vacancies across various RRB regions. These posts fall under Level-1 of the 7th Pay Commission with a starting pay of ₹18000 per month along with allowances. The online application process will begin on 21 January 2026 and will remain open till 20 February 2026 on the official RRB application portal. Candidates between 18 and 33 years of age as on 01 January 2026 who meet the prescribed educational qualifications will be eligible to apply. Complete details regarding post-wise eligibility selection process syllabus and medical standards will be available in the detailed notification published by the respective RRBs.

Aadhaar Supervisor Operator Online Form 2026
Aadhaar Supervisor / Operator – District Recruitment 2025–26 has been released for multiple states across India. Eligible candidates aged 18 years or above with 12th pass, ITI, or Polytechnic qualifications and a valid Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor certificate authorized by UIDAI can apply online. SSC GD Constable Vacancy 2026 - Exam Date
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.
