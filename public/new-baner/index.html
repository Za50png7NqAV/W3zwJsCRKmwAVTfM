PowerGrid PGCIL Field Engineer & Supervisor Admit Card 2025
The Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (PGCIL) has invited applications for 1543 vacancies of Field Engineer and Field Supervisor under the 2025 recruitment drive. Candidates with a B.E./B.Tech/B.Sc in Engineering for Field Engineer posts or a Diploma in relevant discipline for Field Supervisor posts can apply online between 27 August to 17 September 2025. NTA AISSEE 2026 Registration Form
The All India Sainik School Entrance Examination (AISSEE) 2026 is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to Class 6 and Class 9 in Sainik Schools across India for the academic year 2026-27. The online application process will be open from 10 October 2025 to 30 October 2025. Candidates must carefully check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application fee, and exam pattern before applying. The entrance exam will be conducted in January 2026 in offline (OMR-based) mode. The application fee is ₹850 for General/OBC/Defence candidates and ₹700 for SC/ST candidates. Eligible students must fulfill nationality, age, and educational qualification requirements. For detailed information, official notifications, and the online application link, visit the NTA website at https://exams.nta.ac.in/sainik-school-society/

JNV Class IX Admission Form 2026-27
Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has invited applications for Class IX Lateral Entry Admission 2026-27 against vacant seats through a selection test. The exam will be conducted on 7th February 2026 at designated JNVs of respective districts. Eligible students studying in Class VIII during 2025-26 in Government or Government-recognized schools of the same district, and born between 1st May 2011 to 31st July 2013, can apply online at navodaya.gov.in till 23rd September 2025. Admission will be offered strictly on merit basis and is subject to passing Class VIII along with verification of residence, date of birth, and other required documents.

SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025
SBI Platinum Jubilee Asha Scholarship 2025 is a national-level scholarship launched by the SBI Foundation under its CSR initiative to support meritorious students from economically weaker families. Under this scheme, students from Class 9 to Postgraduate level can get financial aid up to ₹20 lakh per year. The aim is to help bright students continue their education without financial burden. Eligible students can apply online through the official website sbiashascholarship.co.in from 19 September 2025 to 15 November 2025. The scheme covers school, college, medical, and professional courses with 50% reservation for girl students.

BSF Sports Quota Vacancy 2025
The Border Security Force (BSF) under the Ministry of Home Affairs has released the Constable (GD) Sports Quota Recruitment 2025 notification for 391 vacancies. Eligible Indian male and female sportspersons who have participated or won medals in recognized National or International competitions (2023–2025) can apply online from 16 October 2025 to 4 November 2025 at rectt.bsf.gov.in. Candidates must have passed Matriculation (10th) and meet prescribed age, physical, and medical standards. The pay scale is Level-3 (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) as per the 7th CPC with admissible allowances. Application fee is ₹159 for UR/OBC male candidates, while SC/ST and female applicants are exempted. Selection will be based on sports merit, documentation, PST, and medical examination.
