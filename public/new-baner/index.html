PNB Apprentice Vacancy 2026
Punjab National Bank has released the PNB Apprentice Recruitment 2025–26 notification for 5138 apprentice posts across India. Graduate candidates can apply online through the BFSI SSC portal from 08 February to 24 February 2026. Selection will be based on an online exam, document verification, local language test, and medical fitness. The apprenticeship duration is 1 year, and selected candidates will receive a monthly stipend based on branch location.

RRB NTPC Inter Level Vacancy 2025 - Application Status
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the notification for RRB NTPC Inter Level Recruitment 2025 (CEN 07/2025) with 3050 vacancies under the 10+2 (Undergraduate) category. Eligible candidates who have passed Intermediate (Class 12th) can apply online for posts like Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Train Clerk, and Commercial cum Ticket Clerk. The application process will start from 28 October 2025 and continue till 27 November 2025. The selection process includes CBT-1, CBT-2, Typing Skill Test (for Clerk/Typist posts), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The pay scale ranges from ₹19,900 to ₹21,700 (Level-2 & Level-3 as per 7th CPC) with additional allowances. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for complete eligibility, age limit, application fees, and exam details.

RRB Group D / Level 1 Vacancy 2026
Railway Recruitment Boards have released the indicative notice for Level-1 / Group D Recruitment under CEN 09/2025 to fill around 21997 vacancies across various RRB regions. These posts fall under Level-1 of the 7th Pay Commission with a starting pay of ₹18000 per month along with allowances. The online application process will begin on 21 January 2026 and will remain open till 20 February 2026 on the official RRB application portal. Candidates between 18 and 33 years of age as on 01 January 2026 who meet the prescribed educational qualifications will be eligible to apply. Complete details regarding post-wise eligibility selection process syllabus and medical standards will be available in the detailed notification published by the respective RRBs.
