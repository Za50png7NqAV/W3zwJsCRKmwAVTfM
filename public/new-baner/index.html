UP Anganwadi Worker Bharti 2026
UP Anganwadi Bharti 2026 is a recruitment drive conducted by the Child Development Services & Nutrition Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh, to fill Anganwadi Worker (कार्यकर्त्री) vacancies across various districts. Eligible female candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) and are permanent residents of the concerned Village/Ward/Nyaya Panchayat can apply online. Candidates should check the district-wise notification for vacancy details, eligibility, important dates, and the application process before submitting their online application.
