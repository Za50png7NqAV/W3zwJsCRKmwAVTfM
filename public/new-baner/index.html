Shramodaya Admission Test 2026–27
Shramodaya Admission Test 2026–27 is conducted for admission to Shramodaya Awasiya Vidyalaya in Madhya Pradesh for Class 6, 7, 8 and 9. The admission is meant for children of registered construction workers under the MP Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. The online application process will be available from 24/12/2025 to 20/01/2026. Selection will be based on an entrance examination, followed by merit-based admission. The exam includes subjects like Hindi, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science and General Aptitude. Eligible students who qualify the test will get admission to Shramodaya residential schools as per district-wise seat availability.

IOCL Northern Region Apprentice Online Form 2025
IOCL Apprentice Recruitment 2025–26 for the Northern Region offers 501 state-wise apprentice vacancies for Trade, Technician, and Graduate categories. The online application process will be open from 27 December 2025 to 12 January 2026 via the NAPS/NATS portals. Eligible candidates with 12th, ITI, Diploma, or Graduation qualifications can apply for one discipline only. Selection will be merit-based, and shortlisted candidates will undergo document verification and medical examination. The apprenticeship tenure will be 12 months under Indian Oil Corporation Limited – Marketing Division, Northern Region.

NTA NCHM JEE 2026 Admission Test
NCHM JEE 2026 is conducted by the National Testing Agency for admission to the B.Sc. Hospitality & Hotel Administration program. Candidates who have passed or are appearing in 10+2 with English can apply. Selection is based on CBT score, rank, counselling and availability of seats in affiliated hotel management institutes.
