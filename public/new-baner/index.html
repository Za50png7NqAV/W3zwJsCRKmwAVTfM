FSSAI Food Licence Registration Online Form 2026
The FSSAI Food License is mandatory for all food-related businesses in India to ensure food safety and compliance with the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. It is issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. There are three types of licenses – Basic, State, and Central – depending on the business size and turnover. Applicants must submit documents like identity proof, address proof, and business registration while applying online through the FSSAI portal. The license is valid for 1 to 5 years, and renewal is required before expiry. Running a food business without a license may lead to heavy penalties or legal action.

MP Gumasta License (Shop & Establishment) Registration 2026
MP Gumasta Licence (Shop & Establishment Registration) is a mandatory registration for businesses in Madhya Pradesh. It provides legal recognition to shops, offices, and commercial establishments. Applicants can apply online through the MP Labour Department Portal by filling out the application form, uploading required documents, and paying the prescribed fee. The registration process is simple and usually completed within 3–7 days. Required documents include Aadhaar card, PAN card, address proof, photo, mobile number, and email ID. This licence is essential for running a legal business and is often required for GST registration, opening a business bank account, and availing government benefits.

Union Bank of India Apprentice Vacancy 2026
