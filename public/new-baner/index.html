Federal Bank Associate Officer Vacancy 2025
The Federal Bank has invited applications for the post of Associate Officer (Sales) under its 2025 recruitment drive. The online application window is open from 25/08/2025 to 03/09/2025. Candidates must be graduates with at least 50% aggregate marks in Class X, Class XII/Diploma, and Graduation, and should also hold a valid two- or four-wheeler driving license. The upper age limit is 27 years (born on or after 01/08/1998), and applicants must be domiciled in Gujarat, Telangana, West Bengal, or Delhi NCR. An application fee of ₹350 (non-refundable) is payable online. The selection process consists of a center-based online aptitude test followed by a personal interview, with selected candidates placed on a one-year probation. The role offers a salary package between ₹4.59 and ₹6.19 LPA, along with additional benefits such as medical coverage, NPS, and career progression opportunities.

DSSSB Delhi High Court Various Post Vacancy 2025
DSSSB has announced 334 vacancies under Advt No. 03/2025 for various posts in the Delhi High Court, including Security Attendant, Court Attendant, and Room Attendant. Bihar BSSC Office Attendant / Attendant (Special) Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Office Attendant / Attendant (Special) posts under advertisement number 06/25. A total of 3727 vacancies have been announced across multiple departments of the Bihar government. Candidates who have passed the 10th (Matric) examination from a recognized board are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 25th August 2025 and remain open until 26th September 2025. The selection will be done through a preliminary objective-type written examination, followed by a main exam for shortlisted candidates. The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the upper age limit varies by category, with applicable relaxations. Reservation benefits will be given as per Bihar government rules, including 35% horizontal reservation for women and 4% for PwD candidates. The recruitment offers a Level-1 pay scale, and candidates are advised to read the full notification before applying online through the official BSSC website: https://bssc.bihar.gov.in.
