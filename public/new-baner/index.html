MP Anganwadi Worker & Helper Vacancy 2026
MP Anganwadi Recruitment 2025 Phase-2 has been released by the Women and Child Development Department, Madhya Pradesh, for a total of 4767 posts, including 1573 Anganwadi Worker (AWW) and 3194 Anganwadi Helper (AWH) vacancies. The recruitment is conducted through the MP Online selection portal on a district-wise basis. Only eligible female candidates who are residents of the concerned village or ward can apply. The minimum qualification required is 12th pass, and the age limit is 18 to 35 years as on 01/01/2025. The online application process will be open from 31/12/2025 to 10/01/2026, with a correction window up to 12/01/2026. Selection will be merit-based, and no written examination will be conducted.

UP Police Constable Vacancy 2025
UP Police has released the Constable Recruitment 2025 notification for 32,679 vacancies across multiple units such as Civil Police, PAC, Special Security Force, Female Battalion, Mounted Police and Jail Warder. Applicants must have passed Intermediate (12th) from a recognized board. The prescribed age limit is 18 to 22 years for male candidates and 18 to 25 years for female candidates, with category-wise relaxation as per government norms. The recruitment process consists of a written test followed by PST, PET, document verification and medical examination. The online application window will be open from 31/12/2025 to 30/01/2026, making it one of the largest police recruitments in Uttar Pradesh.
