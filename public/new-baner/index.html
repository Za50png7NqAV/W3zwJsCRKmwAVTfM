LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship 2025
The LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship 2025 provides financial support to meritorious students from Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) for pursuing higher education in India. The scheme covers courses after Class 10 and 12, including Medical, Engineering, Graduation, Vocational, Diploma, and ITI programs. Scholarships are of two types: General Scholarship and Special Scholarship for Girls, with amounts ranging from ₹15,000 to ₹40,000 per annum. Selection is merit-based and income-sensitive, and the scholarship is credited directly to the student’s bank account.

DRDO Apprentice Vacancy 2025
The DRDO Apprentice Recruitment 2025 has been officially announced by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) for 195 apprentice vacancies at the Research Centre Imarat (RCI), Hyderabad. The recruitment includes Graduate Apprentices (40 posts), Technician/Diploma Apprentices (20 posts), and ITI Trade Apprentices (135 posts) under the Apprenticeship Act, 1961. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online from 27th September 2025 to 28th October 2025 through the official DRDO website. Selection will be based purely on academic merit followed by document verification, and no written exam will be conducted. Candidates with ITI, Diploma, or Graduation in relevant disciplines can apply. This is a great opportunity for freshers to gain valuable hands-on training and experience in India’s premier defence research organisation. Applicants are advised to carefully check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application process, and important dates before applying.

Bihar Police Sub Inspector Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the Bihar Police SI Recruitment 2025 (Advt. No. 05/2025) for 1799 Sub-Inspector (SI) vacancies in the Home Department (Police Section), Government of Bihar. The pay scale for the post is Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400/-). Eligible candidates must hold a graduation degree from a recognized university by 01 August 2025. The age limit is 20–37 years for UR male candidates, 20–40 years for UR female/BC/EBC, and 20–42 years for SC/ST. The selection process includes a preliminary exam, main written exam, physical efficiency test, followed by document verification and medical test. The online application process will be open from 26 September 2025 to 26 October 2025, and the application fee is ₹100/- (payable online). Interested candidates can apply through the official website bpssc.bihar.gov.in.

Canara Bank Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Canara Bank invites online applications for 3500 Graduate Apprentice positions under the Apprentices Act, 1961 for FY 2025-26. Graduates in any discipline from a recognized university are eligible. Candidates must register on the NATS apprenticeship portal before applying on the Canara Bank website. Selection is based on shortlisting, local language test (if applicable), and document verification. Application dates are 23rd September to 12th October 2025, and fees are ₹500 for general/OBC/EWS while SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempted.
