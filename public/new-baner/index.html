MPTRANSCO Various Post Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Madhya Pradesh Power Transmission Company Limited (MPPTCL) has released a recruitment notification for 633 regular posts under the 2025–26 session. This recruitment is open only to the permanent residents of Madhya Pradesh. The available positions include Assistant Engineer (Transmission), Law Officer, Junior Engineer (Transmission and Civil), Line Attendant, and Substation Attendant. The application process is completely online, and the last date to apply is 4th August 2025. The selection will be based on a written examination followed by document verification. Educational qualifications, reservation rules, and other eligibility criteria will follow the Madhya Pradesh government norms. For detailed information and online application, candidates can visit the official website: www.mptransco.in.
