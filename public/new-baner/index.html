National Testing Agency (NTA)
NTA UGC NET December 2025 Exam
The UGC NET December 2025 Notification has been released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for determining the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professor and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in Indian universities and colleges. Candidates with a Master’s degree (55% marks for General and 50% for reserved categories) can apply online from 07 October to 07 November 2025 at ugcnet.nta.nic.in. The exam will be conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode across multiple subjects.

MP Police ASI & Subedar Stenographer Answer Key 2025

IOCL Non-Executive Personnel Vacancy 2025
Indian Oil Corporation Limited has released the IOCL Non-Executive Recruitment 2025 notification for filling multiple non-executive positions in its refinery units across India. The recruitment is open to Diploma and B.Sc qualified candidates for posts including Junior Engineering Assistant, Junior Technical Assistant and Junior Quality Control Analyst. The selection will be carried out through a Computer Based Test followed by a qualifying Skill/Proficiency/Physical Test. Interested candidates can apply online between 20 December 2025 and 09 January 2026. The offered pay scale ranges from ₹25,000 to ₹1,05,000 with standard PSU allowances.

BSF Constable (GD) Sports Quota Vacancy 2025
The BSF Constable (GD) Sports Quota Recruitment 2025 offers an opportunity to join the Border Security Force through 549 Sports Quota vacancies across India. Both male and female candidates who are 10th pass and have recognized sports achievements at national or international level can apply online between 27/12/2025 and 15/01/2026 via rectt.bsf.gov.in. The final selection will be based on sports performance marks, PST, document verification, and medical examination as per the official BSF recruitment process.
