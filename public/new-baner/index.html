SEBI Assistant Manager Grade A Vacancy 2025
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has released the SEBI Officer Grade A (Assistant Manager) Recruitment 2025 notification for 110 vacancies across various streams including General, Legal, IT, Research, Official Language, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. The online application process will be open from 30/10/2025 to 28/11/2025. The Phase-I exam is scheduled for 10/01/2026 and Phase-II for 21/02/2026. Candidates must possess a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in relevant disciplines and be below 30 years of age as on 30/09/2025. The selection process consists of Phase-I, Phase-II, and Interview. The pay scale ranges from ₹62,500 to ₹1,26,100 with an approximate gross salary of ₹1.84 lakh per month (without accommodation). Eligible candidates can apply online through the official website www.sebi.gov.in.

MPPSC Assistant Professor (Computer Science) Vacancy 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released the official notification for the Assistant Professor (Computer Science) Exam 2025 under the Higher Education Department. A total of 87 vacancies are available, including reserved seats for SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and PwD categories. NTA AISSEE 2026 Registration Form
The All India Sainik School Entrance Examination (AISSEE) 2026 is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to Class 6 and Class 9 in Sainik Schools across India for the academic year 2026-27. The online application process will be open from 10 October 2025 to 30 October 2025. Candidates must carefully check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application fee, and exam pattern before applying. The entrance exam will be conducted in January 2026 in offline (OMR-based) mode. The application fee is ₹850 for General/OBC/Defence candidates and ₹700 for SC/ST candidates. Eligible students must fulfill nationality, age, and educational qualification requirements. For detailed information, official notifications, and the online application link, visit the NTA website at https://exams.nta.ac.in/sainik-school-society/
