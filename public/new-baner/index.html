SSC GD Constable Admit Card 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

NTA NEET UG Admit Card 2026
NEET (UG) 2026 is a national-level medical entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. The exam is mandatory for admission to undergraduate medical and allied courses including MBBS, BDS, AYUSH (BAMS, BUMS, BSMS, BHMS), BVSc & AH, and B.Sc Nursing (MNS) across India. Candidates must be at least 17 years old by 31 December 2026 and should have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English. There is no upper age limit and no restriction on the number of attempts. NEET (UG) 2026 will be held on 03 May 2026 in offline mode for a duration of 3 hours and will be conducted in 13 languages. Qualifying NEET is compulsory for admission to all participating institutions.

UPTET Online Form 2026
UPTET 2026 (Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test) online application process has started from 27 March 2026, and candidates can apply through the official website until 26 April 2026. The exam will be conducted from 02 to 04 July 2026. Applicants must meet the required educational qualifications before applying, and the application fee is ₹1000 per paper for General/OBC/EWS, ₹500 for SC/ST, and ₹300 for PwD candidates. It is advised to carefully read the official notification and complete the application form before the last date to avoid any issues.
