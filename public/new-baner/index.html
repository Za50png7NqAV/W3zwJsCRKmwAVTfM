Delhi Police Head Constable Exam Date 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for Delhi Police Head Constable (Assistant Wireless Operator/Tele-Printer Operator) Recruitment 2025. A total of 552 vacancies are available, including 370 posts for male candidates and 182 posts for female candidates. The online application process will remain open from 24 September to 15 October 2025. Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Science and Mathematics or hold an NTC in Mechanic-cum-Operator Electronic Communication System. The age limit is 18 to 27 years, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories. The selection process includes a Computer Based Examination, Physical Endurance & Measurement Test (PE&MT), Trade Test, and Computer Proficiency Test. The pay scale is Level-4 (₹25,500 – ₹81,100) as per the 7th CPC.
