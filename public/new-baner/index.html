Bihar Police Sub Inspector Vacancy 2025 - Admit Card
The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the Bihar Police SI Recruitment 2025 (Advt. No. 05/2025) for 1799 Sub-Inspector (SI) vacancies in the Home Department (Police Section), Government of Bihar. The pay scale for the post is Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400/-). Eligible candidates must hold a graduation degree from a recognized university by 01 August 2025. The age limit is 20–37 years for UR male candidates, 20–40 years for UR female/BC/EBC, and 20–42 years for SC/ST. The selection process includes a preliminary exam, main written exam, physical efficiency test, followed by document verification and medical test. The online application process will be open from 26 September 2025 to 26 October 2025, and the application fee is ₹100/- (payable online). Interested candidates can apply through the official website bpssc.bihar.gov.in.

RSSB REET Mains Primary Teacher Level-1 Vacancy 2025- Exam Date
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the Rajasthan Primary Teacher Recruitment 2025 notification for a total of 5636 Level-1 posts under the Primary Education Department and Sanskrit Education Department. RSSB REET Mains Upper Primary Teacher Level-II Vacancy 2025 - Exam Date
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the REET Mains Level-II Upper Primary School Teacher Recruitment 2025 for a total of 2123 posts under the Sanskrit Education Department. Eligible candidates can apply online from 07/11/2025 to 06/12/2025 through the official website rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in. This recruitment includes vacancies for subjects like Sanskrit, Hindi, English, Social Science, and Maths-Science. Applicants must have a Graduation degree with B.Ed/D.El.Ed or 4-Year Integrated B.A./B.Sc. B.Ed as per NCTE norms. The application fee ranges from ₹400 to ₹600, and the exam is expected between 17/01/2026 to 21/01/2026. Interested candidates should check the official notification for eligibility, syllabus, and document requirements before applying.
