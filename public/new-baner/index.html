RPSC Veterinary Officer Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has announced 1100 vacancies for the post of Veterinary Officer under the Animal Husbandry Department. The recruitment is open to candidates holding a Bachelor’s Degree in Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry or its equivalent from a recognized university in India, along with knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture. The application process will be conducted online, starting from 5 August 2025 and closing on 3 September 2025. The vacancies are distributed among various categories, including General (360), SC (198), ST (183), OBC (209), MBC (50), and EWS (100), with a total of 1100 posts available

Haryana HPSC ADO Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has released the notification for the recruitment of 785 Agricultural Development Officer (ADO) posts under the Administrative Cadre (Group-B) in the Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Department, Haryana. Candidates possessing a B.Sc. (Hons) in Agriculture and having studied Hindi or Sanskrit up to Matric or higher are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 5th August 2025 and will remain open until 25th August 2025 (up to 5:00 PM). Applicants must be between 18 to 42 years of age as on 01 July 2025. The selected candidates will be placed in Pay Level-6 of the 7th CPC pay matrix, with a salary range of ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400. All 27 PwBD reserved posts are included in the total of 785 vacancies. Interested candidates can apply through the official HPSC website at hpsc.gov.in. Category-wise vacancy breakup, reservation rules, fee details, and application instructions are available in the official notification.
