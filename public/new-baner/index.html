BSSC Sports Trainer Vacancy 2025
Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has announced 379 vacancies for Sports Instructor/Trainer under Advt. No. 08/2025. Indian Army TGC 143 Online Form 2025
The Indian Army has released the TGC-143 Notification 2025 for Technical Graduate Course (July 2026 Batch). Unmarried male engineering graduates can apply online from 08/10/2025 to 06/11/2025 through the official website www.joinindianarmy.nic.in. A total of 30 vacancies are available under various engineering streams such as Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, and Computer Science. Selected candidates will undergo 12 months of training at the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Dehradun, and will be granted a Permanent Commission as Lieutenant in the Indian Army. The recruitment process includes shortlisting, SSB Interview, Medical Examination, and Merit List preparation.

IPPB Executive Vacancy 2025
India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) has released the Executive Recruitment 2025 notification for 348 vacancies on engagement from existing Gramin Dak Sevaks (GDS) of the Department of Posts. Eligible GDS employees having a Graduate degree in any discipline can apply online from 09/10/2025 to 29/10/2025 at www.ippbonline.com. Selected candidates will receive a monthly salary of ₹30,000, and selection will be based on merit of graduation marks. The recruitment aims to strengthen IPPB’s doorstep banking network across India by engaging trained GDS executives.

Samagra Portal 2025
The MP Samagra ID Portal (samagra.gov.in) is an official initiative by the Department of Social Justice and Disabled Welfare, Government of Madhya Pradesh. It helps citizens register their families and members under a single database to avail benefits of various state welfare schemes. Through this portal, users can easily apply for a new Samagra ID, print Family or Member ID cards, and update personal details such as name, address, or Aadhaar information online. This digital system ensures transparency, quick access, and smooth delivery of government services to every eligible household in Madhya Pradesh.
