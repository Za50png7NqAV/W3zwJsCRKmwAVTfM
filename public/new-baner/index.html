SSC CPO SI 2025 - Exam City Out
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for CPO SI Recruitment 2025, offering 3073 vacancies for Sub-Inspector posts in Delhi Police and CAPFs. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (Paper-I & II), Physical Tests (PST/PET), Medical Examination, and Document Verification.
