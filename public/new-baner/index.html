NTA UGC NET December 2025 - Exam City

Samagra Portal 2025
The MP Samagra ID Portal (samagra.gov.in) is an official initiative by the Department of Social Justice and Disabled Welfare, Government of Madhya Pradesh. It helps citizens register their families and members under a single database to avail benefits of various state welfare schemes. Through this portal, users can easily apply for a new Samagra ID, print Family or Member ID cards, and update personal details such as name, address, or Aadhaar information online. This digital system ensures transparency, quick access, and smooth delivery of government services to every eligible household in Madhya Pradesh.

CTET February 2026 Registration - Reopen
The CTET February 2026 notification has been released, and the online application process is open from 27/11/2025 to 18/12/2025. Candidates can apply for Paper I (Class 1–5) and Paper II (Class 6–8) based on NCTE eligibility norms. The exam will be conducted on 08 February 2026 in offline OMR mode across multiple exam centers. The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC (single paper) and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The CTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, and all important details regarding syllabus, exam pattern, eligibility, and fee structure are provided in the official notification.

Bihar BPSC AEDO Vacancy 2025 - Exam Date
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the official notification for 935 Assistant Education Development Officer (AEDO) vacancies under the Education Department. The online application process will begin on 27 August 2025 and continue till 26 September 2025. Candidates with a graduate degree from a recognized university are eligible to apply. The salary will be as per Level-5 pay scale (₹29,200 + allowances). Applicants must pay an examination fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges through online mode. Interested candidates should visit the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in to apply online and check detailed guidelines.

BPSC ATP Assistant Town Planner Exam Date 2025
The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has announced 35 vacancies for Assistant Town Planner (ATP) under Advertisement No. 89/2025 in the Urban Development & Housing Department. The online application process will be open from 28th August 2025 to 22nd September 2025 on the official BPSC website. Candidates with a Post Graduate degree in Town Planning, Regional/Urban Planning, City Planning, Country/Transport Planning, Housing, or Environmental Planning are eligible to apply. The selection will be based on a written examination and interview, and appointed candidates will receive a salary under Pay Level 09 (₹53,100 – ₹1,67,800) along with allowances.
