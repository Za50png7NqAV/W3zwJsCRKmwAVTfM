Jharkhand Teacher Eligibility Test JHTET 2026
Jharkhand Teacher Eligibility Test (JHTET) 2026 is a state-level eligibility examination conducted for candidates who wish to become teachers in primary (Class 1–5) and upper primary (Class 6–8) schools in Jharkhand. The exam is organized by the Jharkhand Academic Council (JAC) under the School Education & Literacy Department. It is not a direct recruitment exam but a qualifying test required for future teacher vacancies in the state.

BPSC Auditor Panchayati Raj Admit Card 2026
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the notification for Auditor (Panchayati Raj Department) Recruitment 2026 to fill a total of 102 posts. Candidates having a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce, Economics, Mathematics, or Statistics are eligible to apply. The online application process will start on 05/02/2026 and the last date to apply is 26/02/2026. The selection process includes Preliminary Examination, Main Examination, and Interview. This recruitment offers a stable government job with an attractive pay scale of Level-5 (₹5200–20200 + Grade Pay ₹2800). Interested and eligible candidates must apply online through the official BPSC website within the given dates.

RSSB CET Senior Secondary Level Vacancy 2026
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the Common Eligibility Test (CET) Senior Secondary Level 2026 notification for candidates seeking recruitment to various 12th-level government posts in Rajasthan. Eligible candidates who have passed Senior Secondary (10+2) or an equivalent examination can submit their online applications from 24/06/2026 to 23/07/2026. The CET will be conducted in offline (OMR-based) mode from 23/10/2026 to 25/10/2026. Candidates qualifying the CET will become eligible to apply for various departmental recruitments based on the applicable recruitment rules. Applicants are advised to read the official notification carefully before applying.
