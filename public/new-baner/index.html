RRC Eastern Railway Apprentice Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Eastern Railway, Kolkata has released the official notification for Apprentice Recruitment 2025 for a total of 3115 trade-wise vacancies. The recruitment is open for various divisions and workshops including Howrah, Sealdah, Asansol, Malda, Liluah, Jamalpur, and Kanchrapara. Candidates who have passed Class 10th and possess an ITI certificate in the relevant trade are eligible to apply. The age of the applicant should be between 15 and 24 years. The selection will be based purely on merit, considering the marks obtained in 10th and ITI – no written exam will be conducted. Applications must be submitted online through the official RRC ER website within the prescribed dates.

UP Police One Time Registration 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has launched the One Time Registration (OTR) 2025 process for all aspiring candidates. This OTR is mandatory for those who wish to apply for any upcoming UP Police recruitment exams. By completing the OTR once, candidates will not need to repeatedly enter their details for each application. This system ensures a faster, more transparent, and convenient application process. Interested candidates can register online at uppbpb.gov.in.

Super 100 Examination Form 2025
Super 100 Examination 2025 is a scholarship-based initiative by the Government of Madhya Pradesh to provide free coaching for NEET, JEE, and other competitive exams to talented students studying in government schools. Students currently in Class 10 (academic session 2024–25) can apply online. Selection will be based on a state-level entrance exam. The scheme also includes free hostel facilities, study materials, and expert mentorship for selected candidates.
