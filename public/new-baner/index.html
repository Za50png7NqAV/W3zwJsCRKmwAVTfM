Indian Army SSC Tech Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Indian Army has released the short notification for SSC Technical Entry 66th Course for Men and Women. A total of 379 vacancies are available, including 350 for men and 29 for women. The course will commence in April 2026 at Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai. Candidates with a Degree in Engineering (B.E. / B.Tech) in relevant disciplines are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 16 July 2025 and continue until 14 August 2025 through the official website joinindianarmy.nic.in. This is a great opportunity for aspiring engineers to join the Indian Army as Short Service Commission Officers.
