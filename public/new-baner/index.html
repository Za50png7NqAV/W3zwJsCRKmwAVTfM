Bank of Baroda Sales Manager & Agriculture Officer Vacancy 2025
Bank of Baroda has announced its Recruitment Drive 2025 for a total of 417 vacancies across two departments—Retail Liabilities and Rural & Agri Banking. The available posts include Manager – Sales, Officer – Agriculture Sales, and Manager – Agriculture Sales. The online application process begins on 6th August 2025 and closes on 26th August 2025. Candidates must hold relevant educational qualifications such as a graduation degree (with preference for MBA/PGDM in Sales/Marketing) or a 4-year degree in Agriculture-related fields. The selection process may include an online test, psychometric assessment, group discussion, and/or interview. Selected candidates will be posted anywhere in India and must commit to a 3-year service bond. Interested applicants are advised to visit the official Bank of Baroda website to read the full notification and apply online.

Bihar BSSC 4th Graduate Level Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification for the 4th Graduate Level Combined Competitive Examination 2025. This recruitment aims to fill 1481 vacancies across various departments of the Bihar Government. The posts include Assistant Branch Officer, Planning Assistant, Junior Statistical Assistant, Data Entry Operator (Grade-C), Auditor in the Finance Department, and Auditor in Cooperative Societies. Candidates must be graduates, with specific subject qualifications required for certain posts. The online application process will be open from 18 August to 19 September 2025. The selection will be done through a Preliminary Exam (objective type and open book with NCERT/BSEB textbooks), followed by a Main Exam and Document Verification. The application fee is ₹540 for General/BC/EBC (male) and outside Bihar candidates, and ₹135 for SC/ST, women (Bihar residents), and PwD candidates. Interested and eligible candidates can apply through the official BSSC website at https://bssc.bihar.gov.in.

Chandigarh JBT Teacher Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Samagra Shiksha, Chandigarh has released a notification for the recruitment of 218 Junior Basic Teacher (JBT) posts for Primary Classes (I to V). Candidates from across India are eligible to apply. The minimum qualification includes a Graduation degree from a recognized university, a 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) recognized by NCTE, and passing the CTET (Paper-I). The salary for the post is fixed at ₹45,260 per month. The application process is online and will begin on 07 August 2025 and end on 28 August 2025 (5:00 PM), while the last date to pay the fee is 30 August 2025 (2:00 PM). The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC/EWS, ₹500 for SC, and exempted for PWD candidates. The selection will be done through a written objective type test of 150 marks, with no interview. Category-wise distribution includes 111 posts for General, 44 for OBC, 41 for SC, and 22 for EWS candidates. Interested applicants must visit the official website ssachd.nic.in to apply and read the full notification.

MP Bhulekh (WebGis 2.0) 2025 - Khasra, B1, Naksha Download
MP Bhulekh (WebGis 2.0) 2025 is an official online portal developed by the Revenue Department of the Government of Madhya Pradesh. It allows citizens to access digital land records such as Khasra, B1, and Land Maps (Naksha) in PDF format. Through this platform, users can search by district, tehsil, village, or landowner name, and download digitally signed documents. The portal also provides an option to apply online for a Digital Land Ownership Certificate, ensuring transparency and easy access to verified land details. Services are available in both Hindi and English, making it citizen-friendly and accessible to all.

SBI Clerk (Junior Associate) Vacancy 2025
State Bank of India (SBI) has released the official notification for Junior Associate (Customer Support & Sales) recruitment under Advertisement No. CRPD/CR/2025-26/06. A total of 5583 vacancies have been announced, including 5180 regular and 403 backlog posts, to be filled across various states. The online application process will be open from 06 August to 26 August 2025. Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline, with final year students also eligible to apply provisionally. The age limit is 20 to 28 years as on 01 April 2025, with age relaxation applicable as per government rules. The selection process includes an online Preliminary Exam, Main Exam, and a Local Language Proficiency Test (if applicable). The Preliminary exam is expected in September 2025, and the Main exam in November 2025. The starting basic salary is ₹26,730/-, with gross monthly emoluments of around ₹46,000/- in metro cities. The application fee is ₹750/- for General/OBC/EWS, while it is exempted for SC/ST/PwBD/Ex-servicemen. Selected candidates will be posted in the state/circle they apply for, and no inter-state transfer will be allowed. Interested candidates can apply online through the SBI official website: https://sbi.co.in/web/careers.
