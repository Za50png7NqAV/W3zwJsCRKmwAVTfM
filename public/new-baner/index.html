MPESB Group 1 Sub Group 2 Admit Card 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has announced the Group 1 Subgroup 2 Combined Recruitment Test 2025 for 474 vacancies in different government departments. UP B.Ed. Admission 2026
UP B.Ed. Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) 2026 is conducted by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi for admission to the 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) course (2026–28). Candidates from General and OBC categories must have at least 50% marks in Graduation or Post-Graduation, while Engineering candidates require 55% marks. There is no minimum percentage requirement for SC/ST candidates. The selection process is based on the entrance examination followed by counselling.

CPCT Exam March-2026
The CPCT Exam 2026 is scheduled on 13th, 14th & 15th March-2026. The online application window will remain open from 15 to 29 December 2025. Applicants need to upload scanned photograph, signature and relevant marksheets. Mock test facility is available, candidates with 12th or 10th plus Polytechnic Diploma are eligible and the CPCT T Scorecard will remain valid for 7 years.
