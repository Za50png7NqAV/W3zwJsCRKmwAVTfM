SHS Bihar Ophthalmic Assistant Vacancy 2025
State Health Society Bihar is inviting applications for 220 Ophthalmic Assistant posts under Advt. No. 07/2025. Candidates with 10+2 in Biology/Mathematics and a 2-year Diploma in Optometry or Ophthalmic Assistant are eligible. The application window is open from 14 to 28 August 2025, with a monthly salary of ₹15,000. Selection will be through CBT, document verification, and medical examination.

BTSC Bihar Nursing Tutor Admit Card 2025
The Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of 498 Nursing Tutors under the Health Department, Government of Bihar. Candidates holding M.Sc Nursing, B.Sc Nursing (Basic/Post Basic), or a Diploma in Nursing Education & Administration (DNEA) are eligible to apply. The selection process includes eligibility verification and document scrutiny as per INC and Bihar Nursing Council norms. 35% of the vacancies are reserved for female candidates. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official BTSC website within the given time frame.

DSSSB Delhi High Court Various Post Vacancy 2025
DSSSB has announced 334 vacancies under Advt No. 03/2025 for various posts in the Delhi High Court, including Security Attendant, Court Attendant, and Room Attendant. The online application process will run from 26 August to 24 September 2025. Candidates with 10th pass or ITI qualification are eligible to apply. The recruitment offers a Level-3 pay scale (₹21,700–₹69,100), with an application fee of ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS and exemptions for SC/ST/PwBD/Women/Ex-servicemen. Selection will be through a computer-based test and subsequent stages as per the post.
