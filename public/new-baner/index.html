RRC SR Sports Quota Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Cell (Southern Railway) has released the Sports Quota Recruitment 2025 for Level 1 to 5 posts, with a total of 67 vacancies. Candidates with educational qualifications ranging from 10th Pass/ITI to Graduate are eligible to apply depending on the post. The age limit for applicants is 18–25 years as of 1 January 2026, with age relaxation as per government rules. The application fee is Rs 500 for General/OBC/EWS and Rs 250 for SC/ST/PWD/ESM/Female candidates, which is refundable. Interested candidates must apply online through the official website rrcmas.in. The application process starts on 13 September 2025 and closes on 12 October 2025, while the trials date will be notified later.

IOCL NR Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), Marketing Division – Northern Region, has announced 523 Apprentice vacancies for 2025. The application window is open from 12 September to 11 October 2025.
