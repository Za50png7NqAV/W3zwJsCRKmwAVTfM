Ruk Jana Nahi / Aa Laut Chale (ALC) Dec- 2025 Examination Form
The Madhya Pradesh State Open School (MPSOS) has released the schedule for the Aa Laut Chale (ALC) Dec-2025 Examination. The application process will run from 19 August 2025 to 31 October 2025, giving students enough time to register online. Along with the application, options for Paid, Unpaid, and Duplicate receipts will also be available starting 19 August 2025. This exam provides an important opportunity for students to continue their studies and complete their board education without losing a year.

Delhi DDA Recruitment 2025- Exam City
The Delhi Development Authority (DDA) has released its Recruitment Notification 2025 for a total of 1732 vacancies across various posts including Deputy Director, Assistant Director, Junior Engineer, Stenographer, Patwari, Mali, and Multi Tasking Staff (MTS). The online application process is scheduled from 06 October 2025 to 05 November 2025, and the Computer Based Test (CBT) is expected in December 2025 – January 2026. Candidates can apply online through the official DDA website. The recruitment includes category-wise vacancies (UR, EWS, SC, ST, OBC) and posts for ESM / PwBD candidates. Qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10th/12th pass to Graduate/Degree holders in relevant fields.

DRDO CEPTAM-11 Vacancy 2025
DRDO CEPTAM-11 Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 764 vacancies, including 561 posts for Senior Technical Assistant-B and 203 posts for Technician-A. The online application process will begin on 09 December 2025 and is expected to remain open until 08 January 2026. Eligible candidates can apply online after checking the required qualification, age limit, and fee details. The exam date will be notified later.
