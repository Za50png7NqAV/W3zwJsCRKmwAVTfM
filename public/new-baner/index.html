Chandigarh High Court Reader (Legal) Vacancy 2025
The Punjab and Haryana High Court, Chandigarh has invited online applications for 60 posts of Reader (Legal). Eligible candidates must have 10+2 with 60% marks in English, an LL.B. degree, and computer proficiency. The selection process includes a written exam, computer proficiency test, and viva-voce. The application process is open from 14 July 2025 to 04 August 2025. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for detailed eligibility, syllabus, and instructions.

Chandigarh High Court Peon Vacancy 2025
Chandigarh High Court has invited online applications for 75 posts of Peon (Residential). Eligible candidates with 10th pass qualification, 1-year diploma in cooking/culinary arts, and 3 years of cooking experience can apply from 14 July to 4 August 2025 at www.highcourtchd.gov.in. Applicants from all states are eligible, but reservation and fee relaxation are available only for residents of Punjab, Haryana, and Chandigarh. Selection will be based on a practical test (80 marks) and interview (20 marks). Application fee is ₹700 for general and ₹600 for reserved categories.

ICF Apprentice Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Integral Coach Factory (ICF), Indian Railways has released a notification for the recruitment of Apprentices for the year 2025. A total of approximately 1010 vacancies are available across various trades like Fitter, Electrician, Welder, Painter, Carpenter, Machinist, and MLT. The recruitment is open for both Fresher and Ex-ITI candidates. Applicants must have passed Class 10th or 12th, and ITI is required for certain trades. The selection will be based on merit, without any written exam.
