Samagra Portal 2026 - Downlaod Samagra ID Without Samagra OTP
The MP Samagra ID Portal (samagra.gov.in) is an official initiative by the Department of Social Justice and Disabled Welfare, Government of Madhya Pradesh. It helps citizens register their families and members under a single database to avail benefits of various state welfare schemes. Through this portal, users can easily apply for a new Samagra ID, print Family or Member ID cards, and update personal details such as name, address, or Aadhaar information online. This digital system ensures transparency, quick access, and smooth delivery of government services to every eligible household in Madhya Pradesh.

Shramodaya Admission Test Result 2026–27
Shramodaya Admission Test 2026–27 is conducted for admission to Shramodaya Awasiya Vidyalaya in Madhya Pradesh for Class 6, 7, 8 and 9. The admission is meant for children of registered construction workers under the MP Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. The online application process will be available from 24/12/2025 to 20/01/2026. Selection will be based on an entrance examination, followed by merit-based admission. The exam includes subjects like Hindi, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science and General Aptitude. Eligible students who qualify the test will get admission to Shramodaya residential schools as per district-wise seat availability.

Excellence School (SOE) & Model School (SOM) Class 9 Admission 2026–27 - Result
MP Excellence School Entrance Exam 2027 is conducted for admission to Class 9 in District Level Excellence Schools (SOE) and Block Level Model Schools (SOM) of Madhya Pradesh. Eligible students who have passed or are appearing in Class 8 and are residents of MP can apply online. The application process is open from 30/12/2025 to 16/01/2026. Selection will be done through a Joint Entrance Examination, and reserved category candidates will get reservation benefits as per government rules. The total application fee is ₹200, including portal charges.
