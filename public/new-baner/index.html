IB Multi Tasking Staff Vacancy 2025
The Intelligence Bureau has released the IB MTS Recruitment 2025 notification for 362 Multi-Tasking Staff (General) posts across various SIBs in India. KVS NVS Teaching & Non-Teaching Vacancy 2025
KVS NVS Recruitment 2025 offers more than 14967 Teaching and Non-Teaching vacancies under Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. NPCIL Deputy Manager & Jr. Hindi Translator Vacancy 2025
The Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL) has released the NPCIL Recruitment 2025 notification for 122 vacancies including Deputy Manager (HR, F&A, C&MM, Legal) and Junior Hindi Translator (JHT). The online application process will be open from 07 November to 27 November 2025 at npcilcareers.co.in. Candidates with MBA, CA, CMA, CFA, LLB, or Postgraduate qualifications can apply online. The selection process includes an Online Test and Interview for Deputy Manager posts and a Preliminary plus Advanced Test for JHT. Selected candidates will receive a salary of up to ₹86,955 per month along with various allowances and benefits. Posting can be anywhere in India under the Department of Atomic Energy.

UP Home Guard Vacancy 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Home Guard Recruitment 2025 has announced 41,424 vacancies for Home Guard (Male/Female) posts across all districts of the state. The online application process will run from 18 November 2025 to 17 December 2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10 (High School) from a recognized board and must be between 18 to 30 years of age as on 01 July 2025. The selection process includes Physical Standards Test (PST) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET), where male candidates must complete 4.8 km in 28 minutes and female candidates must complete 2.4 km in 16 minutes. The application fee is ₹400 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹300 for SC/ST candidates. The complete district-wise vacancy list and PET/PST rules are provided in the official notification.

MHA Intelligence Bureau ACIO Result 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has announced the recruitment of 3717 posts for Assistant Central Intelligence Officer (ACIO) Grade-II / Executive.
