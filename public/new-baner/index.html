PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana 2026 - 23th Kisht Date Released
प्रधानमंत्री किसान सम्मान निधि योजना (PM-KISAN) भारत सरकार द्वारा शुरू की गई एक प्रत्यक्ष लाभ अंतरण (DBT) आधारित योजना है, जिसका उद्देश्य देश के छोटे और सीमांत किसानों को वर्ष में ₹6,000 की आर्थिक सहायता प्रदान करना है। यह राशि तीन किस्तों में ₹2,000-₹2,000 करके सीधे किसानों के बैंक खाते में भेजी जाती है। योजना का लाभ उन्हीं किसानों को मिलता है जिनके पास भूमि का रिकॉर्ड मौजूद हो, जिन्होंने eKYC पूरा किया हो, और जो इनकम टैक्स दाता या सरकारी कर्मचारी न हों। इसका संचालन कृषि एवं किसान कल्याण मंत्रालय द्वारा किया जाता है, और किसान इसकी जानकारी और स्टेटस pmkisan.gov.in वेबसाइट पर देख सकते हैं।

DSSSB TGT, IT Assistant & JSA Vacancy 2026
Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released Advertisement No. 03/2026 for the recruitment of 1979 vacancies in various departments of the Government of NCT of Delhi. The recruitment includes posts such as Junior Scientific Assistant, IT Assistant, TGT (Computer Science), Domestic Science Teacher, Special Educator, Data Entry Operator, Lift Operator, Electrical Overseer and others. Online applications will be accepted from 16/06/2026 to 15/07/2026 through the official DSSSB website. Selection will be based on a One Tier Computer Based Examination with negative marking as per DSSSB rules.

SSC GD Constable Answer Key 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.
