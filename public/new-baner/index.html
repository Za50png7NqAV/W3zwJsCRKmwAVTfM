WCDC Bihar Various Post 2025 Online Form
The Women and Child Development Corporation (WCDC), Bihar has released the WCDC Bihar Various Post Recruitment 2025 notification for a total of 77 contractual vacancies. These include posts like Central Administrator, Case Worker, Para Legal Personnel / Lawyer, Para Medical Personnel, Psycho-Social Counsellor, and Office Assistant (with computer knowledge). Except for the Office Assistant post, all other posts are reserved exclusively for female candidates. Only women who are residents of Bihar are eligible to apply (male/female both can apply for Office Assistant). The application process is completely online and will remain open from 26 June 2025 to 18 July 2025 (up to 6:00 PM). Interested candidates can visit the official website wdc.bihar.gov.in for eligibility, category-wise vacancy details, and to submit their application.
