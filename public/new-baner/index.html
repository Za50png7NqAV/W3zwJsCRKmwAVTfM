BTSC Sports Trainer Online Form 2025
Sports Trainer (क्रीड़ा प्रशिक्षक) Recruitment 2025 notification has been released by the Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) for a total of 48 vacancies. The recruitment is for Sports Trainer posts under the state sports services. Eligible candidates having Graduation along with required sports qualification or state/national level sports participation can apply online. The online application process will start from 22/12/2025 and will continue till 22/01/2026. Selection of candidates will be done through a Computer Based Test (CBT) as per the official rules. This recruitment offers a good opportunity for candidates seeking government jobs in the sports sector in Bihar.

CTET February 2026 Registration - Correction Open
The CTET February 2026 notification has been released, and the online application process is open from 27/11/2025 to 18/12/2025. Candidates can apply for Paper I (Class 1–5) and Paper II (Class 6–8) based on NCTE eligibility norms. The exam will be conducted on 08 February 2026 in offline OMR mode across multiple exam centers. The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC (single paper) and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The CTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, and all important details regarding syllabus, exam pattern, eligibility, and fee structure are provided in the official notification.

Bihar BPSC AEDO Vacancy 2025 - Exam Date
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the official notification for 935 Assistant Education Development Officer (AEDO) vacancies under the Education Department. The online application process will begin on 27 August 2025 and continue till 26 September 2025. Candidates with a graduate degree from a recognized university are eligible to apply. The salary will be as per Level-5 pay scale (₹29,200 + allowances). Applicants must pay an examination fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges through online mode. Interested candidates should visit the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in to apply online and check detailed guidelines.

RRB Section Controller Vacancy 2025- Exam Date
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced 368 vacancies for Section Controller posts through CEN 04/2025. The online application process will be open from 15 September to 14 October 2025. Candidates with a graduation degree and age between 20 to 33 years can apply. Selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test, skill test (if required), document verification, and medical examination. The post offers a Level 6 pay scale with attractive allowances.

RRB Group D / Level 1 Vacancy 2026
Railway Recruitment Boards have released the indicative notice for Level-1 / Group D Recruitment under CEN 09/2025 to fill around 22000 vacancies across various RRB regions. These posts fall under Level-1 of the 7th Pay Commission with a starting pay of ₹18000 per month along with allowances. The online application process will begin on 21 January 2026 and will remain open till 20 February 2026 on the official RRB application portal. Candidates between 18 and 33 years of age as on 01 January 2026 who meet the prescribed educational qualifications will be eligible to apply. Complete details regarding post-wise eligibility selection process syllabus and medical standards will be available in the detailed notification published by the respective RRBs.
