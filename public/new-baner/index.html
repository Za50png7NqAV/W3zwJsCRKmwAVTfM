UPCATET Result 2025
The UPCATET Result 2025 has been officially released on June 24, 2025, by Chandra Shekhar Azad University of Agriculture & Technology. Candidates who appeared for the UG, PG, and PhD entrance exams can now check and download their scorecard and rank card from the official website upcatet.net using their registration credentials. The result includes subject-wise marks, group rank, and category rank. The counselling process will begin soon for qualified candidates.

UPSSSC Junior Assistant Mains Admit Card 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the Mains Admit Card for the Junior Assistant Recruitment 2025. Candidates who qualified for the preliminary exam can now download their admit card from the official website, upsssc.gov.in. The admit card contains important details like exam date, reporting time, exam center, and instructions. It is mandatory to carry a printed copy of the admit card along with a valid ID proof on the exam day.
