BSSC Inter Level Vacancy 2025
Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the notification for the Inter Level Combined Competitive Exam 2025 with 23,175 vacancies across various departments. Eligible candidates can register online from 15th October 2025 to 25th November 2025, while the final submission deadline is 27th November 2025. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with computer knowledge and typing skills. This is one of the biggest recruitment drives in Bihar offering opportunities for thousands of aspirants.

CSBC Bihar Police Constable Vacancy 2026
CSBC has released the Bihar Police Special Branch Constable Recruitment 2026 for 83 posts. Candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) are eligible to apply. The online application process starts from 06/02/2026 and ends on 05/03/2026. The application fee for all categories is ₹100, payable online. Selection will be done through a written examination, physical standards test (PST), and document verification. Eligible candidates should apply online through the official CSBC website within the given dates.

SBI Circle Based Officer CBO Vacancy 2026
SBI CBO Recruitment 2026 has been announced by the State Bank of India for 2050 Circle Based Officer (CBO) posts. The online application process will be open from 29 January 2026 to 18 February 2026. Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline and have at least 2 years of experience as an Officer in a Scheduled Commercial Bank or Regional Rural Bank (RRB). The selection process includes an online examination, screening of experience, interview, and local language proficiency test. Selected candidates will be posted in the same circle for which they apply, making this recruitment a great opportunity for experienced banking professionals to join SBI as officers.

JPSC Drug Inspector Online Form 2026
Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has released the Drug Inspector Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt No. 12/2025) for 30 posts under the Health, Medical Education & Family Welfare Department, Jharkhand. Eligible candidates with a degree in Pharmacy, Pharmaceutical Sciences, or Medicine with relevant experience can apply online through the One Time Registration (OTR) system. The online application process starts on 29 January 2026 and the last date to apply is 18 February 2026. Selection will be done through a written examination and interview.
