Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) has released the MLV125 Recruitment 2025 notification for various Group D posts under the Department of Printing and Stationery, Raipur. A total of 19 vacancies are available for positions such as Darkroom Assistant, Pasting Boy, Auxiliary, Inkman/Inker, Junior Binder, Hamal, Sweeper, Watchman, Mortuary Attendant, and Helper. Candidates who have passed 5th or 8th class and possess relevant experience (as per the post) are eligible to apply. Both male and female candidates can submit their applications online through the official website of CG Vyapam. The online application process is open from 27 June 2025 to 25 July 2025. Applicants must ensure to review the official notification carefully and complete the form, including uploading documents and paying the fee online. The written exam is tentatively scheduled for 31 August 2025 at district centers like Raipur, Durg, and Bilaspur.
