ISRO Assistant Rajbhasha Vacancy 2025
ISRO has invited online applications for Assistant (Rajbhasha) posts through Advertisement No. SAC:02:2025. A total of 07 vacancies are available across ISRO Centres and IIST. Candidates must have a Graduation degree with 60% marks, along with Hindi typing speed of 25 w.p.m. and computer proficiency. The age limit is 18 to 28 years as on 02 October 2025. The online application process is open from 12 September 2025 to 02 October 2025. All candidates must pay ₹500 as the application fee, which will be refunded fully or partially depending on category. Selection will be done through a Written Test and Skill Test.
