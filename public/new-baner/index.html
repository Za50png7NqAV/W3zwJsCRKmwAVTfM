CSIR Assistant Section Officer ASO Vacancy 2026
Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR) has released the Assistant Section Officer (ASO) Recruitment 2026 notification for 100 vacancies in General, Finance & Accounts, and Stores & Purchase cadres. Eligible candidates who possess a Bachelor's Degree and are non-recommended candidates of UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) 2024 can apply online from 23/06/2026 to 13/07/2026. The upper age limit is 33 years with relaxation for reserved categories as per rules. The application fee is ₹500 for UR/OBC/EWS candidates, while SC/ST/PwBD/Women/Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted. Selection will be based on UPSC CSE 2024 marks and a qualifying Computer Proficiency Test (CPT).

UP Police Constable Answer Key 2026
UP Police has released the Constable Recruitment 2025 notification for 32,679 vacancies across multiple units such as Civil Police, PAC, Special Security Force, Female Battalion, Mounted Police and Jail Warder. Applicants must have passed Intermediate (12th) from a recognized board. The prescribed age limit is 18 to 22 years for male candidates and 18 to 25 years for female candidates, with category-wise relaxation as per government norms. The recruitment process consists of a written test followed by PST, PET, document verification and medical examination. The online application window will be open from 31/12/2025 to 30/01/2026, making it one of the largest police recruitments in Uttar Pradesh.

CUET UG Final Answer Key 2026
CUET (UG) 2026 is a national-level admission test conducted by the National Testing Agency for undergraduate admissions in Central and other participating universities for the academic session 2026–27. Candidates who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 can apply online. The exam is held in CBT mode, allows up to five subject choices, and uses a standardized marking scheme. Admissions are granted by universities based on CUET scores, merit lists, and program-wise eligibility criteria.
