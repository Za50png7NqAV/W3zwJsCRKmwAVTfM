NABARD Assistant Manager Grade A Vacancy 2025
The National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has released the Grade A Assistant Manager Recruitment 2025 notification for 91 vacancies across various streams including Generalist, Agriculture, IT, Finance, Legal, and Protocol & Security Service. Delhi DDA Recruitment 2025
The Delhi Development Authority (DDA) has released its Recruitment Notification 2025 for a total of 1732 vacancies across various posts including Deputy Director, Assistant Director, Junior Engineer, Stenographer, Patwari, Mali, and Multi Tasking Staff (MTS). BSSC Stenographer Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification Advt. No. 07/2025 for the recruitment of 432 Stenographer and Stenotypist Grade-III posts in various departments and district offices. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with stenography (Hindi/English), typing, and basic computer knowledge. The selected candidates will get a salary in Pay Level-4 (₹25,500 – ₹81,100/-). The age limit is 18 to 37 years for UR male, 40 years for UR female, OBC and EBC candidates, and 42 years for SC/ST candidates, with 10 years of additional relaxation for PwD candidates. The application fee is ₹100/- to be paid online. The online registration will be open from 25th September 2025 to 5th November 2025, and the last date for fee payment is 3rd November 2025. Interested candidates can apply online only through the official website bssc.bihar.gov.in

MPESB MP Police Constable Admit Card 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPESB) has announced the MP Police Constable Recruitment 2025 for a total of 7500 Constable (General Duty – SAF & DEF) posts. The online application process will start from 15/09/2025 and remain open till 29/09/2025, while the correction window will be available up to 04/10/2025. The written examination is scheduled to begin from 30/10/2025. Candidates applying for this recruitment must have passed 10th/12th (for UR/SC/OBC/EWS) or at least 8th standard (for ST candidates) from a recognized board. The minimum age limit is 18 years and the maximum is 33 years for general category male candidates, with age relaxations applicable for reserved categories and women as per rules. The application fee is ₹500 for UR, ₹250 for SC/ST/OBC/EWS (MP domicile), while departmental candidates need to pay ₹200 (UR) or ₹100 (reserved categories) along with applicable portal charges. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for candidates seeking a stable government job in the Madhya Pradesh Police Department.
