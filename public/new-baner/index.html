BSSC Stenographer Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification Advt. No. 07/2025 for the recruitment of 432 Stenographer and Stenotypist Grade-III posts in various departments and district offices. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with stenography (Hindi/English), typing, and basic computer knowledge. The selected candidates will get a salary in Pay Level-4 (₹25,500 – ₹81,100/-). The age limit is 18 to 37 years for UR male, 40 years for UR female, OBC and EBC candidates, and 42 years for SC/ST candidates, with 10 years of additional relaxation for PwD candidates. The application fee is ₹100/- to be paid online. The online registration will be open from 25th September 2025 to 5th November 2025, and the last date for fee payment is 3rd November 2025. Interested candidates can apply online only through the official website bssc.bihar.gov.in

Union Bank SO (Wealth Manager) Vacancy Admit Card 2025
Union Bank of India has announced the Wealth Manager Recruitment 2025 for 250 Specialist Officer (MMGS-II) vacancies. The post offers a pay scale of ₹64,820–₹93,960 plus allowances, with an approximate CTC of ₹21 lakh in Mumbai. Candidates must be between 25 and 35 years of age as on 1 August 2025, hold a full-time 2-year MBA/MMS/PGDBA/PGDBM/PGPM/PGDM from a recognized institution, and have at least 3 years of experience in Wealth Management at the Officer/Manager level. Desirable certifications include NISM, IRDAI, NCFM, or AMFI. The application process will be open from 5 August to 25 August 2025, with fees of ₹177 for SC/ST/PwBD candidates and ₹1,180 for others. The selection process may include an online examination, group discussion, and personal interview. Selected candidates will serve a probation period of 2 years and must sign a service bond to serve the bank for a minimum of 3 years or pay ₹2,50,000 plus taxes if leaving early.
