HDFC Scholarship 2025-26
HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECSS Scholarship 2025-26 is a financial assistance programme aimed at helping meritorious students from economically weaker sections continue their education without interruption. The scholarship is open for school students (Class 1 to 12), as well as those pursuing diploma, ITI, polytechnic, undergraduate, and postgraduate courses. Under this initiative, eligible students facing financial challenges, family crises, or risk of dropout can receive support of up to INR 75,000. The programme ensures that bright and deserving students get equal opportunities to pursue their academic goals and build a successful future.
