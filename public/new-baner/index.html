BSSC Sports Trainer Vacancy 2025
Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has announced 379 vacancies for Sports Instructor/Trainer under Advt. No. 08/2025. AAI Graduate & Diploma Apprenticeship Vacancy 2025
Airports Authority of India (AAI) has announced the AAI Apprentice Recruitment 2025-26 for 20 posts of Graduate and Diploma Apprentices under the Apprentices Act, 1961 at RCDU/FIU/CRSD & E&M Workshop, Safdarjung Airport, New Delhi. Candidates who have completed their BE/B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc, B.Com, BA, BBA, or Diploma (Mechanical, Automobile, EEE, ECE, Instrumentation, Material Management) after 2021 are eligible to apply. The apprenticeship duration will be 1 year, with a monthly stipend of ₹15,000 for graduates and ₹12,000 for diploma holders. Selection will be based on merit and document verification, and applications can be submitted through the NATS portal (nats.education.gov.in) till 24 November 2025. Preference will be given to candidates from the Delhi/NCR region.

MPTAAS Profile Registration 2025
MPTAAS Profile Registration 2025 is a mandatory process for SC, ST, and OBC students in Madhya Pradesh to apply for Post Matric Scholarships. The profile is created on the Tribal Affairs portal, enabling students to apply for schemes, track status, and receive benefits via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer). Important documents like Aadhaar, Samagra ID, caste certificate, domicile proof, and bank details are required. Once registered, students can log in and apply for scholarships online.

SSC Jr Engineer Online Self-Slot Booking Form 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for the Junior Engineer (JE) Recruitment 2025. The vacancies are available in various departments such as BRO, CPWD, CWC, MES, NTRO, and others for trades including Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical. Candidates must have a Diploma or Degree in the relevant engineering discipline. Some departments also require two years of work experience. The application process is completely online, and the selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (Paper 1), Paper 2, and Document Verification. This recruitment is open to both male and female candidates across India. Interested applicants can visit the official SSC website https://ssc.gov.in for more details and to apply online.

SSC CPO SI 2025 Online Self Slot Booking
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for CPO SI Recruitment 2025, offering 3073 vacancies for Sub-Inspector posts in Delhi Police and CAPFs. Eligible candidates with a bachelor’s degree can apply online from 26 September to 16 October 2025. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (Paper-I & II), Physical Tests (PST/PET), Medical Examination, and Document Verification. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for graduates to join Delhi Police and CAPFs with a pay scale of Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400).
