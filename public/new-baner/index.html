BPSC Lower Division Clerk LDC Admit Card 2025
The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of Lower Division Clerk (LDC) posts with a total of 26 vacancies. Candidates who have passed Intermediate (10+2) in any stream from a recognized board and possess computer operation and typing skills are eligible to apply. This recruitment is primarily for domicile candidates of Bihar, who will receive reservation benefits as per their category. Candidates from other states can also apply but will be considered under the General (Unreserved) category only, without any reservation or age relaxation. The application process is completely online. Out of the total posts, 13 are for General, 3 for EWS, 4 for SC, 1 for ST, 2 for EBC, 2 for BC, and 1 for BC Female.

Azim Premji Foundation Scholarship 2025 – Online Application Begins
The Azim Premji Scholarship 2025 is a flagship initiative of the Azim Premji Foundation aimed at supporting girl students from disadvantaged backgrounds to continue their higher education. Under this program, eligible students receive ₹30,000 per year for the entire duration of their first undergraduate degree or diploma course (2–5 years) at recognized government or private colleges across India. To qualify, the applicant must have passed Class 10 and 12 as a regular student from a government school or college and secured admission in the first year of a UG or diploma course for the 2025–26 academic session. The scholarship is open to students from select states and union territories including Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Puducherry, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Telangana, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, and Uttarakhand. The application process for the Scholarship Cohort 2025 will begin on 10 September 2025, and candidates must submit documents like a passport-size photograph, Aadhaar card, and signature image. Importantly, there is no application fee, and students are advised to beware of fraud or impostors.

SBI Manager & Deputy Manager Vacancy 2025
The State Bank of India (SBI) has announced the SBI SCO Recruitment 2025 for a total of 59 Specialist Cadre Officer vacancies on a regular basis, including the posts of Manager (MMGS-III) and Deputy Manager (MMGS-II) in the Digital Platforms domain. Candidates must hold a B.E./B.Tech. in IT, Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical, or related fields, or an MCA with at least 60% marks, while an MBA is preferred. Relevant work experience of 5 years for Manager and 3 years for Deputy Manager in Digital Payments, FinTech, CRM, or related fields is mandatory. The age limit is 28–35 years for Manager and 25–32 years for Deputy Manager (as on 31.08.2025). The application fee is ₹750 for General, OBC, and EWS candidates, while SC, ST, and PwBD candidates are exempted. The selection process will be based on shortlisting and interview. Eligible candidates can apply online from 11th September to 2nd October 2025 through the official SBI Careers website.

UP Police Sub Inspector SI Vacancy 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has announced the UP Police Sub Inspector (SI) Recruitment 2025. The online application process will be open from 12 August 2025 to 11 September 2025, and fee adjustments for submitted payments can be made until 13 September 2025. The application fee is ₹500/- for General, EWS, and OBC candidates, while SC and ST candidates will have to pay ₹400/-. The recruitment is for posts in Uttar Pradesh, and applications must be submitted online.
