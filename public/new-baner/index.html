CSBC Bihar Police Recruitment 2025
The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC), Bihar has released Advertisement No. 03/2025 for the recruitment of 4128 vacancies including Prohibition Constable, Jail Warder, and Mobile Squad Constable. BHEL Artisan Vacancy 2025 Admit Card Out
Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) has announced 515 vacancies for Artisan Grade IV posts under various trades such as Fitter, Welder, Turner, Machinist, Electrician, Electronics Mechanic, and Foundryman for the year 2025.
