BPSC Deputy Director (Fire Service) Vacancy 2026
The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the notification for Deputy Director (Fire Service) Recruitment 2026. A total of 14 vacancies are available under this recruitment. The online application process will be open from 13 January 2026 to 03 February 2026. Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s degree in Science or Engineering, along with recognized fire service training and a minimum of 12 years of relevant work experience. The selection will be based on educational qualifications, work experience, and interview. This recruitment offers a senior-level career opportunity for experienced fire service professionals.
