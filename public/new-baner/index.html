SBI Deputy Manager & AVP Vacancy 2026
The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the SBI SCO Recruitment 2026 notification for 116 Specialist Cadre Officer posts, including Assistant Vice President (IS Audit) and Deputy Manager (IS Audit and CA–Internal Audit). The online application process started on 23/02/2026 and will remain open until 15/03/2026. The selection will be based on shortlisting and interview only.
