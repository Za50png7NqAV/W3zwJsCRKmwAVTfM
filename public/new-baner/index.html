MP Rojgar Panjiyan Online Registration 2026
MP Rojgar Panjiyan 2026 is the official employment registration system launched by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Through this portal (mprojgar.gov.in), job seekers can register for free, get notified about government and private job opportunities, participate in job fairs, and renew their registration every 3 years. The process is fully online, and applicants need to provide basic personal information, educational documents, and Aadhaar details to complete their profile.

NTA NEET UG 2026 Re Exam Answer Key
NEET (UG) 2026 is a national-level medical entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. The exam is mandatory for admission to undergraduate medical and allied courses including MBBS, BDS, AYUSH (BAMS, BUMS, BSMS, BHMS), BVSc & AH, and B.Sc Nursing (MNS) across India. Candidates must be at least 17 years old by 31 December 2026 and should have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English. There is no upper age limit and no restriction on the number of attempts. NEET (UG) 2026 will be held on 03 May 2026 in offline mode for a duration of 3 hours and will be conducted in 13 languages. Qualifying NEET is compulsory for admission to all participating institutions.
