Delhi DDA Recruitment 2025
The Delhi Development Authority (DDA) has released its Recruitment Notification 2025 for a total of 1732 vacancies across various posts including Deputy Director, Assistant Director, Junior Engineer, Stenographer, Patwari, Mali, and Multi Tasking Staff (MTS). MPESB Candidate Profiling / Profile Panjiyan Form Online
To apply for any government recruitment under the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB), all candidates must complete the Candidate Profile Registration process. This is a one-time mandatory step where applicants register using Aadhaar eKYC, upload personal and academic details, and receive a unique Profile ID. This ID is required for all future MPESB applications. The process is simple, online, and essential for anyone seeking government jobs in Madhya Pradesh.

UPPSC APO Recruitment 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the official notification for Assistant Prosecution Officer (APO) Recruitment 2025 with 182 vacancies. Eligible candidates with a Law degree (LLB) can apply online from 16 September 2025 to 16 October 2025 through the UPPSC portal. The selection process includes Prelims, Mains, and Interview, followed by document verification and medical examination. Applicants must be between 21 to 40 years of age as on 1 July 2025. The pay scale ranges from Rs. 47,600 to Rs. 1,51,100, offering a great career opportunity for law graduates in Uttar Pradesh.

SBI Clerk (Junior Associate) Prelims Admit Card 2025 Out
State Bank of India (SBI) has released the official notification for Junior Associate (Customer Support & Sales) recruitment under Advertisement No. CRPD/CR/2025-26/06. A total of 5583 vacancies have been announced, including 5180 regular and 403 backlog posts, to be filled across various states. The online application process will be open from 06 August to 26 August 2025. Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline, with final year students also eligible to apply provisionally. The age limit is 20 to 28 years as on 01 April 2025, with age relaxation applicable as per government rules. The selection process includes an online Preliminary Exam, Main Exam, and a Local Language Proficiency Test (if applicable). The Preliminary exam is expected in September 2025, and the Main exam in November 2025. The starting basic salary is ₹26,730/-, with gross monthly emoluments of around ₹46,000/- in metro cities. The application fee is ₹750/- for General/OBC/EWS, while it is exempted for SC/ST/PwBD/Ex-servicemen. Selected candidates will be posted in the state/circle they apply for, and no inter-state transfer will be allowed. Interested candidates can apply online through the SBI official website: https://sbi.co.in/web/careers.

UP Police Sub Inspector SI Vacancy 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has announced the UP Police Sub Inspector (SI) Recruitment 2025. The online application process will be open from 12 August 2025 to 11 September 2025, and fee adjustments for submitted payments can be made until 13 September 2025. The application fee is ₹500/- for General, EWS, and OBC candidates, while SC and ST candidates will have to pay ₹400/-. The recruitment is for posts in Uttar Pradesh, and applications must be submitted online.
