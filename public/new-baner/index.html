UPSC IES / ISS Vacancy 2026
Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the notification for Indian Economic Service (IES) and Indian Statistical Service (ISS) Examination 2026. A total of 44 vacancies (16 for IES and 28 for ISS) are available. Eligible candidates can apply online from 11/02/2026 to 03/03/2026. The written examination will commence from 19/06/2026. Candidates must meet the prescribed educational qualifications in Economics or Statistics as per service requirement. The selection process includes a written examination followed by an interview.

Indian Army Agniveer Rally Recruitment 2026
Indian Army Rally Recruitment 2026-27 notification has been released for Agniveer GD, Technical, Assistant, Tradesman, Women Military Police, Soldier Technical (Nursing Assistant), Sepoy Pharma and JCO Religious Teacher posts. NTA NCET 2026 Online Form
NCET 2026 (National Common Entrance Test) is a national-level entrance examination conducted by NTA for admission to the 4-Year Integrated Teacher Education Programme (ITEP) in participating Central, State, and other recognized universities. Candidates who have passed Class 12 or are appearing in 2026 are eligible to apply. The exam is conducted in Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode and includes Language, Teaching Aptitude, General Test, and Domain-specific subjects. Qualified candidates will be admitted through the counselling process as per university rules.
