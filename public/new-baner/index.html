NIACL Apprentice Vacancy 2026
The New India Assurance Company Limited (NIACL), a Government of India Undertaking, has released the Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification for 550 Apprentice posts under the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS). CUET UG Result / Score Card 2026
CUET (UG) 2026 is a national-level admission test conducted by the National Testing Agency for undergraduate admissions in Central and other participating universities for the academic session 2026–27. Admissions are granted by universities based on CUET scores, merit lists, and program-wise eligibility criteria.

Central Bank of India Apprentice Vacancy 2026
Central Bank of India has released the Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification for 4500 Apprentice posts under the Apprentices Act, 1961. Interested candidates who have completed a Graduation degree on or after 01 January 2022 and are aged between 20 and 28 years can apply online from 12 June 2026 to 22 June 2026. Selected candidates will undergo 12 months of apprenticeship training and receive a monthly stipend of ₹15,000. The selection process includes an Online Examination, Local Language Test, Document Verification, and Medical Fitness Test. This recruitment offers a valuable opportunity for graduates seeking experience in the banking sector.

RRB Section Controller Vacancy 2026
Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have released a short notification for Section Controller Recruitment 2026 (CEN No. 03/2026). A total of 119 vacancies have been announced for the Section Controller post. Candidates holding a Bachelor’s Degree (Graduate) from a recognized university are eligible to apply. The age limit is 20 to 33 years as on 01/07/2026. Selected candidates will receive a salary under Pay Level-6 with an initial pay of ₹35,400 per month. The online application process is expected to begin on 15/07/2026 and continue until 14/08/2026. Candidates should read the detailed notification carefully for complete eligibility conditions, application fee details, and selection process.
