Indian Navy SSC Officer Various Posts Vacancy 2026
Indian Navy has released the notification for Short Service Commission (SSC) Officers Jan 2027 Course. Applications are invited from unmarried male and female candidates for Executive, Education and Technical Branches. Eligible candidates having BE/B.Tech, MSc, MBA, MCA, M.Tech with minimum 60% marks can apply online from 24 January 2026 to 24 February 2026. The selection process includes shortlisting based on normalized marks, SSB interview and medical examination. Selected candidates will undergo training at Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Kerala and will receive a starting salary of approximately ₹1,25,000 per month.

CUET (UG) 2026 Online Form
CUET (UG) 2026 is a national-level admission test conducted by the National Testing Agency for undergraduate admissions in Central and other participating universities for the academic session 2026–27. Candidates who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 can apply online. The exam is held in CBT mode, allows up to five subject choices, and uses a standardized marking scheme. Admissions are granted by universities based on CUET scores, merit lists, and program-wise eligibility criteria.

Indian Army JAG Entry Scheme 124th Course 2026
Indian Army JAG Recruitment 2026 is conducted for the 124th Course (October 2026) under the Judge Advocate General (JAG) Branch. This recruitment is open for unmarried male and female law graduates for Short Service Commission (SSC). A total of 08 vacancies are available. The online application process will be open from 27 January 2026 to 27 February 2026. Selection will be based on CLAT PG 2025 score, SSB interview, medical examination, and final merit list. Selected candidates will undergo 49 weeks of training at the Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai.
