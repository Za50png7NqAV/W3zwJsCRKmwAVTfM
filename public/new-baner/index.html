FSSAI Food License Registration Online Form
The FSSAI Food License is mandatory for all food-related businesses in India to ensure food safety and compliance with the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. It is issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. There are three types of licenses – Basic, State, and Central – depending on the business size and turnover. Applicants must submit documents like identity proof, address proof, and business registration while applying online through the FSSAI portal. The license is valid for 1 to 5 years, and renewal is required before expiry. Running a food business without a license may lead to heavy penalties or legal action.

DRDO Apprentice Vacancy 2025
The DRDO Apprentice Recruitment 2025 has been officially announced by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) for 195 apprentice vacancies at the Research Centre Imarat (RCI), Hyderabad. The recruitment includes Graduate Apprentices (40 posts), Technician/Diploma Apprentices (20 posts), and ITI Trade Apprentices (135 posts) under the Apprenticeship Act, 1961. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online from 27th September 2025 to 28th October 2025 through the official DRDO website. Selection will be based purely on academic merit followed by document verification, and no written exam will be conducted. Candidates with ITI, Diploma, or Graduation in relevant disciplines can apply. This is a great opportunity for freshers to gain valuable hands-on training and experience in India’s premier defence research organisation. Applicants are advised to carefully check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application process, and important dates before applying.
