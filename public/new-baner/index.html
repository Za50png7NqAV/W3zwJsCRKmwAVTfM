BPSC Project Manager Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of 09 Project Manager posts under the Bihar Industries Service. Eligible candidates can apply online from 10th September 2025 to 6th October 2025. Candidates must hold at least a second-class degree in Engineering, MBA, PG Diploma, Economics, CA, CMA, Pharmacy, or related disciplines. The age limit is 21 to 37 years for UR male candidates, with relaxations applicable for reserved categories as per rules. The selection process will include Preliminary Exam (Objective), Main Written Exam, and Interview. The pay scale is Level–9.

Indian Army SSC Tech 67th Women Vacancy 2026
The Indian Army has announced SSC (Technical) 67 Women Recruitment 2026 for female engineering candidates. A total of 29 vacancies are available in Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics & Communication and Mechanical engineering streams. Eligible unmarried women can apply online between 06 January 2026 and 04 February 2026 at joinindianarmy.nic.in. The selection process includes shortlisting, SSB interview, medical examination and preparation of merit list. Selected candidates will undergo 49 weeks of training and will be paid a stipend of ₹56,100 per month during training.
