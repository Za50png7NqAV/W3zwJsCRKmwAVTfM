BTSC Food Analyst Vacancy 2026
BTSC Food Analyst Recruitment 2026 has been released by the Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) for 10 Food Analyst posts. Candidates with Bachelor's, Master's, or Doctorate degrees in Food Technology, Chemistry, Microbiology, Biotechnology, Agriculture, Veterinary Science, Fisheries Science, Food & Nutrition, or related disciplines may apply, subject to the eligibility conditions mentioned in the notification. Applicants must also have passed the FSSAI Food Analyst Examination and possess the required experience in food analysis. The online application process will commence on 20 May 2026 and continue until 19 June 2026. Selection will be based on a Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by document verification. Candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully before applying.

UPSSSC Lower PCS Vacancy 2026
UPSSSC Lower Subordinate Recruitment 2026 is a state-level government job notification released for 2285 posts under various departments of Uttar Pradesh.
