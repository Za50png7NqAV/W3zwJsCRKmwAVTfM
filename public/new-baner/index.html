Excellence School (SOE) & Model School (SOM) Class 9 Admission 2026–27 - Admit Card
MP Excellence School Entrance Exam 2027 is conducted for admission to Class 9 in District Level Excellence Schools (SOE) and Block Level Model Schools (SOM) of Madhya Pradesh. Eligible students who have passed or are appearing in Class 8 and are residents of MP can apply online. The application process is open from 30/12/2025 to 16/01/2026. Selection will be done through a Joint Entrance Examination, and reserved category candidates will get reservation benefits as per government rules. The total application fee is ₹200, including portal charges.

Delhi High Court Junior Judicial Assistant / Restorer 2026
Delhi High Court has released the Junior Judicial Assistant / Restorer Recruitment 2026 notification for a total of 152 posts. The online application process will start from 04-02-2026 and the last date to apply is 23-02-2026. Candidates must be graduates from a recognized university and should have a minimum English typing speed of 35 WPM. The selection process includes Preliminary Examination, Main Descriptive Examination, English Typing Test, and Interview. Applications are accepted online only through the official website.

BPSSC Bihar Police ASI Operation Vacancy 2026
Bihar Police Radio Assistant Sub Inspector (Operation) Recruitment 2026 has been released by BPSSC for a total of 462 posts. The recruitment is open to candidates having a B.Sc. degree with Physics from a recognized university. The selection process includes a written examination (preliminary and main) followed by a physical efficiency test (PET), medical examination, and document verification. Eligible candidates can apply online from 04 February to 04 March 2026 through the official website. This recruitment offers a stable Level-5 government job in the Bihar Police Radio (Wireless) wing with good career growth opportunities.

UP Police Constable Exam Date 2025
UP Police has released the Constable Recruitment 2025 notification for 32,679 vacancies across multiple units such as Civil Police, PAC, Special Security Force, Female Battalion, Mounted Police and Jail Warder. Applicants must have passed Intermediate (12th) from a recognized board. The prescribed age limit is 18 to 22 years for male candidates and 18 to 25 years for female candidates, with category-wise relaxation as per government norms. The recruitment process consists of a written test followed by PST, PET, document verification and medical examination. The online application window will be open from 31/12/2025 to 30/01/2026, making it one of the largest police recruitments in Uttar Pradesh.
