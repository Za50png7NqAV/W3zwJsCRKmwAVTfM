MPESB MP Police Constable Vacancy 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPESB) has announced the MP Police Constable Recruitment 2025 for a total of 7500 Constable (General Duty – SAF & DEF) posts. The online application process will start from 15/09/2025 and remain open till 29/09/2025, while the correction window will be available up to 04/10/2025. The written examination is scheduled to begin from 30/10/2025. Candidates applying for this recruitment must have passed 10th/12th (for UR/SC/OBC/EWS) or at least 8th standard (for ST candidates) from a recognized board. The minimum age limit is 18 years and the maximum is 33 years for general category male candidates, with age relaxations applicable for reserved categories and women as per rules. The application fee is ₹500 for UR, ₹250 for SC/ST/OBC/EWS (MP domicile), while departmental candidates need to pay ₹200 (UR) or ₹100 (reserved categories) along with applicable portal charges. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for candidates seeking a stable government job in the Madhya Pradesh Police Department.

ISRO SAC Group C Vacancy 2025
Space Applications Centre (SAC), Ahmedabad – Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has released a recruitment notification for 55 vacancies of Technician ‘B’ and Pharmacist ‘A’ under Group ‘C’ posts. Eligible candidates can apply online through sac.gov.in from 24 October 2025 to 13 November 2025. The recruitment includes various trades such as Fitter, Machinist, Electrician, Electronics Mechanic, IT/ICTSM, Lab Assistant (Chemical Plant) and Refrigeration & Air Conditioning. The selection process consists of a Written Test followed by a Skill Test (qualifying nature). Salary: Level 3 (₹21,700–₹69,100) for Technician B and Level 5 (₹29,200–₹92,300) for Pharmacist A Age Limit: 18–35 years (as on 13/11/2025) Qualification: 10th Pass + ITI/NTC/NAC in relevant trade or Diploma in Pharmacy (First Class)

IB ACIO-II/Tech Vacancy 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs, has released the official notification for IB ACIO-II/Technical Recruitment 2025. A total of 258 vacancies are available for disciplines in Computer Science & Information Technology and Electronics & Communication. Eligible candidates with valid GATE 2023, 2024, or 2025 scores can apply online from 25/10/2025 to 16/11/2025 through www.mha.gov.in. The recruitment includes Level-7 pay scale (₹44,900–₹1,42,400), with additional security allowances and other benefits. Selection will be based on GATE score, Skill Test, and Interview.

EMRS Teaching & Non-Teaching Vacancy 2025
The National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) has announced the EMRS Recruitment 2025 (ESSE-2025) notification for a total of 7267 vacancies across teaching and non-teaching posts. The vacancies include Principal, PGT, TGT, Female Staff Nurse, Hostel Warden, Accountant, Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Lab Attendant. Candidates can apply online until 23rd October 2025, with application fees ranging from ₹500 to ₹2500 depending on the post and category. The eligibility criteria vary by position, requiring qualifications from 10th/12th to Graduate, Postgraduate, B.Ed., CTET, or professional degrees. The age limit is set between 30 to 50 years, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories. This recruitment provides a major opportunity for aspirants seeking teaching and non-teaching jobs in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) across India.
