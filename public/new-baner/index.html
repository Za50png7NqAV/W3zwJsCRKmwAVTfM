Indian Navy Agniveer Apprentice INET Vacancy 2026
Indian Navy has released the Agniveer (Apprentice) Recruitment 2027 Notification for unmarried male candidates. Eligible candidates who have passed Class 10th with at least 50% marks and completed a 3-year AICTE-recognized Diploma with 50% marks can apply online. The selection process includes INET Examination, Physical Fitness Test (PFT), Stage-II Written Examination, and Medical Examination. Selected candidates will receive a monthly package starting from ₹30,000 along with various allowances, insurance benefits, and Seva Nidhi benefits. Interested candidates can submit their applications online from 05 June 2026 to 29 June 2026 through the official Indian Navy recruitment portal.

UPSSSC Auditor & Assistant Accountant Vacancy 2026
The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the notification for the Auditor and Assistant Accountant Main Examination 2026. A total of **1829 vacancies** have been announced for various departments of the Uttar Pradesh Government. Candidates who have qualified **UPSSSC PET 2025** and possess the required educational qualifications can apply online. The online application process will begin on **14 July 2026** and continue until **03 August 2026**. Interested candidates are advised to check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application fee, selection process, and other important details before submitting their application forms. This recruitment offers an excellent opportunity for eligible graduates seeking government jobs in the accounting and auditing sector.

UPSSSC Excise Constable Vacancy 2026
UPSSSC has released the Excise Constable Recruitment 2026 notification for a total of 722 posts. Candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) and appeared in PET 2025 are eligible to apply. The application process is online, starting from 04/06/2026 and ending on 24/06/2026. The selection will be based on PET score shortlisting, followed by a written examination, physical test, and document verification. The age limit is 18 to 40 years, with relaxation as per government rules.
