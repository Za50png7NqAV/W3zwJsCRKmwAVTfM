NBEMS Group A, B & C Various Posts Vacancy 2026
The National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS) has released the NBEMS Recruitment 2026 Notification for 53 vacancies across various posts including Junior Assistant, Stenographer, Junior Accountant, Junior Programmer, and Deputy Director (Medical). The online application process will begin on 29/06/2026 and continue until 20/07/2026. Candidates with qualifications ranging from 12th Pass, Graduation, Engineering, and Postgraduate Medical Degrees can apply as per the post requirements. The application fee is ₹1,500 + 18% GST for General/OBC candidates, while SC/ST/PwBD/Women candidates are exempted from paying any fee. Interested applicants should carefully review the official notification for detailed eligibility criteria, age limits, selection process, and other important instructions before applying online.

HSSC CET Group D Vacancy 2026
Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released the CET Group D Recruitment 2026 notification for recruitment to various Group D posts in Haryana Government departments, boards, and corporations. Eligible candidates who have passed Matriculation (10th Class) with Hindi or Sanskrit as a subject can apply online through the official website. The online application process starts from 19 June 2026 and the last date to apply is 03 July 2026. Selection will be based on the Common Eligibility Test (CET) score followed by document verification. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for detailed information regarding eligibility, age limit, reservation, examination pattern, and recruitment process before applying.

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana 2026 - 23th Installment Release Today
प्रधानमंत्री किसान सम्मान निधि योजना (PM-KISAN) भारत सरकार द्वारा शुरू की गई एक प्रत्यक्ष लाभ अंतरण (DBT) आधारित योजना है, जिसका उद्देश्य देश के छोटे और सीमांत किसानों को वर्ष में ₹6,000 की आर्थिक सहायता प्रदान करना है। यह राशि तीन किस्तों में ₹2,000-₹2,000 करके सीधे किसानों के बैंक खाते में भेजी जाती है। योजना का लाभ उन्हीं किसानों को मिलता है जिनके पास भूमि का रिकॉर्ड मौजूद हो, जिन्होंने eKYC पूरा किया हो, और जो इनकम टैक्स दाता या सरकारी कर्मचारी न हों। इसका संचालन कृषि एवं किसान कल्याण मंत्रालय द्वारा किया जाता है, और किसान इसकी जानकारी और स्टेटस pmkisan.gov.in वेबसाइट पर देख सकते हैं।
