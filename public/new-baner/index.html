RRB Group D / Level 1 Vacancy 2026
Railway Recruitment Boards have released the indicative notice for Level-1 / Group D Recruitment under CEN 09/2025 to fill around 21997 vacancies across various RRB regions. These posts fall under Level-1 of the 7th Pay Commission with a starting pay of ₹18000 per month along with allowances. The online application process will begin on 21 January 2026 and will remain open till 20 February 2026 on the official RRB application portal. Candidates between 18 and 33 years of age as on 01 January 2026 who meet the prescribed educational qualifications will be eligible to apply. Complete details regarding post-wise eligibility selection process syllabus and medical standards will be available in the detailed notification published by the respective RRBs.

CSBC Bihar Police Constable Vacancy 2026
CSBC has released the Bihar Police Special Branch Constable Recruitment 2026 for 83 posts. Candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) are eligible to apply. Eligible candidates should apply online through the official CSBC website within the given dates.

CTET February 2026 Registration - Admit Card
The CTET February 2026 notification has been released, and the online application process is open from 27/11/2025 to 18/12/2025. Candidates can apply for Paper I (Class 1–5) and Paper II (Class 6–8) based on NCTE eligibility norms. The exam will be conducted on 08 February 2026 in offline OMR mode across multiple exam centers. The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC (single paper) and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The CTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, and all important details regarding syllabus, exam pattern, eligibility, and fee structure are provided in the official notification.
