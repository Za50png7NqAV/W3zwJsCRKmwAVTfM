MPESB Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025 Answer Key Out
Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the notification for the Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025. A total of 10,150 Varg 3 Primary Teacher posts will be recruited under the School Education Department. Eligible candidates who have qualified the MP Primary TET 2020 or 2024 can apply online. Applicants must check the detailed eligibility, age limit, and other conditions in the official notification before applying. Candidates from outside MP can also apply but will not get any domicile reservation or relaxation benefits.

BSF Sports Quota Vacancy 2025
The Border Security Force (BSF) under the Ministry of Home Affairs has released the Constable (GD) Sports Quota Recruitment 2025 notification for 391 vacancies. Eligible Indian male and female sportspersons who have participated or won medals in recognized National or International competitions (2023–2025) can apply online from 16 October 2025 to 4 November 2025 at rectt.bsf.gov.in. Candidates must have passed Matriculation (10th) and meet prescribed age, physical, and medical standards. The pay scale is Level-3 (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) as per the 7th CPC with admissible allowances. Application fee is ₹159 for UR/OBC male candidates, while SC/ST and female applicants are exempted. Selection will be based on sports merit, documentation, PST, and medical examination.

BSSC Inter Level Vacancy 2025
Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the notification for the Inter Level Combined Competitive Exam 2025 with 23,175 vacancies across various departments. Eligible candidates can register online from 15th October 2025 to 25th November 2025, while the final submission deadline is 27th November 2025. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with computer knowledge and typing skills. This is one of the biggest recruitment drives in Bihar offering opportunities for thousands of aspirants.
