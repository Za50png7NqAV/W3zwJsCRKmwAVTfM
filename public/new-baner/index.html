RSSB CET Senior Secondary Level Vacancy 2026
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the Common Eligibility Test (CET) Senior Secondary Level 2026 notification for candidates seeking recruitment to various 12th-level government posts in Rajasthan. Eligible candidates who have passed Senior Secondary (10+2) or an equivalent examination can submit their online applications from 24/06/2026 to 23/07/2026. The CET will be conducted in offline (OMR-based) mode from 23/10/2026 to 25/10/2026. Candidates qualifying the CET will become eligible to apply for various departmental recruitments based on the applicable recruitment rules. Applicants are advised to read the official notification carefully before applying.

Aadhaar NPCI Mapping Online 2026 | Update Bank DBT
NPCT DBT (National Payments Corporation of India – Direct Benefit Transfer) enables the Indian government to transfer subsidies, pensions, scholarships, and other benefits directly to beneficiaries’ bank accounts. It ensures fast, secure, and transparent payments by linking beneficiaries’ Aadhaar numbers with their bank accounts. Payments can be tracked via bank statements, the DBT Bharat Portal, or the UMANG app. This system covers various schemes such as LPG subsidies, MGNREGA wages, farmer support, pensions, and student scholarships.
