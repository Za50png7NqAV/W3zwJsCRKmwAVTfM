IBPS Specialist Officer SO 15th Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the notification for the Specialist Officer (SO) CRP SPL-XV Recruitment 2025. A total of 1007 vacancies have been announced for various Scale I posts including IT Officer, Agriculture Field Officer (AFO), Rajbhasha Adhikari, Law Officer, HR/Personnel Officer, and Marketing Officer. The online application process will be open from 1st July 2025 to 21st July 2025. The Preliminary Examination is scheduled for August 2025, followed by the Main Examination in November 2025. Interviews are likely to be conducted in January/February 2026. Applications will be accepted in online mode only. For more details and to apply, visit the official website www.ibps.in.

Indian Navy B.Tech Officer Entry Scheme Jan 2026 Batch
Indian Navy has invited online applications for the 10+2 B.Tech Cadet Entry Scheme (January 2026 Batch). Eligible unmarried male and female candidates who have passed 12th with 70% marks in PCM and 50% in English can apply. Selected candidates will undergo a four-year B.Tech course at the Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala, Kerala.
