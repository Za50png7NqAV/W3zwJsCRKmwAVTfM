MP Rojgar Panjiyan Online Registration 2026
MP Rojgar Panjiyan 2026 is the official employment registration system launched by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Through this portal (mprojgar.gov.in), job seekers can register for free, get notified about government and private job opportunities, participate in job fairs, and renew their registration every 3 years. The process is fully online, and applicants need to provide basic personal information, educational documents, and Aadhaar details to complete their profile.

MPESB Candidate Profiling / Profile Panjiyan Form Online
To apply for any government recruitment under the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB), all candidates must complete the Candidate Profile Registration process. This is a one-time mandatory step where applicants register using Aadhaar eKYC, upload personal and academic details, and receive a unique Profile ID. This ID is required for all future MPESB applications. The process is simple, online, and essential for anyone seeking government jobs in Madhya Pradesh.
