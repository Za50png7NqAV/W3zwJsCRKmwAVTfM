BRLPS JEEViKA Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Bihar Rural Livelihoods Promotion Society (BRLPS), under the Rural Development Department of the Government of Bihar, has released the official notification for JEEViKA Recruitment 2025. A total of 2747 vacancies have been announced across various posts including Block Project Manager, Livelihood Specialist, Area Coordinator, Accountant, Office Assistant, Community Coordinator, and Block IT Executive. The required educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from Intermediate (for female Community Coordinators) to Graduation, Post-Graduation, Diploma, or B.Tech. The minimum age for applicants is 18 years, while the upper age limit goes up to 42 years depending on the category. The selection process involves a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and a typing test for relevant positions. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website https://brlps.in from 30th July to 18th August 2025. This is a great opportunity to contribute to Bihar’s largest rural development mission and support the empowerment of rural communities.

MPESB MP Police Constable Admit Card 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPESB) has announced the MP Police Constable Recruitment 2025 for a total of 7500 Constable (General Duty – SAF & DEF) posts. The online application process will start from 15/09/2025 and remain open till 29/09/2025, while the correction window will be available up to 04/10/2025. The written examination is scheduled to begin from 30/10/2025. Candidates applying for this recruitment must have passed 10th/12th (for UR/SC/OBC/EWS) or at least 8th standard (for ST candidates) from a recognized board. The minimum age limit is 18 years and the maximum is 33 years for general category male candidates, with age relaxations applicable for reserved categories and women as per rules. The application fee is ₹500 for UR, ₹250 for SC/ST/OBC/EWS (MP domicile), while departmental candidates need to pay ₹200 (UR) or ₹100 (reserved categories) along with applicable portal charges. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for candidates seeking a stable government job in the Madhya Pradesh Police Department.

IOB Specialist Officers Admit Card 2025
Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) has released a notification for the recruitment of 127 Specialist Officers in MMG Scale II & III across various departments, including IT, Risk, Information Security, Treasury, Corporate Credit, Software Engineering, and Data Science. Eligible candidates can apply online from 12.09.2025 to 03.10.2025 at www.iob.in. Age, educational qualifications, and post-qualification experience will be considered as on 01.09.2025. MPPSC Food Safety Officer Exam Date 2025
Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of Food Safety Officer (FSO) with a total of 67 vacancies. The application process will be conducted online, and the job location is Madhya Pradesh. Candidates from all over India are eligible to apply, but those from other states will be treated as General (UR) category and will not receive any reservation or fee relaxation. Applicants must hold a Bachelor's, Master's, or Ph.D. degree in Food Technology, Agriculture, Biotechnology, Chemistry, Veterinary Science, Medicine, or a related field, or possess an equivalent qualification recognized by the Central Government. The age limit is 21 to 40 years, with age relaxation applicable as per rules for reserved categories. For complete details and to apply, visit the official website mppsc.mp.gov.in.
