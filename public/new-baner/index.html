Indian Army SSC Tech 67th Women Vacancy 2026
The Indian Army has announced SSC (Technical) 67 Women Recruitment 2026 for female engineering candidates. A total of 29 vacancies are available in Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics & Communication and Mechanical engineering streams. Eligible unmarried women can apply online between 06 January 2026 and 04 February 2026 at joinindianarmy.nic.in. The selection process includes shortlisting, SSB interview, medical examination and preparation of merit list. Selected candidates will undergo 49 weeks of training and will be paid a stipend of ₹56,100 per month during training.

Indian Army SSC Technical 67th Men Vacancy 2026
Indian Army has announced SSC (Tech) 67 Men Recruitment 2026 for 350 engineering graduate vacancies. Eligible unmarried male candidates aged 20–27 years can apply online between 07/01/2026 and 05/02/2026. Selection includes shortlisting, SSB interview, medical test and merit list. Selected candidates will receive training at OTA Gaya and be appointed as Lieutenant.

IAF Agniveervayu Intake 01/2027 Batch Online Form
The Indian Air Force has invited online applications for Agniveervayu Intake 01/2027 under the Agnipath Scheme from unmarried male and female candidates. The recruitment is for a four-year service period and includes an online written examination, physical fitness test, adaptability tests, and medical examination. Selected candidates will receive a fixed monthly package, training, insurance cover, and a Seva Nidhi package on completion of service. The application process is completely online and eligible candidates must apply within the prescribed dates.
