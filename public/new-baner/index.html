BOB Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Bank of Baroda has released the official notification for 2700 Apprentice posts under the Apprentices Act, 1961. The online application process started on 11 November 2025 and will continue till 01 December 2025. Candidates having a Graduation Degree in any discipline can apply through the official website bankofbaroda.in or via the BFSI SSC portal. Selection will be done through an Online Exam, Document Verification, and Local Language Test. Selected candidates will receive a monthly stipend of ₹15,000 during the 1-year apprenticeship training.

ECGC Probationary Officer Vacancy 2025
ECGC Limited, a Government of India enterprise, has released the official notification for the recruitment of 30 Probationary Officers (PO) for FY 2025–26, including 28 Generalist and 2 Specialist (Rajbhasha/Hindi) posts. The online application process will be open from 11 November to 2 December 2025, and the online examination is scheduled for 11 January 2026. Candidates must be graduates (for Generalist) or postgraduates in Hindi/English (for Specialist). The salary package is highly attractive, with a CTC of around ₹20 lakh per annum. Selection will be through online exam and interview, and shortlisted candidates may be posted anywhere in India under ECGC Ltd.
