HDFC Scholarship 2025-26
HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECSS Scholarship 2025-26 is a financial assistance programme aimed at helping meritorious students from economically weaker sections continue their education without interruption. The scholarship is open for school students (Class 1 to 12), as well as those pursuing diploma, ITI, polytechnic, undergraduate, and postgraduate courses. Under this initiative, eligible students facing financial challenges, family crises, or risk of dropout can receive support of up to INR 75,000. The programme ensures that bright and deserving students get equal opportunities to pursue their academic goals and build a successful future.

MPPSC District Public Health Nurse Officer Vacancy 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released Advertisement No. 05/2025 dated 04 September 2025 for the recruitment of District Public Health Nurse Officer under the Public Health and Medical Education Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh. A total of 12 vacancies have been announced for this post with a pay scale of ₹15600–39100 + Grade Pay ₹5400. Candidates must have M.Sc. Nursing / B.Sc. Nursing / Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing from a recognized institute, valid registration with the MP Nurses Registration Council, and at least 10 years of relevant experience. The age limit is 21 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The online application process will be open from 25/09/2025 to 24/10/2025, with a correction window from 01/10/2025 to 26/10/2025 and hardcopy submission till 31/10/2025. The application fee is ₹250 for MP reserved categories, ₹500 for UR/other states, and ₹50 per correction. Interested candidates can apply through mponline.gov.in or mppsc.mp.gov.in

RRB Paramedical Various Posts Vacancy 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.
