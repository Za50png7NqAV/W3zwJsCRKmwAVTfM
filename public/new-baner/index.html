UPPSC GIC Lecturer Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has announced the UPPSC GIC Lecturer Recruitment 2025 for a total of 1,518 vacancies in various Government Inter Colleges across the state. The vacancies include 777 posts in the Male Branch, 694 posts in the Female Branch, 45 posts in Sparsh Government Inter College for Special Education, and 2 posts in the Uttar Pradesh Jail Training School (Teaching Staff). Candidates must possess a Post Graduate degree in the relevant subject along with a B.Ed. from an NCTE-recognized institution, with additional qualifications required for special and jail training school posts. The online application process will run from 12 August 2025 to 12 September 2025, and selection will be conducted through a Preliminary Examination, Main Written Examination, and Document Verification.

UP Police Sub Inspector SI Vacancy 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has announced the UP Police Sub Inspector (SI) Recruitment 2025. The online application process will be open from 12 August 2025 to 11 September 2025, and fee adjustments for submitted payments can be made until 13 September 2025. The application fee is ₹500/- for General, EWS, and OBC candidates, while SC and ST candidates will have to pay ₹400/-. The recruitment is for posts in Uttar Pradesh, and applications must be submitted online.
