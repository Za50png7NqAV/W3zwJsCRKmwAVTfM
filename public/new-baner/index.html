RRB Exam Calendar 2026
The official RRB Exam Calendar 2026 has been issued by the Railway Recruitment Board to outline the annual recruitment planning for upcoming railway vacancies. The calendar covers key posts including Assistant Loco Pilot, Technicians, NTPC Graduate and Undergraduate posts, Junior Engineer, Paramedical staff, Ministerial and Isolated categories, Section Controller, and Level-1 Group D. It explains the quarterly schedule for vacancy assessment, OIRMS processing, indent submission, and draft CEN preparation. Candidates should note that this calendar indicates the planning cycle only, while detailed notifications and exam dates will be announced separately through individual CEN notifications.

PNB Local Bank Officer LBO Vacancy 2025 - Exam Date
Punjab National Bank (PNB) has released the official notification for LBO Recruitment 2025, offering 750 Local Bank Officer (LBO) vacancies across India. Eligible graduates with at least one year of banking experience can apply online from 03/11/2025 to 23/11/2025 through the official website pnbindia.in. Bank of India Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Bank of India has announced the Apprentice Recruitment 2025–26 for 400 training seats across multiple states in India. Graduate candidates fulfilling the age and educational eligibility criteria can apply online from 25 December 2025 to 10 January 2026. The selection process includes an online objective examination and a qualifying local language test. The apprenticeship tenure is one year, and selected candidates will receive a stipend of ₹13,000 per month. This apprenticeship offers valuable banking experience but does not lead to permanent employment.

SSC CPO SI 2025 - Answer Key Out
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for CPO SI Recruitment 2025, offering 3073 vacancies for Sub-Inspector posts in Delhi Police and CAPFs. Eligible candidates with a bachelor’s degree can apply online from 26 September to 16 October 2025. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (Paper-I & II), Physical Tests (PST/PET), Medical Examination, and Document Verification. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for graduates to join Delhi Police and CAPFs with a pay scale of Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400).
