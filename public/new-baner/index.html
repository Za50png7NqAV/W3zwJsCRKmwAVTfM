ONGC Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) has released the official notification for Apprentice Recruitment 2025 under the Apprentices Act, 1961. A total of 2743 vacancies have been announced across India in various sectors such as Northern, Western, Eastern, Southern, Mumbai, and Central.Eligible candidates with ITI, Diploma, or Graduate qualifications can apply for trades like Fitter, Electrician, Computer Operator, Lab Chemist, Mechanical Executive, Accounts Executive, and others. The online application process began on 16/10/2025 and will continue till 06/11/2025 through the official ONGC Apprentice portal – ongcapprentices.ongc.co.in. Selection will be based on merit of qualifying exam marks, and no examination fee is required. Selected apprentices will receive a monthly stipend up to ₹12,300 during the 12-month training period.

MPPGCL Assistant & Junior Engineer Vacancy 2025
Madhya Pradesh Power Generating Company Limited (MPPGCL) has released the Phase-II Recruitment 2025-26 notification for 131 posts of Assistant Engineer, Junior Engineer, Chemist, and Office Assistant. Eligible candidates can apply online from 17 October 2025 to 15 November 2025 through the MPOnline portal at https://chayan.mponline.gov.in. Selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (CBT) followed by document verification. Candidates must hold the prescribed qualifications (BE/B.Tech/Diploma/M.Sc/12th + Computer Diploma) as per post. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for technical and administrative aspirants in the MP Power sector under a government enterprise.

IBPS SO 15th Pre Result 2025 Out
The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the notification for the Specialist Officer (SO) CRP SPL-XV Recruitment 2025.
