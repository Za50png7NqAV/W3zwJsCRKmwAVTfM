RRC WCR Apprentice Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), West Central Railway has announced 2,865 Apprentice vacancies under Notification No. 01/2025. The online application process will run from 30th August to 29th September 2025. Candidates must have passed 10th Class with at least 50% marks along with an ITI certificate (NCVT/SCVT) in the relevant trade. The age limit is 15 to 24 years with relaxation as per rules. Selection will be purely based on merit (10th + ITI marks). Application fee is ₹141 for UR/OBC/EWS and ₹41 for SC/ST/PwBD/Women. Interested candidates can apply online through the official website wcr.indianrailways.gov.in.

BPSC Polytechnic Department Head Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has announced 218 vacancies for the post of Department Head in Government and Women Polytechnic Colleges. Eligible candidates with the required academic qualifications and teaching/industry experience can apply online from 02 September 2025 to 30 September 2025. The minimum age is 33 years, there is no upper age limit, and the retirement age is 65 years.

BPSC Sports Officer Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has announced 33 vacancies for the posts of District Sports Officer, Assistant Director (Sports/Youth), and Lecturer (SH & Colleges) under the Bihar Sports Service Cadre through Advt. No. 90/2025. Candidates with a Graduate Degree and a Diploma/PG Diploma in Sports Coaching from a recognized institute are eligible to apply. The online application window will remain open from 02 September 2025 to 30 September 2025, and applicants need to pay a fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges. This is a good opportunity for candidates with sports coaching qualifications to build their career in the Bihar Sports Department.
