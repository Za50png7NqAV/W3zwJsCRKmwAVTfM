PowerGrid PGCIL Field Engineer & Supervisor Vacancy 2025
The Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (PGCIL) has invited applications for 1543 vacancies of Field Engineer and Field Supervisor under the 2025 recruitment drive. BOB Local Bank Officers LBO Admit Card 2025
Bank of Baroda (BOB) has announced 2500 vacancies for the post of Local Bank Officer (JMG/S-I) across multiple states. Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline from a recognized university. Professional qualifications like CA, Engineering, or Medical are also accepted. Applicants must have at least 1 year of experience as an officer in a Scheduled Commercial Bank or Regional Rural Bank listed under the Second Schedule of the RBI. Experience in NBFCs, cooperative banks, and fintechs will not be considered. The recruitment is open state-wise with preference for candidates familiar with the local language.

Bihar BPSC AEDO Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the official notification for 935 Assistant Education Development Officer (AEDO) vacancies under the Education Department. The online application process will begin on 27 August 2025 and continue till 26 September 2025. Candidates with a graduate degree from a recognized university are eligible to apply. The salary will be as per Level-5 pay scale (₹29,200 + allowances). Applicants must pay an examination fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges through online mode. Interested candidates should visit the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in to apply online and check detailed guidelines.

Oil India Limited Vacancy 2025
Oil India Limited, a Maharatna PSU, has released its Recruitment Notification 2025 for a total of 102 vacancies in Grade A, B, and C posts. The vacancies include positions such as Superintending Engineer, Senior Officers in various disciplines (Chemical, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, IT, Petroleum, HR, Legal, Finance, Public Affairs, Geology, Geophysics, Electronics & Communication, Fire & Safety, HSE, Security, Company Secretary), along with Confidential Secretary and Hindi Officer. Both male and female candidates are eligible to apply. The application process is completely online, starting from 26 August 2025 and closing on 26 September 2025. Candidates will be selected through Computer Based Test (CBT), Personal Interview, and Skill/Proficiency Test as per the post. The recruitment offers attractive pay scales ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹2,20,000 plus allowances, making it a great opportunity for engineering and non-engineering graduates to build a career with Oil India Limited.
