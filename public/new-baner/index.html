MP RTE Admission 2026-27
RTE Admission 2026-27 Madhya Pradesh is conducted under the 25% reservation quota in private schools as per the Right to Education Act 2009. Eligible children from Nursery, KG-1 and Class 1 can apply through the official online portal. The admission process includes online application, document verification, lottery selection and final reporting at the allotted school. Applicants must fulfill age criteria, category eligibility (SC/ST/OBC/BPL/Divyang/HIV/COVID orphan/Weaker Section) and residence requirements of Madhya Pradesh.
