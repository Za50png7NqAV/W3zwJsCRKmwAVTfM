KVS NVS Teaching & Non-Teaching Vacancy 2025 - Exam City
KVS NVS Recruitment 2025 offers more than 14967 Teaching and Non-Teaching vacancies under Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. Eligible candidates can apply online from 14/11/2025 to 04/12/2025 for posts such as PRT, TGT, PGT, Principal, Clerk, Stenographer, Librarian and others. SSC GD Constable Vacancy 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

UP Police SI Confidential, ASI Clerk and Accounts Vacancy 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has issued the official notification for Police Sub Inspector (Confidential) and Police Assistant Sub Inspector (Clerk and Accounts) posts for the year 2025. Released under Advertisement No. PRPB-B (Cl.)/2025, the recruitment includes a total of 537 vacancies. Online applications will be accepted from 20/12/2025 to 19/01/2026.
