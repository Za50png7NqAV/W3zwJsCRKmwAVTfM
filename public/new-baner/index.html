BPSC Bihar Assistant Engineer 2025 Admit Card
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the Admit Card for the Assistant Engineer (AE) Recruitment 2025 under Advertisement Nos. 29/2025, 30/2025, and 31/2025. The exam is scheduled to be held from 17th to 19th July 2025, and candidates can download their admit cards from 14th July 2025 by visiting the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in. The admit card is a mandatory document to appear in the exam, and it contains key details like exam date, time, venue, and instructions.

SIDBI Assistant Manager Grade A & B Vacancy 2025
Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Assistant Manager Grade A (General) and Manager Grade B (General, Legal, IT) posts, with a total of 76 vacancies. BHEL Artisan Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) has announced 515 vacancies for Artisan Grade IV posts under various trades such as Fitter, Welder, Turner, Machinist, Electrician, Electronics Mechanic, and Foundryman for the year 2025. These vacancies are spread across multiple BHEL units like BAP, HPVP, HEEP, TP, and others. Candidates must have passed Class 10th along with ITI (NTC) and NAC in the respective trade. A minimum of 60% marks is required for General and OBC candidates, and 55% for SC/ST in both qualifications. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for skilled ITI holders to join one of India's leading public sector enterprises.
