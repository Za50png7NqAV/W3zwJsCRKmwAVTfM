UPSSSC Excise Constable Vacancy 2026
UPSSSC has released the Excise Constable Recruitment 2026 notification for a total of 722 posts. Candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) and appeared in PET 2025 are eligible to apply. The application process is online, starting from 04/06/2026 and ending on 24/06/2026. The selection will be based on PET score shortlisting, followed by a written examination, physical test, and document verification. The age limit is 18 to 40 years, with relaxation as per government rules.

RRB NTPC Graduate Level Vacancy 2026 - Answer Key
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced the RRB NTPC Graduate Level Recruitment 2025 (CEN 06/2025) with around 5800 vacancies under the graduate category. Eligible candidates with a Bachelor’s degree can apply online for posts such as Station Master, Goods Guard, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Accounts Assistant, Commercial Apprentice, and Traffic Assistant. The online application window will remain open from 21 October 2025 to 20 November 2025. The selection process includes CBT-1, CBT-2, Computer Based Aptitude Test (for Station Master & Traffic Assistant), Typing Skill Test (for Clerk/Typist posts), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The salary ranges from ₹25,500 to ₹35,400 (Level-4 to Level-6 as per 7th CPC) along with allowances. Candidates must check the official notification for detailed eligibility, post-wise vacancies, exam pattern, and other important updates.

UPSSSC Teacher Cadre (JTC) Vacancy 2026
UPSSSC Assistant Teacher Recruitment 2026: Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the notification for Assistant Teacher (Special Education) recruitment 2026. A total of 58 posts are available for various special schools including hearing impaired, visual impairment, mental disability and deaf blind schools. Candidates who have 12th pass with Special Education Diploma, RCI registration and UPSSSC PET 2025 score card are eligible to apply. Interested candidates can submit the online application form from 06/04/2026 to 27/04/2026 through the official website. Applicants are advised to read the official notification carefully before applying.
