Delhi Police Constable Admit Card Out 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the Delhi Police Constable (Executive) Recruitment 2025 notification for 7565 vacancies. Both male and female candidates can apply online from 22 September to 21 October 2025. The recruitment process includes a Computer-Based Examination, followed by Physical Endurance & Measurement Tests (PE&MT). Eligible candidates must have passed 10+2, and male applicants must hold a valid LMV driving license. The pay scale is Level-3 (₹21,700 – ₹69,100). Reserved categories and women candidates are exempted from the application fee.

BSSC Inter Level Vacancy 2025
Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the notification for the Inter Level Combined Competitive Exam 2025 with 23,175 vacancies across various departments. Eligible candidates can register online from 15th October 2025 to 25th November 2025, while the final submission deadline is 27th November 2025. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with computer knowledge and typing skills. This is one of the biggest recruitment drives in Bihar offering opportunities for thousands of aspirants.
