RRB Paramedical Staff 2025 - Revised Exam Date Releasede
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.

CTET February 2026 Registration - Re Exam Date
The CTET February 2026 notification has been released, and the online application process is open from 27/11/2025 to 18/12/2025. Candidates can apply for Paper I (Class 1–5) and Paper II (Class 6–8) based on NCTE eligibility norms. The exam will be conducted on 08 February 2026 in offline OMR mode across multiple exam centers. The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC (single paper) and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The CTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, and all important details regarding syllabus, exam pattern, eligibility, and fee structure are provided in the official notification.

RRB Section Controller Vacancy 2025 - Answer Key
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced 368 vacancies for Section Controller posts through CEN 04/2025. The online application process will be open from 15 September to 14 October 2025. Candidates with a graduation degree and age between 20 to 33 years can apply. Selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test, skill test (if required), document verification, and medical examination. The post offers a Level 6 pay scale with attractive allowances.

Railway RRB Technician Exam City 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the RRB Technician Recruitment 2025 notification for Technician Grade-1 (Signal) and Technician Grade-3 (Open Line) posts.
