RRB Paramedical Staff 2025 - Exam City
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.

EMRS Teaching & Non-Teaching Tier II Exam City 2025
The National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) has announced the EMRS Recruitment 2025 (ESSE-2025) notification for a total of 7267 vacancies across teaching and non-teaching posts. The vacancies include Principal, PGT, TGT, Female Staff Nurse, Hostel Warden, Accountant, Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Lab Attendant. Candidates can apply online until 23rd October 2025, with application fees ranging from ₹500 to ₹2500 depending on the post and category. The eligibility criteria vary by position, requiring qualifications from 10th/12th to Graduate, Postgraduate, B.Ed., CTET, or professional degrees. The age limit is set between 30 to 50 years, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories. This recruitment provides a major opportunity for aspirants seeking teaching and non-teaching jobs in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) across India.

IAF Agniveervayu Intake 01/2027 Batch Online Form - Re-Open
The Indian Air Force has invited online applications for Agniveervayu Intake 01/2027 under the Agnipath Scheme from unmarried male and female candidates. The recruitment is for a four-year service period and includes an online written examination, physical fitness test, adaptability tests, and medical examination. Selected candidates will receive a fixed monthly package, training, insurance cover, and a Seva Nidhi package on completion of service. The application process is completely online and eligible candidates must apply within the prescribed dates.
