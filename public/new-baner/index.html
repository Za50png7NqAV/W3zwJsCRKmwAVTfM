BOB Local Bank Officers LBO Exam Date 2025
Bank of Baroda (BOB) has announced 2500 vacancies for the post of Local Bank Officer (JMG/S-I) across multiple states. Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline from a recognized university. Professional qualifications like CA, Engineering, or Medical are also accepted.
