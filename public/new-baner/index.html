RPSC Statistical Officer Vacancy 2025
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the official notification for Statistical Officer Recruitment 2025 under the Economic & Statistics Department. Indian Army TES 55 July 2026 Batch Vacancy
The Indian Army has released the TES-55 (10+2 Technical Entry Scheme) notification for July 2026 batch. Unmarried male candidates who have passed Class 12 (PCM) with minimum 60% marks and have appeared in JEE (Main) 2025 can apply. A total of 90 vacancies are available under this entry. The online application is open from 14/10/2025 to 13/11/2025 at www.joinindianarmy.nic.in. Selected candidates will undergo 4 years of training (3 years at CTW + 1 year at IMA Dehradun) and will be commissioned as Lieutenants with a starting pay level of ₹56,100/- (Level 10). No application fee is required — candidates can apply free of cost.

Bihar BSSC Office Attendant / Attendant (Special) Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Office Attendant / Attendant (Special) posts under advertisement number 06/25. A total of 3727 vacancies have been announced across multiple departments of the Bihar government. Candidates who have passed the 10th (Matric) examination from a recognized board are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 25th August 2025 and remain open until 26th September 2025. The selection will be done through a preliminary objective-type written examination, followed by a main exam for shortlisted candidates. The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the upper age limit varies by category, with applicable relaxations. Reservation benefits will be given as per Bihar government rules, including 35% horizontal reservation for women and 4% for PwD candidates. The recruitment offers a Level-1 pay scale, and candidates are advised to read the full notification before applying online through the official BSSC website: https://bssc.bihar.gov.in.

Bihar BSSC 4th Graduate Level Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification for the 4th Graduate Level Combined Competitive Examination 2025. This recruitment aims to fill 1481 vacancies across various departments of the Bihar Government. The posts include Assistant Branch Officer, Planning Assistant, Junior Statistical Assistant, Data Entry Operator (Grade-C), Auditor in the Finance Department, and Auditor in Cooperative Societies. Candidates must be graduates, with specific subject qualifications required for certain posts. The online application process will be open from 18 August to 19 September 2025. The selection will be done through a Preliminary Exam (objective type and open book with NCERT/BSEB textbooks), followed by a Main Exam and Document Verification. The application fee is ₹540 for General/BC/EBC (male) and outside Bihar candidates, and ₹135 for SC/ST, women (Bihar residents), and PwD candidates. Interested and eligible candidates can apply through the official BSSC website at https://bssc.bihar.gov.in.
