RBI Assistant Per Admit Card 2026
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has released the RBI Assistant Recruitment 2026 notification for 650 Assistant posts. Eligible candidates with a Bachelor’s degree (minimum 50% marks, pass class for SC/ST/PwBD) and knowledge of word processing can apply online from 16/02/2026 to 08/03/2026. RPSC Sub Inspector & Subedar Admit Card 2026
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the official notification for Rajasthan Police SI and Platoon Commander Recruitment 2025-26 under advertisement number 05/Exam/SI-PC/RPSC/EP-I/2025-26. A total of 1015 vacancies have been announced for various posts including Sub Inspector (AP, IB, Sahariya, Scheduled Area) and Platoon Commander (RAC).
