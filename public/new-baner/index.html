Yantra India Limited Apprentice Vacancy 2026
Yantra India Limited (YIL) has released the Apprentice Recruitment Notification 2026 for 3979 Trade Apprentice posts, including 2843 Ex-ITI and 1136 Non-ITI vacancies.
