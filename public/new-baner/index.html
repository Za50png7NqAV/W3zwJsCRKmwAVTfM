NTA AISSEE 2026 Registration Form
The All India Sainik School Entrance Examination (AISSEE) 2026 is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to Class 6 and Class 9 in Sainik Schools across India for the academic year 2026-27. The online application process will be open from 10 October 2025 to 30 October 2025. Candidates must carefully check the eligibility criteria, age limit, application fee, and exam pattern before applying. The entrance exam will be conducted in January 2026 in offline (OMR-based) mode. The application fee is ₹850 for General/OBC/Defence candidates and ₹700 for SC/ST candidates. Eligible students must fulfill nationality, age, and educational qualification requirements. For detailed information, official notifications, and the online application link, visit the NTA website at https://exams.nta.ac.in/sainik-school-society/

LIC AE, AAO Generalist Pre Result 2025
The Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) has announced the LIC AE & AAO Recruitment 2025 for a total of 841 vacancies. The recruitment includes posts for Assistant Engineer (Civil/Electrical), Assistant Administrative Officer (AAO Specialist – CA, CS, Actuarial, Legal, Insurance), and AAO Generalist. The online application process is open from 16/08/2025 to 08/09/2025. The Preliminary Exam will be held on 03/10/2025 followed by the Main Exam on 08/11/2025, while the Interview schedule will be announced later. Candidates with a Bachelor’s Degree in the relevant discipline (Engineering, Law, Finance, Actuarial Science, Company Secretary, Insurance, or any stream for Generalist) are eligible to apply. The application fee is ₹700 + GST + charges for General/OBC/EWS candidates and ₹85 + GST + charges for SC/ST/PwBD candidates. The selection process will be conducted in three stages: Prelims, Mains, and Interview.
