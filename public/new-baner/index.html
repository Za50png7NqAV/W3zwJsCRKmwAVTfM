BPSC Lower Division Clerk LDC Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of Lower Division Clerk (LDC) posts with a total of 26 vacancies. Candidates who have passed Intermediate (10+2) in any stream from a recognized board and possess computer operation and typing skills are eligible to apply. This recruitment is primarily for domicile candidates of Bihar, who will receive reservation benefits as per their category. Candidates from other states can also apply but will be considered under the General (Unreserved) category only, without any reservation or age relaxation. The application process is completely online. Out of the total posts, 13 are for General, 3 for EWS, 4 for SC, 1 for ST, 2 for EBC, 2 for BC, and 1 for BC Female.
