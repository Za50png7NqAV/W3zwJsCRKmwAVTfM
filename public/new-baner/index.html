Rajasthan Administrative Services apply online for 2026
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the official notification for Rajasthan State and Subordinate Services Combined Competitive Examination 2026. A total of 607 vacancies have been announced for various State Service and Subordinate Service posts including RAS, RPS, Rajasthan Accounts Service, Tehsildar Service, Assistant Jailor, CDPO and other departments. SSC GD Constable Admit Card 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

RBI Assistant Mains Admit Card 2026
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has released the RBI Assistant Recruitment 2026 notification for 650 Assistant posts. Eligible candidates with a Bachelor’s degree (minimum 50% marks, pass class for SC/ST/PwBD) and knowledge of word processing can apply online from 16/02/2026 to 08/03/2026. The selection process includes a Preliminary Exam, Main Exam and Language Proficiency Test (LPT). The Preliminary Exam is scheduled for 11/04/2026 and the Main Exam for 07/06/2026. The starting basic pay is ₹29,000 with initial gross monthly salary approximately ₹58,514 along with allowances as per RBI rules.
