MPESB Exam Calendar 2026
MPESB Exam Calendar 2026 outlines the tentative schedule of recruitment, entrance and eligibility examinations to be conducted by the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board. As per the calendar, various exams including Group-1, Group-2, Group-3, Group-4, Police Constable, Staff Nurse, ITI Training Officer, Nursing, Agriculture and Teacher Eligibility Tests are scheduled between February 2026 and October 2026. Candidates preparing for MP government jobs and entrance examinations can use this calendar to plan their preparation in advance, while final dates will be confirmed through official notifications issued by MPESB.

MPESB Group 1 Sub Group 2 Vacancy 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has announced the Group 1 Subgroup 2 Combined Recruitment Test 2025 for 474 vacancies in different government departments. Applicants must possess a postgraduate qualification in the relevant discipline as specified in the departmental recruitment rules. The online application window will be active from 24 December 2025 to 07 January 2026 on esb.mp.gov.in. Selection of candidates will be based on a written examination and subsequent stages as per the official notification.
