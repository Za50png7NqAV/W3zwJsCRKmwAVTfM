HPSC AE, Municipal Engineer & Sub Divisional Engineer Vacancy 2025
Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has announced 153 Civil Engineer vacancies in various departments including Public Works (B&R), Municipal Corporation, and Panchayati Raj. The posts include Assistant Engineer, Municipal Engineer, and Sub Divisional Engineer for candidates holding a B.E./B.Tech in Civil Engineering (AICTE approved). The online application process will start from 12 August 2025 and will remain open until 1 September 2025, 5:00 PM. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official HPSC website.

HPSC Assistant District Attorney ADA Vacancy 2025
HPSC has announced 255 Assistant District Attorney (ADA) vacancies under Advt. No. 18/2025. The online application process will run from 13 August to 2 September 2025 through the official website hpsc.gov.in. Candidates must hold an LLB degree and meet the age criteria of 21–42 years. The selection process includes a screening test and a subject knowledge test. The post carries a pay scale of FPL-9 (₹53,100–₹1,67,800).

Rajasthan Sub Inspector Vacancy 2025 Apply Online
Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the official notification for Rajasthan Police SI and Platoon Commander Recruitment 2025-26 under advertisement number 05/Exam/SI-PC/RPSC/EP-I/2025-26. A total of 1015 vacancies have been announced for various posts including Sub Inspector (AP, IB, Sahariya, Scheduled Area) and Platoon Commander (RAC). Candidates holding a graduate degree and having knowledge of Hindi in Devanagari script and Rajasthani culture are eligible to apply. The online application process will begin on 10 August 2025 and the last date to apply is 08 September 2025. Applicants from all states of India can apply for general posts, while certain reserved posts are limited to Rajasthan domicile candidates only.

IOB Indian Overseas Bank Apprentice Vacancy 2025
Indian Overseas Bank has announced the Apprentice Recruitment 2025 for 750 vacancies across various states and UTs. Applications will be accepted online from 10 August to 20 August 2025 through the BFSI SSC portal, with the online exam tentatively scheduled for 24 August 2025. Graduates aged 20–28 years are eligible, with relaxations as per government norms. Selection will be based on an online test, local language proficiency, and document verification. Stipend ranges from ₹10,000 to ₹15,000 depending on posting location.
