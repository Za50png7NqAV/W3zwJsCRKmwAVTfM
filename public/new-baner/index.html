MPPSC Assistant Professor (Computer Science) Vacancy 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released the official notification for the Assistant Professor (Computer Science) Exam 2025 under the Higher Education Department. A total of 87 vacancies are available, including reserved seats for SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and PwD categories. Eligible candidates must hold a Master’s degree (M.Sc, M.Tech, M.E, or MCA) with at least 55% marks and have qualified NET/SET/SLET or possess a Ph.D. as per UGC norms. The online application process runs from 31/10/2025 to 30/11/2025, and the exam is scheduled for 04/01/2026. The selection process includes a written test followed by an interview.

MPESB MP Police Constable Vacancy 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPESB) has announced the MP Police Constable Recruitment 2025 for a total of 7500 Constable (General Duty – SAF & DEF) posts. The online application process will start from 15/09/2025 and remain open till 29/09/2025, while the correction window will be available up to 04/10/2025. The written examination is scheduled to begin from 30/10/2025. Candidates applying for this recruitment must have passed 10th/12th (for UR/SC/OBC/EWS) or at least 8th standard (for ST candidates) from a recognized board. The minimum age limit is 18 years and the maximum is 33 years for general category male candidates, with age relaxations applicable for reserved categories and women as per rules. The application fee is ₹500 for UR, ₹250 for SC/ST/OBC/EWS (MP domicile), while departmental candidates need to pay ₹200 (UR) or ₹100 (reserved categories) along with applicable portal charges. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for candidates seeking a stable government job in the Madhya Pradesh Police Department.

BSF Sports Quota Vacancy 2025
The Border Security Force (BSF) under the Ministry of Home Affairs has released the Constable (GD) Sports Quota Recruitment 2025 notification for 391 vacancies. Eligible Indian male and female sportspersons who have participated or won medals in recognized National or International competitions (2023–2025) can apply online from 16 October 2025 to 4 November 2025 at rectt.bsf.gov.in. Candidates must have passed Matriculation (10th) and meet prescribed age, physical, and medical standards. The pay scale is Level-3 (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) as per the 7th CPC with admissible allowances. Application fee is ₹159 for UR/OBC male candidates, while SC/ST and female applicants are exempted. Selection will be based on sports merit, documentation, PST, and medical examination.
