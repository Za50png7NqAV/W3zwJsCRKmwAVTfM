UP B.Ed. Admission 2026
UP B.Ed. Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) 2026 is conducted by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi for admission to the 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) course (2026–28). Candidates from General and OBC categories must have at least 50% marks in Graduation or Post-Graduation, while Engineering candidates require 55% marks. There is no minimum percentage requirement for SC/ST candidates. The selection process is based on the entrance examination followed by counselling.

IB Security Assistant/ Executive Tier-II Admit Card 2026
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has released a notification for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive posts for the year 2025. A total of 4987 vacancies are available across various states and Union Territories. Candidates must have passed 10th standard, possess knowledge of the local language/dialect, and hold a domicile certificate of the region applied for. The post offers a Level-3 pay scale (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) along with special allowances. This is a central government Group 'C' non-gazetted, non-ministerial post, offering job security and growth in the intelligence sector. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal before the last date.

CUET (UG) 2026 Online Correction Form
CUET (UG) 2026 is a national-level admission test conducted by the National Testing Agency for undergraduate admissions in Central and other participating universities for the academic session 2026–27. Candidates who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 can apply online. The exam is held in CBT mode, allows up to five subject choices, and uses a standardized marking scheme. Admissions are granted by universities based on CUET scores, merit lists, and program-wise eligibility criteria.
