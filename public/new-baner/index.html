MP RTE Admission 2026-27
RTE Admission 2026-27 Madhya Pradesh is conducted under the 25% reservation quota in private schools as per the Right to Education Act 2009. Eligible children from Nursery, KG-1 and Class 1 can apply through the official online portal. The admission process includes online application, document verification, lottery selection and final reporting at the allotted school. Applicants must fulfill age criteria, category eligibility (SC/ST/OBC/BPL/Divyang/HIV/COVID orphan/Weaker Section) and residence requirements of Madhya Pradesh.

MPESB Candidate Profiling / Profile Panjiyan Form Online
To apply for any government recruitment under the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB), all candidates must complete the Candidate Profile Registration process. This is a one-time mandatory step where applicants register using Aadhaar eKYC, upload personal and academic details, and receive a unique Profile ID. This ID is required for all future MPESB applications. The process is simple, online, and essential for anyone seeking government jobs in Madhya Pradesh.

MPESB Forest Guard, Jail Prahari & Field Guard Vacancy 2026
MP Forest & Jail Recruitment 2026 has been announced for 1679 vacancies including Forest Guard (728 posts), Field Guard (169 posts), Jail Prahari (757 posts), and Assistant Jail Superintendent (25 posts). Candidates who have passed 10+2 can apply for Forest Guard, Field Guard, and Jail Prahari posts, while a Bachelor’s Degree is required for Assistant Jail Superintendent. MP Rojgar Panjiyan Online Registration 2026
MP Rojgar Panjiyan 2026 is the official employment registration system launched by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Through this portal (mprojgar.gov.in), job seekers can register for free, get notified about government and private job opportunities, participate in job fairs, and renew their registration every 3 years. The process is fully online, and applicants need to provide basic personal information, educational documents, and Aadhaar details to complete their profile.
