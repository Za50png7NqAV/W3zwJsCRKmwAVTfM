PM Vishwakarma Yojana 2026
प्रधानमंत्री विश्वकर्मा योजना भारत सरकार द्वारा पारंपरिक कारीगरों और शिल्पकारों के लिए शुरू की गई एक महत्वाकांक्षी योजना है। इसका उद्देश्य बढ़ई, लोहार, सुनार, कुम्हार, दर्जी, नाई, धोबी और अन्य पारंपरिक व्यवसायों से जुड़े लोगों को पहचान, कौशल प्रशिक्षण, आधुनिक उपकरण, सस्ती ऋण सुविधा और विपणन सहायता प्रदान करना है। योजना के अंतर्गत लाभार्थियों को PM Vishwakarma प्रमाण पत्र एवं आईडी कार्ड, ₹15,000 तक की टूलकिट सहायता, ₹500 प्रतिदिन प्रशिक्षण स्टाइपेंड तथा 5% ब्याज दर पर ₹3 लाख तक का बिना गारंटी ऋण उपलब्ध कराया जाता है। इसके अलावा डिजिटल लेनदेन को बढ़ावा देने और उत्पादों की ब्रांडिंग एवं मार्केटिंग के लिए भी सहायता दी जाती है, जिससे कारीगरों की आय और रोजगार के अवसर बढ़ सकें।

UP Police Constable Exam City Details 2026
UP Police has released the Constable Recruitment 2025 notification for 32,679 vacancies across multiple units such as Civil Police, PAC, Special Security Force, Female Battalion, Mounted Police and Jail Warder. Applicants must have passed Intermediate (12th) from a recognized board. The prescribed age limit is 18 to 22 years for male candidates and 18 to 25 years for female candidates, with category-wise relaxation as per government norms. The recruitment process consists of a written test followed by PST, PET, document verification and medical examination. The online application window will be open from 31/12/2025 to 30/01/2026, making it one of the largest police recruitments in Uttar Pradesh.

Allahabad High Court RO, ARO & Computer Assistant Vacancy 2026
The High Court of Judicature at Allahabad has released the Allahabad High Court Recruitment 2026 Notification (Advertisement No. 02/RO-ARO & CA/2026) for 543 vacancies. DSSSB TGT, IT Assistant & JSA Vacancy 2026
Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released Advertisement No. 03/2026 for the recruitment of 1979 vacancies in various departments of the Government of NCT of Delhi. CSBC Prohibition Constable, Jail Warder Admit Card 2026
The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC), Bihar has released Advertisement No. 03/2025 for the recruitment of 4128 vacancies including Prohibition Constable, Jail Warder, and Mobile Squad Constable. Eligible candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) can apply online from 06/10/2025 to 05/11/2025 through the official website csbc.bihar.gov.in. The selection process includes a written exam, physical measurement test, and physical efficiency test (PET). Interested applicants must check the official notification for complete details on eligibility, age limit, application fees, and physical standards before applying.
