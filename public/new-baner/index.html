Bihar SHS Lab Technician Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The State Health Society, Bihar invites online applications for 1075 posts of Laboratory Technicians, including 1068 Lab Technician and 7 Senior Lab Technician (NTEP) vacancies. Candidates must have 10+2 (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with DMLT/BMLT or relevant M.Sc./B.Sc. qualifications for senior posts. The selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (CBT) followed by document verification. Application fees range from ₹125 to ₹500 depending on category. Salary offered is ₹15,000 for Lab Technician and ₹24,000 for Senior Lab Technician.

UPSC Prosecutor & Lecturer Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has announced 84 vacancies through Advertisement No. 12/2025. Bihar BPSC AEDO Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the official notification for 935 Assistant Education Development Officer (AEDO) vacancies under the Education Department. The online application process will begin on 27 August 2025 and continue till 26 September 2025. Candidates with a graduate degree from a recognized university are eligible to apply. The salary will be as per Level-5 pay scale (₹29,200 + allowances). Applicants must pay an examination fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges through online mode. Interested candidates should visit the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in to apply online and check detailed guidelines.

RRB Section Controller Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced 368 vacancies for Section Controller posts through CEN 04/2025. The online application process will be open from 15 September to 14 October 2025. Candidates with a graduation degree and age between 20 to 33 years can apply. Selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test, skill test (if required), document verification, and medical examination. The post offers a Level 6 pay scale with attractive allowances.
