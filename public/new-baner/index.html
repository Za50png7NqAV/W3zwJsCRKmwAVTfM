Reliance Foundation Scholarships 2025-26
Reliance Foundation Scholarships 2025–26 support talented Indian students pursuing Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) programs. Scholarships provide financial aid up to ₹2,00,000 for UG and ₹6,00,000 for PG students, along with mentorship, networking, workshops, internships, and volunteering opportunities. Eligible students must be first-year learners in full-time regular courses, meet academic criteria, and complete a mandatory online aptitude test. Last date to apply: 4th October 2025 via the official Reliance Foundation Scholarships portal.

NPCT DBT 2025: Benefits, Purpose, and Complete Guide
NPCT DBT (National Payments Corporation of India – Direct Benefit Transfer) enables the Indian government to transfer subsidies, pensions, scholarships, and other benefits directly to beneficiaries’ bank accounts. It ensures fast, secure, and transparent payments by linking beneficiaries’ Aadhaar numbers with their bank accounts. Payments can be tracked via bank statements, the DBT Bharat Portal, or the UMANG app. This system covers various schemes such as LPG subsidies, MGNREGA wages, farmer support, pensions, and student scholarships.

SCI Assistant Manager & Executive Vacancy 2025
The Shipping Corporation of India Ltd. (SCI) has invited applications for 75 vacancies of Assistant Manager (E2) and Executive (E0). The online application process will run from 06 September 2025 to 27 September 2025. Maximum age limit is 27 years (with relaxations as per Govt. norms). The selection process includes Online Test, Group Discussion, and Personal Interview. Application fee is ₹500 for UR/OBC-NCL/EWS and ₹100 for SC/ST/PwBD/ESM. Interested candidates can apply online through the official website shipindia.com
