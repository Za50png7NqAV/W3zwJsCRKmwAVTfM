IB Security Assistant/Executive Admit Card 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has released a notification for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive posts for the year 2025. A total of 4987 vacancies are available across various states and Union Territories. Candidates must have passed 10th standard, possess knowledge of the local language/dialect, and hold a domicile certificate of the region applied for. The post offers a Level-3 pay scale (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) along with special allowances. This is a central government Group 'C' non-gazetted, non-ministerial post, offering job security and growth in the intelligence sector. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal before the last date.

CSBC Bihar Police Recruitment 2025
The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC), Bihar has released Advertisement No. 03/2025 for the recruitment of 4128 vacancies including Prohibition Constable, Jail Warder, and Mobile Squad Constable. Eligible candidates who have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) can apply online from 06/10/2025 to 05/11/2025 through the official website csbc.bihar.gov.in. The selection process includes a written exam, physical measurement test, and physical efficiency test (PET). Interested applicants must check the official notification for complete details on eligibility, age limit, application fees, and physical standards before applying.

SSC CPO SI & CAPF Vacancy 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for CPO SI Recruitment 2025, offering 3073 vacancies for Sub-Inspector posts in Delhi Police and CAPFs. Eligible candidates with a bachelor’s degree can apply online from 26 September to 16 October 2025. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (Paper-I & II), Physical Tests (PST/PET), Medical Examination, and Document Verification. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for graduates to join Delhi Police and CAPFs with a pay scale of Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400).
