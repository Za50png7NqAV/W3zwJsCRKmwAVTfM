BHEL Artisan Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) has announced 515 vacancies for Artisan Grade IV posts under various trades such as Fitter, Welder, Turner, Machinist, Electrician, Electronics Mechanic, and Foundryman for the year 2025. These vacancies are spread across multiple BHEL units like BAP, HPVP, HEEP, TP, and others. Candidates must have passed Class 10th along with ITI (NTC) and NAC in the respective trade. A minimum of 60% marks is required for General and OBC candidates, and 55% for SC/ST in both qualifications. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for skilled ITI holders to join one of India's leading public sector enterprises.

MPESB Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025 Online Form
Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the notification for the Primary School Teacher Selection Test 2025. A total of 10,150 Varg 3 Primary Teacher posts will be recruited under the School Education Department. Eligible candidates who have qualified the MP Primary TET 2020 or 2024 can apply online. Applicants must check the detailed eligibility, age limit, and other conditions in the official notification before applying. Candidates from outside MP can also apply but will not get any domicile reservation or relaxation benefits.

Jharkhand SSC ANM Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Female Health Workers (A.N.M.) under the Regular and Backlog vacancies for the year 2025. A total of 3,181 posts are available, including 3,020 regular and 161 backlog vacancies. Only female candidates who are domicile of Jharkhand and have completed 10th (Matric) with at least 45% marks, along with 18 months A.N.M. training and registration in Jharkhand State Nursing Council, are eligible to apply. The application process will be conducted online through the official JSSC website. Interested and eligible candidates must read the official notification thoroughly and apply within the specified dates.
