RRC NWR Apprentice Vacancy 2025
North Western Railway (NWR) Jaipur has released the official notification for 2162 Act Apprentice vacancies under the Apprentices Act 1961 for the year 2025-26. The online application process will start from 03 October 2025 and will remain open till 02 November 2025 at rrcjaipur.in. Candidates must have passed 10th Class with at least 50% marks and hold an ITI certificate in the relevant trade (NCVT/SCVT) to be eligible. The age limit is set between 15 to 24 years with relaxation applicable for SC, ST, OBC, PwBD, and Ex-Servicemen as per government rules. Selection will be done on the basis of a merit list prepared from the average of 10th and ITI marks, without any written exam. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS candidates, while SC/ST, PwBD, and Female candidates are exempted from fees. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for ITI qualified youth to gain practical training and build their career with Indian Railways.

BPSC Project Manager Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of 09 Project Manager posts under the Bihar Industries Service. Eligible candidates can apply online from 10th September 2025 to 6th October 2025. Candidates must hold at least a second-class degree in Engineering, MBA, PG Diploma, Economics, CA, CMA, Pharmacy, or related disciplines. The age limit is 21 to 37 years for UR male candidates, with relaxations applicable for reserved categories as per rules. The selection process will include Preliminary Exam (Objective), Main Written Exam, and Interview. The pay scale is Level–9.

BSSC Stenographer Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has released the official notification Advt. No. 07/2025 for the recruitment of 432 Stenographer and Stenotypist Grade-III posts in various departments and district offices. The minimum qualification required is Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent along with stenography (Hindi/English), typing, and basic computer knowledge. The selected candidates will get a salary in Pay Level-4 (₹25,500 – ₹81,100/-). The age limit is 18 to 37 years for UR male, 40 years for UR female, OBC and EBC candidates, and 42 years for SC/ST candidates, with 10 years of additional relaxation for PwD candidates. The application fee is ₹100/- to be paid online. The online registration will be open from 25th September 2025 to 5th November 2025, and the last date for fee payment is 3rd November 2025. Interested candidates can apply online only through the official website bssc.bihar.gov.in

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has invited applications for Class IX Lateral Entry Admission 2026-27 against vacant seats through a selection test. FSSAI Food License Registration Online Form
The FSSAI Food License is mandatory for all food-related businesses in India to ensure food safety and compliance with the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. It is issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. There are three types of licenses – Basic, State, and Central – depending on the business size and turnover. Applicants must submit documents like identity proof, address proof, and business registration while applying online through the FSSAI portal. The license is valid for 1 to 5 years, and renewal is required before expiry. Running a food business without a license may lead to heavy penalties or legal action.
