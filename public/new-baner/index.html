SSC CPO SI 2025 - Exam Date Out
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for CPO SI Recruitment 2025, offering 3073 vacancies for Sub-Inspector posts in Delhi Police and CAPFs. Eligible candidates with a bachelor’s degree can apply online from 26 September to 16 October 2025. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (Paper-I & II), Physical Tests (PST/PET), Medical Examination, and Document Verification. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for graduates to join Delhi Police and CAPFs with a pay scale of Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400).

SSC Jr Engineer Exam Date 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for the Junior Engineer (JE) Recruitment 2025. The vacancies are available in various departments such as BRO, CPWD, CWC, MES, NTRO, and others for trades including Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical. Candidates must have a Diploma or Degree in the relevant engineering discipline. Some departments also require two years of work experience. The application process is completely online, and the selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (Paper 1), Paper 2, and Document Verification. This recruitment is open to both male and female candidates across India. Interested applicants can visit the official SSC website https://ssc.gov.in for more details and to apply online.

ECGC Probationary Officer Vacancy 2025
ECGC Limited, a Government of India enterprise, has released the official notification for the recruitment of 30 Probationary Officers (PO) for FY 2025–26, including 28 Generalist and 2 Specialist (Rajbhasha/Hindi) posts. The online application process will be open from 11 November to 2 December 2025, and the online examination is scheduled for 11 January 2026. Candidates must be graduates (for Generalist) or postgraduates in Hindi/English (for Specialist). The salary package is highly attractive, with a CTC of around ₹20 lakh per annum. Selection will be through online exam and interview, and shortlisted candidates may be posted anywhere in India under ECGC Ltd.

IAF AFCAT 01/2026 Batch Vacancy 2025
The Indian Air Force (IAF) has announced the AFCAT 01/2026 Batch Recruitment 2025 for Flying and Ground Duty (Technical & Non-Technical) branches. The online application process will begin on 10 November 2025 and continue till 9 December 2025 through the official website afcat.cdac.in. Candidates holding a graduate or engineering degree with Physics and Mathematics at 10+2 level can apply. The selection process includes an online written test, AFSB interview, and medical examination. Successful candidates will join the AFCAT 01/2026 course commencing in January 2026 as Group-A Gazetted Officers in the Indian Air Force
