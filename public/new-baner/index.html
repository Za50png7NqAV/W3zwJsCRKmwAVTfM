UPSC EPFO Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the Special Advertisement No. 52/2025 for direct recruitment to a total of 230 vacancies in the Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) under the Ministry of Labour and Employment. These include 156 posts of Enforcement Officer/Accounts Officer (EO/AO) and 74 posts of Assistant Provident Fund Commissioner (APFC). Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university. For the APFC post, qualifications such as a degree in Law, MBA, CA, or equivalent are desirable. The online application process will begin on July 29, 2025, and close on August 18, 2025. Selection will be based on a written examination, and postings will be across India. Interested candidates can apply online at https://upsconline.nic.in.

RSSB Platoon Commander Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has invited online applications for the post of Platoon Commander under the Rajasthan Home Guard Department. A total of 84 vacancies are available for this Group B, non-gazetted government job. Candidates must possess a graduation degree, along with knowledge of Hindi written in Devanagari script and familiarity with Rajasthan's culture. Only those who have appeared in the CET (Graduation Level) 2024 exam are eligible to apply. Selected candidates will be placed under a 2-year probation period with fixed monthly remuneration. The pay scale is as per Pay Matrix Level-10 (₹9300–34800 + Grade Pay ₹3600). Applications are accepted online only through the official RSSB portal.

BSF Constable Sports Quota Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Border Security Force (BSF) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Constable (General Duty) under Sports Quota 2025. A total of 241 vacancies are available for meritorious male and female sportspersons who have participated at national or international levels. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matric) from a recognized board and must possess valid sports certificates. The application process is completely online and starts from 25 July 2025. Eligible candidates can apply before the last date, 20 August 2025. This is a golden opportunity for young sportspersons to serve in BSF with pride.
