Jharkhand SSC ANM Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Female Health Workers (A.N.M.) under the Regular and Backlog vacancies for the year 2025. A total of 3,181 posts are available, including 3,020 regular and 161 backlog vacancies. Only female candidates who are domicile of Jharkhand and have completed 10th (Matric) with at least 45% marks, along with 18 months A.N.M. training and registration in Jharkhand State Nursing Council, are eligible to apply. The application process will be conducted online through the official JSSC website. Interested and eligible candidates must read the official notification thoroughly and apply within the specified dates.

RBI Officers Grade A & B Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has announced 28 vacancies for various Grade A and B Officer posts under the 2025 recruitment drive. Candidates applying for these positions must meet the minimum educational qualifications such as LLB, BE/B.Tech, Master’s Degree, or have military experience, along with relevant work experience where required. This is a great opportunity for eligible candidates to join the RBI as an officer and contribute to the nation’s central banking system. Interested applicants can submit their applications through the official RBI website within the given deadline.
