BOB Credit Analyst & Relationship Manager Vacancy 2025
Bank of Baroda has released the official notification BOB/HRM/REC/ADVT/2025/14 for the recruitment of 50 regular posts in the Corporate & Institutional Credit Department. The vacancies include Manager, Senior Manager, and Chief Manager positions for Credit Analyst and Relationship Manager roles. Eligible candidates having a Post Graduate degree in Finance or CA/CMA/CS/CFA with 3–8 years of experience can apply online from 10/10/2025 to 30/10/2025. The selection process may include an online test, group discussion, and interview. Applications are invited from candidates willing to serve anywhere in India.
