RRC WCR Apprentice Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), West Central Railway has announced 2,865 Apprentice vacancies under Notification No. 01/2025. The online application process will run from 30th August to 29th September 2025. Candidates must have passed 10th Class with at least 50% marks along with an ITI certificate (NCVT/SCVT) in the relevant trade. The age limit is 15 to 24 years with relaxation as per rules. Selection will be purely based on merit (10th + ITI marks). Application fee is ₹141 for UR/OBC/EWS and ₹41 for SC/ST/PwBD/Women. Interested candidates can apply online through the official website wcr.indianrailways.gov.in.

Patna High Court Stenographer Vacancy 2025 Online Form
Patna High Court has invited applications for 111 Stenographer posts through its Recruitment 2025 notification. The vacancies include 68 regular and 43 backlog posts. Candidates who have passed Intermediate (12th) with English Shorthand (80 WPM), English Typing (40 WPM) and a 6-month Computer Course are eligible to apply. The online application process will be open from 21st August 2025 to 19th September 2025 on the official website. The selection process will consist of a Written Test, Skill Test, and Interview. Selected candidates will receive a salary in Level-4 (₹25,500 – ₹81,100) with allowances.
