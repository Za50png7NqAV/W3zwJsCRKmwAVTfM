MPPGCL Vacancy 2025 Apply Online Form
Madhya Pradesh Power Generating Company Limited (MPPGCL) has released a recruitment notification for 346 vacancies across various posts including Assistant Engineer, Junior Engineer, Plant Assistant, Office Assistant, Stenographer, Fireman, Security Guard, and more. Candidates can apply online through the MPOnline portal from 23rd July 2025 to 21st August 2025. The selection process will be based on Computer Based Test (CBT), followed by Physical Test (for selected posts like Security Guard and Fireman) and Document Verification. Category-wise reservation is applicable only for the domicile candidates of Madhya Pradesh. Candidates from other states will be treated under the Unreserved (UR) category.

BPSC ATP Assistant Town Planner Vacancy 2025
The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has announced 35 vacancies for Assistant Town Planner (ATP) under Advertisement No. 89/2025 in the Urban Development & Housing Department. The online application process will be open from 28th August 2025 to 22nd September 2025 on the official BPSC website. Candidates with a Post Graduate degree in Town Planning, Regional/Urban Planning, City Planning, Country/Transport Planning, Housing, or Environmental Planning are eligible to apply. The selection will be based on a written examination and interview, and appointed candidates will receive a salary under Pay Level 09 (₹53,100 – ₹1,67,800) along with allowances.
