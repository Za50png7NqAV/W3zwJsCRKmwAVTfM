UPPSC Computer Assistant Admit Card 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of Computer Assistant posts in 2025. A total of 13 vacancies have been announced for eligible male and female candidates across India. To apply, candidates must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) along with either a valid “O” Level certificate from NIELIT or a Diploma in Computer Science. The minimum age required is 18 years, and selected candidates will receive an initial salary in the ₹25,500 – ₹81,100 pay scale (Level-4). The application process is online only and will be available soon on the official website uppsc.up.nic.in. Interested candidates are advised to regularly check the website for the release of the online form and notification.

Yantra India Limited Apprentice Vacancy 2026
Yantra India Limited (YIL) has released the Apprentice Recruitment Notification 2026 for 3979 Trade Apprentice posts, including 2843 Ex-ITI and 1136 Non-ITI vacancies. Eligible candidates who have passed 10th class or ITI can apply online from 01/02/2026 to 03/03/2026 through the official website. Selection will be based on merit, and selected apprentices will receive a monthly stipend of ₹8,200 (Non-ITI) and ₹9,600 (Ex-ITI). This recruitment offers a great opportunity for candidates seeking apprenticeship training in various Ordnance and Ordnance Equipment Factories across India.
