UPSC Assistant Director (Systems) Vacancy 2025 Online Form
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released Advertisement No. 10/2025 inviting online applications for the post of Assistant Director (Systems) in the Directorate of Income Tax (Systems), Department of Revenue, Ministry of Finance. A total of 45 vacancies are available under various categories including UR, EWS, OBC, SC, ST, and PwBD. The position is classified as Group ‘A’ Gazetted, Non-Ministerial and offers a Level-10 pay scale under the 7th CPC. Candidates with qualifications such as BE/B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, or equivalent in Computer Science/Applications along with relevant experience in electronic data processing are eligible to apply. The role involves software development, debugging, maintenance, and supervision of technical staff. The headquarters is in New Delhi, but the selected candidates can be posted anywhere in India. The last date to apply online is 14 August 2025, and applications must be submitted through the official website https://upsconline.gov.in/ora/.

NSP One Time Registration (OTR) 2025
NSP OTR (One Time Registration) is a new mandatory process introduced on the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) from the academic year 2025 onwards. All students must create an OTR ID using their Aadhaar-linked mobile number before applying for any scholarship. This ensures a unique and secure identity for every applicant, prevents duplication, and streamlines the scholarship process.
