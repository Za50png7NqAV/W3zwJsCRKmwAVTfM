BPSC Assistant Public Sanitation & Waste Management Officer Vacancy 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) invites online applications for 54 posts of Assistant Public Sanitation & Waste Management Officer under the Department of Urban Development & Housing, Bihar. Candidates from all states of India, both male and female, are eligible. Required qualifications include a Bachelor’s degree in Chemistry/Environmental Science, B.E./B.Tech in Chemical/Civil/Environmental Science/Public Health Engineering/Biotechnology, or a degree in Planning/Architecture. Age limit (as of 01.08.2025) is 21–37 years for unreserved males, 21–40 years for unreserved females/OBC/EBC, and 21–42 years for SC/ST. Application fee is ₹100 (plus ₹200 biometric fee if Aadhaar is not provided). Apply online between 05.09.2025 and 30.09.2025 at www.bpsc.bihar.gov.in

Reliance Foundation Scholarships 2025-26
Reliance Foundation Scholarships 2025–26 support talented Indian students pursuing Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) programs. Scholarships provide financial aid up to ₹2,00,000 for UG and ₹6,00,000 for PG students, along with mentorship, networking, workshops, internships, and volunteering opportunities. Eligible students must be first-year learners in full-time regular courses, meet academic criteria, and complete a mandatory online aptitude test. Last date to apply: 4th October 2025 via the official Reliance Foundation Scholarships portal.
