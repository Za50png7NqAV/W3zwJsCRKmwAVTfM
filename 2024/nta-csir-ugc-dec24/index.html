    NTA - Council of Scientific & Industrial Research CSIR

    Short Details of Notification NTA CSIR NET December 2024 Exam

    Important Dates

    • Application Begin : 09/12/2024
    • Last Date for Apply Online : 02/01/2025
    • Pay Exam Fee Last Date : 03/01/2025
    • Correction Date : 04-05 January 2025
    • Exam Date : 16-28 February 2025
    • Admit Card Available : Before Exam
    • Result Declared : Notified Soon

    Application Fee

    • General : 1150/-
    • OBC / EWS: 600/-
    • ST / SC : 325/-
    • Pay the Examination Fee Through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / UPI Only

    Eligibility

    • Age Limit as on 01/02/2025
    • Maximum Age : 30 Years for JRF Only.
    • Maximum Age : No Limit for Lectureship (LS) / Assistant Professor.

    Subject Details

    • Chemical Science
    • Earth, Atmospheric, Ocean and Planetary Science
    • Life Science
    • Mathematical Science
    • Physical Science

    Education-Wise Details

    Education Category Minimum Marks Required Available Subjects
    M.Sc or Equivalent Degree (General/OBC) 55%
    • M.Sc / Equivalent Degree with 55% Marks for General / OBC Candidates.
    • For SC / ST and PH Candidates : 50% Marks.
    • Integrated Course and B.E/ B.Tech / B.Pharma and MBBS Candidates Are Also Eligible for CSIR NET 2024.
    M.Sc or Equivalent Degree (SC/ST/PH) 50%
    B.E/B.Tech/B.Pharma/MBBS (Integrated Course) Eligible (Same percentage criteria as above based on category)
