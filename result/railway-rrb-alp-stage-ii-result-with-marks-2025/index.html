Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)

Assistant Loco Pilot ALP

Ad
Advertisement

📝 Short Info

Exam Name : RRB Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) CEN 01/2024 Stage II
Admit Card Release Date : 30 April 2025
CBT-II Result Declared Date : 01 July 2025
Exam Mode : Computer Based Test (CBT)
Exam Centers : Across India – Major RRB Zones
👨‍💻 How to do:
  • Visit the official RRB website: Candidates from any state can apply through their regional RRB portal (e.g., rrbcdg.gov.in) or the central DigiAlm portal: rrb.digialm.com

    • Click on the Result Link:
    Find and click on the link titled RRB ALP Stage II Result 2025 or “CEN 01/2024 CBT-2 Scorecard.

    Login to Your Account:
    Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth (DOB) as used during the application, then click the Login button.

    Check Your Scorecard:
    Once logged in, your CBT-2 result and section-wise marks will be displayed on the screen.

    Download the PDF:
    Click the Download or Print button to save your scorecard as a PDF for future use.

    Print a Hard Copy:
    Take a printout of the scorecard for CBAT (Aptitude Test) and document verification purposes.

    Check Cutoff & Shortlist Info:
    Also check the official notification or PDF result list to see the cutoff marks and whether you are shortlisted for CBAT.

🙋‍♂️Faqs❓
Q. When was the RRB ALP Stage 2 Result 2025 released??

👉 The result was declared on July 1, 2025.

Q. How can I check or download my result??

👉 Visit rrbcdg.gov.in or rrb.digialm.com, log in using your Registration Number and Date of Birth, and download your scorecard.

Q. What details are mentioned in the scorecard??

👉 Your scorecard will include: Section-wise marks, Total marks, Qualifying status, Cutoff information (if applicable)

Q. Is the result released for all RRB zones??

👉 Yes, the Stage II results are released simultaneously for all participating RRB zones.

Q. When will the CBAT (Aptitude Test) be conducted??

👉 The CBAT is scheduled for July 15, 2025, in two shifts.

Q. Is there a PDF of shortlisted candidates available??

👉 Yes, a PDF containing the roll numbers of shortlisted candidates for CBAT is available on the official website.

Q. What should I do if I can’t log in on DigiAlm portal??

👉 Double-check your registration number and DOB (in DD-MM-YYYY format). Try again during non-peak hours if the site is slow.

Q. My roll number is not in the PDF. What does that mean??

👉 If your roll number is not in the PDF, it means you have not qualified for the next stage (CBAT).

Q. What documents are required for CBAT??

👉 You must carry: CBT-2 scorecard (printed), A-1 Vision Certificate, Valid Photo ID, Admit Card

Q. Can I appear for CBAT if I failed Stage II??

👉 No, only candidates who have qualified Stage II are eligible to appear for CBAT.
